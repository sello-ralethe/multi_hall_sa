Title: Register farm feeds and pet food

Language: en
Date scraped: 2025-03-05 18:26:04

==================================================

To manufacture, import, or sell farm feed or pet food, you must be registered in terms of the Fertilizers, Farm Feeds, Agricultural Remedies and Stock Remedies Act, 1947 (Act 36 of 1947).

Farm feed and pet food are substances obtained by crushing or gritting condimental food, vitamins, or minerals. They are used for feeding domestic animals or livestock.

Before submitting your application, you must send a sample of the farm feed or pet food to any accredited laboratory. The product must be analysed according to the specified requirements.

Please note: Product registrations are valid for three years. You must renew the registration after three years.

It takes up to four months
