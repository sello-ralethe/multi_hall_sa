Title: Ukuguqulwa kwe-close corporation ibe yinkampani
Source URL: https://www.gov.za/zu/services/services-organisations/change-registration/ukuguqulwa-kwe-close-corporation-ibe-yinkampani
Language: zu
Date scraped: 2025-03-05 17:12:15

==================================================

Uma i-close corporation(i-CC) yakho isikhulile nawe usufisa ukuncintisana nezinkampani ezinkulu emakethe efanayo, ungayiguqula ibe yinkampani kwiKhomishana yokuBhalisa iziNkampani neMpahla yokuZakhela (i-CIPC). Ukuguqula i-CC yakho ibe yinkampani kuzokunika ithuba lokukwazi ukumema ezinye izingxenye ukuze zitshale izimali  njengabanikazimasheya ebhizinisini lakho.

Wonke amalungu e-CC kufanele asayinde incwadi yokuguqulwa kwe-CC ibe yinkampani bese beba  ngabanikazimasheya enkampanini. Uma ufuna ukuguqula igama le-CC, kufanele ulibekise igama.

Thola ulwazi  oluthe xaxa mayelana nokuguqulwa kwe-CC ibe yinkampanikwabe-CIPCnoma uthintane nabo kule nombolo:  086 100 2472.
