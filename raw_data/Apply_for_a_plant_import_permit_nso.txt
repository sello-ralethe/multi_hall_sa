Title: Fotosanithari sa go iša ntle dimela le ditšweletšwa tša dimela
Source URL: https://www.gov.za/nso/services/export-permits-import/phytosanitary-permit
Language: nso
Date scraped: 2025-03-05 17:29:38

==================================================

Go iša ntle dimela le ditšweletšwa tša dimela o swanetše go dira kgopelo ya tumelelo ya fotosanithari yeo e ntšhwago ke Mokgatlo wa Setšhaba wa Tšhireletšo ya Dimela yeo e išago ntle.O swanetše go nyakišiša gore maemo a go iša ntle fotosanithari  a naga yeo e išago ntle, yeo e tlogo dira kgopelo ya ditšweletšwa tša dimela tša gago. Ge diphahlo di sepelelana le dinyakwa tša go iša ntle, Mokgatlo wa Setšhaba wa Tšhireletšo ya Dimela o tla ntšha setifikeiti sa fotosanithari.Ditšweletšwa tša semela di swanetšwe go išwa ntle le setifikeiti sa mathomo sa fotosanithari.

It might take about 48 hours to process your application provided there are no additional activities.

Ditefišo tša 2025
