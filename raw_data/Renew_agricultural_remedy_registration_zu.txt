Title: Imvuselelo noma sokukhansela ubhaliso yemithi yezolimo

Language: zu
Date scraped: 2025-03-05 18:27:11

==================================================

Kufanele ubhalisele ukuvuselela noma ukucisha ikhambi lezolimo njalo emva kweminyaka emithathu. Isicelo sakho kufanele sihanjiswe ku Mbhalisi womthetho wamashumi amathathu nesithupha kunyaka ka 1947 kusikhungo sezolimo ngaphambi kukamhla wamashumi amathathu ku Mashi wonyaka wokugcina isicelo sakho esiphela ngawo.

Uma ikhambi lezolimo ofuna ukulivuselela selubhaliswe omunye umuntu, kufanele uthole imvume kulowomuntu ngaphambi kokuba uvuselele.

Faka ikhambi esigujini esihlanzekile bese uyasivala. Awuvumelekeile ukusebenzisa isigumbu kabili uma usukhiphe ikhambi. Uma kuyikhambi eliwuketshezi, qiniseka ukuthi isigubhu asichithi ikhambi uma usithulula. Isigubhu kufanele sihambisane nomthetho wezinga lase Ningizimu Afrika elibizwa nge 10229.

Ihhovisi likambhalisi likhipha izincwadi zokuvuselela kanye namafomu okubhalisa kubobonke ababhalisele kungagapheli iminyaka emithathu.

Isicelo siphumelela uma kutholakala ngokugcwele ukubhalisela ukuvuselela kwefomu  A elikathathu, Ifomu B kanye nefomu C elikabili kanye nenani lokubhalisa ikhambi ngalinye.

Imali ekhokhwayo yowezi-2024/25

Ihhovisi lika Mbhalisi likhipha izincwadi zokuvuselela nokubhalisa ukuba nguMnini kungakapheli iminyaka emithathu.
