About a licence to operate an unreserved postal service

Private individuals or businesses may apply for a licence to operate an unreserved postal or courier service.

Only theSouth African Post Officemay:

Registration is valid for a period of three year from the date of issue of a registration certificate.

What you should do

How long does it take

The licence may be ready after 30 days.

How much does it cost

Tarrifs 2020/21
