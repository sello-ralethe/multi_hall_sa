Title: Kopo ya ngodiso ya mobu bakeng sa ho boloka nare
Source URL: https://www.gov.za/st/services/agricultural-business-management/kopo-ya-ngodiso-ya-mobu-bakeng-sa-ho-boloka-nare
Language: st
Date scraped: 2025-03-05 18:27:53

==================================================

Ebang o lakatsa ho rua nare, o tlameha ho qala ka ho ngodisa naha le Lefapha la Temothuo, meru le Ditlhapi (DAFF).

O tlameha ho qala ka ho fumana tumello ho tswa ho balaodi ba amehang ba tlhokomelo ya tlhaho porovenseng.

Nako ya ho dumellwa ha kopo ke Balaodi ba Diphoofolo ba Porovenseng e fapana ho tloha porovenseng e nngwe ho ya ho e nngwe.

Hang ha kopo e se e amohetswe Bolaoding ba Bophelo ba Diphoofolo, tshebetso ya boingodiso e ka nka matsatsi a ka bang a leshome a mosebetsi.

Ditjeho tsa tshebeletso di tla fapana, ho itshetlehile ka porovense e amehang.
