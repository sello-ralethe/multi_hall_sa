Uhlelo lokuthuthukisa ezamasiko lutholwa amabhizinisi asemthethweni abhekele amabanga aphansi kuya kwaphezulu kwezamasiko. Okwasolwandle , okwasemadanyiniÂ  kanye nemikhiqizo engagcinwa yezinhlanzi. Isizo lwemali lunikezwa abafake izicelo ezintsha, ezokuthuthukisa noma ukunweba umsebenzi.

Ithimba lokuthuthukisa ezamasiko esemthethweni e Ningizimu Afrika. Lokhu kuhlanganisa kuhlanganisa izilwane ezihlala emanzini, nokukhiqizwa kanye nokunakekela kwezinhlobonhlobo zezinhlanzi. Usizo lunikezelwa kwizicelo eziphumelele zokwenzancono nokuthuthukisa lomsebenzi.

Loluhlelo lunikeza ngemali ekhokhwayo engafika kuzigidi ezingamashumi amane okungaba imishini, isakhiwo noma ukuthuthukiswa kwebhilidi, kanye nemisebenzi yokuthuthukisa ebhalwe kumthetho wesishiyagalombili wohlelolokuthuthukisa ezamasiko.
