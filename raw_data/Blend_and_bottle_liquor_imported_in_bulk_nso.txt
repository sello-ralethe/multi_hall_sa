Title: Kgopelo ya go tswaka bjala bjoo bo rekilwego ka ntle
Source URL: https://www.gov.za/nso/services/liquor/kgopelo-ya-go-tswaka-bjala-bjoo-bo-rekilwego-ka-ntle
Language: nso
Date scraped: 2025-03-05 18:05:42

==================================================

Go tswaka bjala bjo o bo rekilego nageng ya ka ntle ka bontši ka maikemišetšo a go bo rekiša o swanetše go hwetša tumelelo go tšwa go Kgoro ya Temo, Kagodikgwa, Boreahlapi (DAFF). Bjala bja go rekwa nageng ya ka ntle o ka bo tswaka le setšweletšwa sa go tšwa ka mo Afrika Borwa, meetse goba le setšweletšwa se sengwe sa go tšwa moše wa mawatle.

Ga go na nako ye itšeng ye e beetšwego tirelo ye.

Ditefišo tša 2024/25

Application for blending of liquor imported in bulk
