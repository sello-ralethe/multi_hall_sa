Title: Kgopela CCMA go dira dikwanelo tša melawana ya kgwabo nakong ya go ngala mošomo goba go notlelwa ntle
Source URL: https://www.gov.za/nso/services/services-organisations/labour/trade-unions/kgopela-ccma-go-dira-dikwanelo-t%C5%A1a-melawana-ya
Language: nso
Date scraped: 2025-03-05 18:29:34

==================================================

Mokgatlo wa bašomi wo o ingwadišitšego o ka dumelela maloko a wona le bathekgi go gwaba ka maikemišetšo a go dira ditšhupetšo tša khutšo.

kgwabo e ka swarelwa mo gongwe le mo gongwe mo batho ba kgonago go fihla gona. E ka swarelwa ka ntle ga meago goba ka gare ka tumelelo ya mongmošomo.

Ge eba go na le go se kwane mabapi le melawana ya kgwabo, mokgatlo wa bašomi wo o ingwadišitšego goba mongmošomo a ka kgopela Khomišene ya Kagišo, Poelano le Bonamodi (CCMA) go dira dikwanelo gare ga maphakga a mabedi a.

Ge go se na kwanelo ye e fihleletšwego, CCMA e swanetše go hloma melawana ya kgwabo, e šeditše kudu mabaka a itšego a lefelo la mošomo goba mafelo a mangwe go gwaba le melawana e mengwe ya go sepelelana le maitshwaro a mabotse.

Hwetša dintlha ka botlalo mabapi lego hwetša kwano ka ga melawana ya kgwabogoba ikgokaganye leCCMA.
