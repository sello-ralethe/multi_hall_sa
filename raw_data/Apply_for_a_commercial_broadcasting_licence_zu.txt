Title: Faka isicelo selayisensi yosizo lokusakaza kokuthengisa

Date scraped: 2025-03-05 17:40:43

==================================================

Ilayisensi yosizo lokusakaza kokuthengisa inikezwa inhlangano ehlinzeka ngokusakaza ngenhloso yokuthola inzuzo noma njengengxenye yebhizinisi elenza inzuzo.Inhlangano Yezokusakaza YaseNingizimu Afrika (i-SABC)yiyo kuphela enelayisensi yokusakazela umphakathi eNingizimu Afrika.

Noma ngubani ohlose ukuhlinzeka ngosizo lokusakaza kokuthengisa kufanele abe nelayisensi yokusakaza kokuthengisa.

Ifomu lokufaka isicelo kanye nendawo ephakanyisiwe kuyingxenye yesimemo esithile sokufaka isicelo esishicilelwe KuSomqulu Kahulumeni.

Ukuthola ilayisensi kungathatha izinyanga eziyisithupha kuya kweziyishumi nambili.

Tariffs 2023/24

Ifomu lokufaka isicelo lizoba yingxenye yamashedula azoshicilelwa kuSomqulu Kahulumeni .
