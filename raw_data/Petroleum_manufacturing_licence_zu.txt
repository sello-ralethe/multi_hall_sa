Ilayisense yokukhiqiza uphalafini ikuvumela ukuthiÂ  ukhiqize imikhiqizo kaphalafini uyidayise ezitolo ezidayisa ngesamba nabathengisi abanamalayisense, ngaphandle kokukhiphela izimpahla ngaphandle kwalelizwe.

Uyacelwa ukuba uqaphele: uma ukwazi ukwenza imikhiqizo kaphalafini ngaphandle kwelayisense.

Uyothathwa njengonelayisense, kodwa kumele ufake isicelo selayisense yokukhiqiza esikhathini esiyizinyanga eziyisi-6 kusukela esikhathini sokuqala ukusebenza koMthetho, uma kungukuthi wawusebenza njengomkhiqizi ngaphambi kokuqala ukusebenza koMthetho, ziyi-17 Mashi 2006.

Isikhathi esinqunyiwe

Isinqumo kumele sithathwe ezinsukwini ezingama-90 kusukela osukwini lapho uMnyango wamukele khona ubufakazi obukhombayo ukuthi isaziso sakho sokufaka isicelo simenyezelwe kumaphephandaba.
