Xa unokuthi ufune ukuvelisa, ungenise ngaphakathi kwelizwe okanye wakhe isithuthi eMzantsi Afrika ngenjongo yokufumana inzuzo, kufuneka kuqala ubhalise kwisebe lezothutho lephondo.

Ukuba nje isebe lifumane isicelo sakho, liza kuthumela umhloli ukufumanisa ukuba uyahlangabezana na nemimiselo efanelekileyo. Ishishini lakho ngokunjalo liza kujongana noqinisekisoyiNkonzo yamaPolisa yoMzantsi Afrika (i-SAPS).

Ubhaliso luxhomekeke kulwamkelo nguMEC ofanelekileyo kwaye isatifiketi sobhaliso sikhutshwa ngenxa yoloo lwamkelo..

Qhagamshelana nesebe lezothutho lephondo ngendleko.
