==================================================

Ukuba ufuna ukungenisa impahla elawulwayo okanye esele isetyenzisiwe kunye neza kuba yeyomntu wesibini eMzantsi Afrika, kufuneka ube nemvume ephuma kwiKomishoni yoLawulo lwezoRhwebo lwaMazwe ngaMazwe (ITAC).

Uludwe lwempahla ephantsi kwemiqathango yolawulo luyafumaneka kulo mnathazwewww.itac.org.za.

Ukuba unikezele ngeenkcukacha zakho, olu ludwe lungaposelwa, luthunyelwe ngefeksi okanye nge-imeyili kuwe.

Kungathatha ukuya kwiintsuku ezintathu ukugqibezela ukuchophela nokuphumeza isicelo sakho.

iITAC ayijiki imali yokuhlawulela iinkonzo kodwa uzakuhlawula yempahla abaqokeleli be rhafu (SARS). Irhafu yempahla ibalwa ngomyinge wentengo yempahla leyo (ichaziwe kuluhlu lomthetho werhafu yemveliso yokuthengiswa ). Malunga nolwazi malunga nerhafu yempahla yabaqokeleli be rhafu, vndwendwela iwebhu sayithi yabaqokeleli berhafu iSARS.
