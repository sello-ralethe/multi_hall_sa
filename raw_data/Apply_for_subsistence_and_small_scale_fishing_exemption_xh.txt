Title: Isicelo somnyinyiva wokuxolelwa malunga nokulobela ukuphila nokulobela  amashishinana  amancinane
Source URL: https://www.gov.za/xh/services/services-organisations/permits-licences-and-rights/fishing-permits/isicelo-somnyinyiva
Language: xh
Date scraped: 2025-03-05 17:56:09

==================================================

Kufanele ufake isicelo semvume yokulobela ukuziphilisa ukuba uhlala ngase lunxwemeni kwaye ufuna ukusebenzisa izilwanyana nezityalo eziphilayo zasolwandle.

Kufanele ufake isicelo kwiikomiti zasekuhlaleni ezilawula ngokubambisana.

Ukuze ufumane imvume, kufanele wanelise yonke imiqathango.

Qaphela: Akukho semthethweni ukulobela ukuziphilisa nokuzilobela ngaphandle kwephepha-mvume. Okwangoku abalobi abaziphilisa ngokuloba balawulwa ngukuloba ngezimvume kude kugqitywe umthetho olawula abalobi bamashishini amancinci.

Imvume yokuloba ivuselelwa ngonyaka. Ngaphambi kokuba kukhutshwe iimvume, abasebenzi besebe bachaza imiqathango nemilinganiselo yengxowa.

Ukungahambisani nale miqathango kungakhokelela ekubeni kuthathwe amanyathelo omthetho aquka ukunqunyanyiswa, ukosulwa okanye ukurhoxiswa kwemvume.

Ukugqitywa kwesicelo kungathabatha inyanga okanye ngaphezulu kuxhomekeka kumthamo wezicelo. Amagosa eSebe azakubiza intlanganiso yoluntu ukukhupha ezi zimvume kubo bonke abalobi ababhalisiweyo.

Lenkonzo isimahla kude kugqitywe ngomthetho ozakulawula abalobi bamashishini amancinci.

Akukho Maxwebhu agcwaliswayo.
