Title: Lenaneo le Hlokolotsi la Disebediswa tsa Motheo
Source URL: https://www.gov.za/st/services/services-organisations/business-incentives/lenaneo-le-hlokolotsi-la-disebediswa-tsa-motheo
Language: st
Date scraped: 2025-03-05 17:22:02

==================================================

CIP ke kerante ya karolelano ya ditjeo bakeng sa diporojeke tse etseditsweng ho ntlafatsa disebediswa tsa motheo tse hlokolotsi ka hare ho naha ya Aforika Borwa.

Kerante e akaretsa ditjeo tse dumelletsweng tsa tlhabollo ho tloha ho bonyane ba 10% ho isa ho boholo ba 30% ho yeng ho paloyohle ya ditjeo tsa tlhabollo tse dumelletsweng tsa disebediswa tsa motheo.

Thola lesedi le fetang lena mabapi le lenaneo lena ho tswa hoLefapha la Kgwebisano le Indaseteri.
