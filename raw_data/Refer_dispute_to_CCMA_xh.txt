Title: Ukuthumela impikiswano kwi-CCMA ukuze kwenziwe uxolelwaniso (conciliation) okanye uxolelwaniso-nomiselo sigqibo

Language: xh
Date scraped: 2025-03-05 18:28:25

==================================================

Ukuba ungumgeshwa kwaye unempikiswano nomqashi wakho, umqashi wakho unempikiswano nawe, ngomba ofana nalo:

unga cela ikomishoni yothethwano, ukubonisana nokuthatha izigqibo (CCMA) ukuba ingenelele kuthethwano okanye ukuthatha isigqibo kwimpikiswano. Imanyano yabasebenzi okanye umbutho wabaqashi ungayithumela nawo. Awudingi mvume yomnye umntu xa ufuna ukusa isikhalazo sakho kuCCMA.

Ukuphikisana ngokulandelayo akunakwazi ukusiwa kuCCMA:

Fumana ngokupheleleyo ngokuthumela uxambuliswano ku CCMAokanye qhagamshela i-CCMA.
