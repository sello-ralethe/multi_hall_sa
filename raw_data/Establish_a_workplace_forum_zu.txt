Title: Faka isicelo sokusungula isithangami sasemsebenzini
Source URL: https://www.gov.za/zu/services/trade-unions/isicelo-sokusungula-iforamu-yasemsebenzini
Language: zu
Date scraped: 2025-03-05 18:29:07

==================================================

Isithangami sasemsebenzini ngamakomidi abasebenzi akhethwe ngabasebenzi endaweni yokusebenza. Lamakomidi ahlangana nabaqashi njalo ngezikhathi ezithile axoxe nabo ngezindaba eziphathelene nomsebenzi.

Lezi zithangami azithathi indawo yezingxoxo eziba khona phakathi komqashi nabasebenzi ezimayelana nezimo zokusebenza, kodwa zidingida izindaba ezifaneleka ukuxazululwa ngokuxoxisana. Lezi zihlanganisa izindaba ezingathinti amaholo, njengokuhlelwa kabusha komkhiqizo nokwethulwa kwezobuchwepheshe ezintsha.

Izithangami zasemsebenzini zisungulelwa:

Thola ulwazi oluthe xaxa mayelananokusungula isithangami sasemsebenzininoma thintana ne-CCMA.
