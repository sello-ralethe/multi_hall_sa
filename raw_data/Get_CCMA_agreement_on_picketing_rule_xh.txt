Title: Ukucela i-CCMA ifumane isivumelwano malunga nemigaqo yokupiketha
Source URL: https://www.gov.za/xh/services/services-organisations/labour/trade-unions/ukucela-i-ccma-ifumane-isivumelwano-malunga
Language: xh
Date scraped: 2025-03-05 18:29:24

==================================================

Imanyano yabasebenzi ebhalisiweyo ingavumela uqhankqalazo loxolo lwamalungu ayo nabalandeli.

Uqhankqalazo lungabanjwa naphi na apho amalungu oluntu angafika.

Ukuba kukhona ukuphikisana ngemithetho yoqhankqalazo, imanyano yabasebenzi ebhalisiweyo okanye umqashi banga cela ikomishoni yothethwano, ukubonisana nokuthatha izigqibo (CCMA) ukuba yenze isivumelwano kumaqela aphikisanayo.

ukuba asikho isivumelwano ekufikelelwa kuso, iCCMA kufanele imisele imithetho yokuqhankalaza, ithatha ingqalelo imeko zalendawo youkusebenza okanye indawo zokuqhankqalaza nayo yonke imek oyentlonipho.

Fumana ngokupheleleyo malunganokwenza isivumelano semithetho yokuqhankqalazaokanye qhagamshela i- CCMA.
