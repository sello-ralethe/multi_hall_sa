==================================================

Icebo lokunceda amacandelo akhetiweyo (SSAS) yimali ebuyiswayo nengumlinganiselo ka 80ku20 wokuhlulelana ngendleko ukuncedisa ngezimali kwikhansile ezithengisela kwamanye amazwe, ezibambiseneyo, kunye nemibutho yabavelisi bempahla.

Isikimu sienkqubo ezinye ezincinane ezibizwa nge imali engakhethe kunye nemali yokuncedisa amaqumrhu akhulayo okuthengisa ngaphandle kweli (PFEE).

Fumana ngokubanzi ngale nkqubo kwisebelezorwebo nophuhliso (iDTI).
