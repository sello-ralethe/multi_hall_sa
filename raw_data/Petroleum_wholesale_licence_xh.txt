Title: Ukufaka isicelo sephepha-mvume lokuthengisa iimveliso ze-oli okanye ipetroleum ngesixa
Source URL: https://www.gov.za/xh/services/petroleum/ukufaka-isicelo-selayisensi-yewulusela
Language: xh
Date scraped: 2025-03-05 18:18:02

==================================================

Ukuba ufuna ukuthengisa iimveliso zepetroleum kubathengi abaza kuyisebenzisa ngokwabo, kufuneka ufake isicelo sephepha-mvume lokuthengisa ngesixa kwiSebe lezaMandla.

Eli phepha-mvume likunika imvume yokuthenga iimveliso zepetroleum ngesixa (iilitha ezili-1 500, ngexesha) kumvelisi onephepha-mvume, uze uzithengisele abathengi bona abaza kuzisebenzisa.

Iphepha-mvume lisemthethweni ukuba nje ishishini lakho lisaqhuba.

Ufanele ukuba uyifumane impendulo zingaphelanga iintsuku ezingama-90 ukusuka ngosuku iSebe elibufumene ngalo ubungqina bokupapashwa kwesaziso sokufaka isicelo.
