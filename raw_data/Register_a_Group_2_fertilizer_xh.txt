Ukwenza okanye ukuthengisa isichumiso esikwiqela le-2, kufuneka ubhalise neGosa loMthetho wama-36 we-1947. Isichumiso seqela le-2 siqulathe ngaphantsi kwe-100kg yenitrogen (N), phosphorus (P) okanye ipotassium (K) njengezona zithako ziphambili. Ezi zichumiso zisekwaziwa njengomgquba.Kufuneka ube ngummi waseMzantsi Afrika okanye umntu womthetho one-ofisi  eMzantsi Afrika ukuze ebe selungelweni.

Ukuba isichumiso ofuna ukusibhalisa sesibhaliswe ngomnye umntu, kufuneka ufumane imvume ebhaliweyo evela kulowo ubhalise isichumiso phambi kokuba usibhalise.

Kufuneka uthumele isichumiso kwindlu yophando lwenzululwazi esemthethweni phambi kokufaka isicelo. Indlu yophando lwenzululwazi iya kuhlalutya kwaye ivavanye isichumiso ize ikunike isiqinisekiso. Kwisicelo sakho esiya kwiGosa loBhaliso, kufuneka uqhoboshele isiqinisekiso sohlalutyo.

IGosa loBhaliso aliyi kusamkela isicelo sakho ngaphandle kwesiqinisekiso sohlalutyo esivela kwindlu yophando lwenzulwazi esemthethweni.

Qaphale: Kufuneka uhlaziye ubhaliso lwakho rhoqo emva kweminyaka emithathu phambi komhla wama-31 kweyoMnga.
