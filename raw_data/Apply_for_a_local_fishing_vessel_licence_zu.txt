Ukuze usebenzise umkhumbi wokudoba e Ningizimu Afrika, Umkhumbi kufanele ubhaliswe nefulege likahulumeni wase Ningizimu Afrika.Â  Kufanele ubhalisele isitifiketi sokuphepha kwababhekele ezokuphepha emanzini e Ningizimu Afrika (Samsa) kanye negunya lombkhumbi wokudoba Emnyangweni Wezolimo, Amahlathi kanye Nabadobi Ekapa.

Ukuze uphumelele, kufanele ukuthi:

Umkhumbi wokudoba wakuleli onezimfanelo, amathulizi, ukudla, isikebhe kanye nophethilomu. It may also include anyassisting one or more vessels at sea in performance of any activity relating to fishing.

Kwenziwa lokhu okulandelayo, kodwa akungcini nje la kuphela:

Akukho emthethweni ukusebenzisa umkhumbi ongabhalisiwe. Uma ungenawo amalungelo okudoba, kufanele ubenesivumelwano nonelungelo lokudoba ukuze usebenzise umkhumbi wakho.

Umkhumbi obhaliselwe ukwenza imali akuvumelekile ukuba usetshenziswe uma umuntu ezidobela nje ngaphandle kwemvumo yokwenza imali.

Imikhumbi ebhaliswe ngenhloso yokwenza imali ixhumene namalungelo athile okudoba futhi ingasetshenziselwa lokho kuphela.

Isikhathi esibekelwe umkhumbi wokudoba sixhumene nesikhathi esibekwe esitifiketini sakwa Samsa..

Isicelo singathatha izinsuku zokusebenza ezintathu noma ngaphezulu, kusukela ngosuku kuqinisekiswe ukuthi kukhokhiwe, kuya nangokuthi ufake isicelo sakho ngokuyikho.
