Title: Isicelo sobhaliso yomanyolo e-Group 2

==================================================

Ukwenza noma ukudayisa umanyolo isigaba sesibili, Kufanele ubhalise kumbhalisi we Act 36 ka 1947. Isigaba sesibili sikamanyolo isqukethe 100g/kg we nitrogen (N), phosphorus (P) or potassium (K).Ube ngowakuleli noma obhalise kuleli ngokomthetho Ukuze ukwazi ukuthola imvumo.

Uma umanyolo ofuna ukuwubhalisela sewubhaliswe omunye umuntu, Kufanele uthole imvumo ebhalwe phansi kumuntu obhalisele lolohlobo lukamanyolo.

Kufanele uhambise umanyolo la uzocutshungulwa khona ngaphambi kokuba uhambise isicelo sakho. Umanyolo uyobe usucwaningwa bese uthola isicifiketi socwaningo. Umbhalisi angeke asamikele isicelo sakho umangabe  singekho isicifiketi socwaningo.

Qaphela: Kufanele uvuselele imvumo yakho njalo emva kweminyaka emithathu ngaphambi komhlaka 31 December.
