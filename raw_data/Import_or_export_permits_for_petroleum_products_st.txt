Title: Kopo ya ya diphemiti tsa ho tlisa kapa ho romela dihlahiswa tsa petroliyamo
Source URL: https://www.gov.za/st/services/import/kopo-ya-ho-reka-le-ho-romela-dihlahiswa-tsa-petroliamo-tsa-ditswantle-le-ho-romela
Language: st
Date scraped: 2025-03-05 17:33:46

==================================================

Haeba o batla ho tlisa kapa ho romela dihlahiswa tsa petroliyamo kantle ho Aforika Borwa, o tlameha ho fumana tumello ho tswa Lefapheng la Dimenerale le Eneji (DME) pele ho fumana phemiti ho tswa Khomisheneng ya Tsamaiso ya Kgwebisano Matjhabeng (ITAC).

Ho tliswa le ho romela dihlahiswa tsa petroliyamo ho itshetlehile taolong ya ho tlisa/romela kantle mme kahoo khamphani kapa motho ya nang le maikemisetso a ho tlisa kapa ho romela dihlahiswa tsa petroliyamo kahare kapa kantle ho Aforika Borwa o hloka phemiti ya ho tlisa kapa ho romela kantle.

Ho ntshwa ha diphemiti tsa ho tlisa kapa ho romela petroliyamo kantle ho itshetlehile ditlhokong tse laolwang ke Lefapha la Dimenerale le Eneji (DME).

Ho amoheleha ho dumellwa, o tlameha ho iteanya le DME mabapi le tsamaiso e hlokehang.

Thepa e jwaloka oli e tala, peterole, parafini, dioli tse nolofatsang, digase tsa petroliyamo, jeli ya petroliyamo, bitumen ya petroliyamo le boka ba parafini di itshetlehile mehatong ya taolo ya ho tlisa le ho romela kantle.

Ela hloko: Diphemiti tsa ho tlisa di amoheleha dikgwedi tse 12 mme diphemiti tsa ho romela kantle dikgwedi tse tsheletseng.

Nako e behetsweng ho ntshwa ha diphemiti tsa ho tlisa ke matsatsi a mararo a mosebetsi.

Tshebeletso ke mahala.
