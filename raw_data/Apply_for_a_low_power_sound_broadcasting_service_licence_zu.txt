Title: Ilayisense yemisebenzi yokusakaza enomsindo ophansi

Language: zu
Date scraped: 2025-03-05 17:42:18

==================================================

UMthetho Wezokusakaza we-1999(uMthetho 4 we-1999), uchaza umsebenzi wokusakaza onomsindo ophansi njengomsebenzi wokusakaza onomsindo ofinyelela emphakathini othile, esikhungweni esizimele noma emphakathini uwonkana okhipha amandla angeqi kwi-watt eyodwa.

Ilayisense yemisebenzi yokusakaza enomsindo ophansi ingagunyazwa ngaphansi kwezigaba ezilandelayo:

Amalayisense emisebenzi yokusakaza enomsindo ophansi omphakathi nawokungenisa imali kumele asebenze ngokusemthethweni iminyaka emithathu, futhi ilayisense yemisebenzi yokusakaza enomsindo ophansi yemicimbi ekhethekile iyosebenza izinsuku ezingamashumi amathathu.

Izicelo ezingagcwalisiwe ngokuphelele zizobuyiselwa kumfakisicelo futhi awukho umthwalo noma ukuphazamiseka okuzoba semahlombe e-Icasa

Ukugunyazwa kwale layisense kungase kuthathe izinyanga ezimbili kuya kwezintathu.

Amanani 2020/21

Ifomu lesicelo lizoba yingxenye yezithasiselo ezizoshicilelwa kuGazethi Kahulumeni.
