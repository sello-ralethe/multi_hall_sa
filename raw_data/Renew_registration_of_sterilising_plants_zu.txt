Title: Isicelo sokubhalisela ukubulala amagciwane ezakhiweni
Source URL: https://www.gov.za/zu/services/fertilizers-farm-feeds-agricultural-remedies/isicelo-sobhaliso-lwezindawo-okwenziwa-kuzo
Language: zu
Date scraped: 2025-03-05 18:27:31

==================================================

Kufanele uvuselele isicelo sakho sokubhalisela ukubulala amagciwane esakhiweni njalo emva kweminyaka emithathu. Hambisa isicelo sakho ku-Nobhala woMthetho wama-36  we-1947  ungakashayi umhla wama-31 kuNdasa wonyaka okuphela ngaso isikhathi sesicelo sakho.

Kumele uxhumane nomnyango wezolimo kwisifundazwe sakho ukuze uthole udokotela kahulumeni wezilwane endaweni yangakini ukuze ahlole ukubulawa kwamagciwane esakhiweni sakho. Udokotela wezilwane uzokunika isitifiketi emva kohlolo okumele usifake nefomu lesicelo. Isitifiketi kufanele siqinisekise ukuthi isakhiwo sakho:

Uma uhluleka ukuvuselela isicelo sokubulala amagciwane esakhiweni sakho ngaphambi komhla noma ngomhla wama-31 kuNdasa kunyaka isicelo sakho esiphelelwa isikhathi ngawo, uNobhala woMthetho wama-36 we-1947 uzosula ukubhaliswa kwakho kokuqala futhi isitifiketi sizophelelwa isikhathi sokusebenza.

5.    Khokha imali yokufaka isicelo ngesheke, nge-postal order noma nge-money order ku-Director-General: Department of Agriculture, Land Reform and Rural Development, i-Reference: Sterilising Plant. Uma uzihambisela isicelo sakho kanye nemali yokufaka isicelo mathupha, qiniseka ukuthi irisidi lokuthi ukhokhile uyalithola. Uma ukhokha nge-Electronic Transfer Account, khokha kule mininingwane ye-akhawunti elandelayo:
