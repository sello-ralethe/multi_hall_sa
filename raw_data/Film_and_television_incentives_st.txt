Title: Ditsiane tsa Tlhahiso ya Ditshwantsho le Thelevishine
Source URL: https://www.gov.za/st/services/services-organisations/business-incentives/ditsiane-tsa-tlhahiso-ya-ditshwantsho-le
Language: st
Date scraped: 2025-03-05 17:23:00

==================================================

Mmuso wa Aforika Borwa o fana ka pakana ya ditsiane ho hodisa indasteri ya ona ya tlhahiso ya ditshwantsho le tlhahiso tse tla tla. Ditsiane tse di kenyelletsa Tlhahiso ya Ditshwantsho tsa kantle, Tlhahiso tsa Thelevishini le ditsiane tsa tlhahiso tse tlang ho hohela bahlahisi ba ditshwantsho tsa kantle ho naha hore ba tlo hlahisetsa le ho nkela ditshwantsho tsa bona naheng ya rona ya Aforika Borwa, le ho etsa tse hlokehang ka mora ho hatiswa ha setshwantsho. Ditsiane tsa Tlhahiso ya Ditshwantsho le Thelevishini ya Aforika Borwa mmoho le ditsiane tsa tlhahiso tse kopanetsweng di ikemiseditse ho thusa bahlahisi ba lehae ba ditshwantsho tlhahisong ya ditshwantsho tsa lehae.

Thola lesedi le fetang lena mabapi le lenaneo lena ho tswa hoLefapha la Kgwebisano le Indaseteri.
