Title: Inkqubo encedisa ezokhenketho
Source URL: https://www.gov.za/xh/services/services-organisations/business-incentives/inkqubo-encedisa-ezokhenketho
Language: xh
Date scraped: 2025-03-05 17:47:09

==================================================

Inkqubo encedisa ezokhenketho imali yesibonelelo ebuyiswayo enjongo yayo ikuku ncedisa ukuphuhliswa kwamaqumrhu ezokhenketho azakuvula amathuba emisebenzi nokongeza sasaza utyalo mali kwindawo zonke.

Esi sisibonelelo sokuqala okanye ukongeza ezokhenketho ezifana ne:

Fumana ngokubanzi kwiSebe lezokhenketho.
