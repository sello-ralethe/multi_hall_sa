Title: Tlhahlobo ya dimela bakeng sa ho tsamaisetsa
Source URL: https://www.gov.za/st/services/export-permits-import/phytosanitary-permit
Language: st
Date scraped: 2025-03-05 17:29:43

==================================================

Ho tsamaisetsa dimela le dihlahiswa tsa dimela dinaheng tse ding, o tlameha ho etsa kopo ya phemiti ya tlhahlobo ya dimela e fumantshwang ke Mokgatlo wa Naha wa Tshireletso ya Dimela (NPPO) wa naha eo ho tsamaiswang ho tswa ho yona.O tlameha ho tseba dipehelo tsa tlhahlobo ya dimela bakeng sa ho tlisa thepa tsa naha eo o tsamaisetsang ho yona bakeng sa dihlahiswa tsa dimela. Ebang thepa e tsamaisana le dipehelo, NPPO e tla fana ka sethifikhate sa tlhahlobo ya dimela.Dihlahiswa tsa dimela dimela di tlameha ho tsamaiswa le sethifikhate sa tlhahlobo eseng kopi ya sona.

It might take about 48 hours to process your application provided there are no additional activities.

Ditefiso tsa 2025

Application for a permit for the importation of controlled goods
