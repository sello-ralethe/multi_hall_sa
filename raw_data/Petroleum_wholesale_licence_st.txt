Title: Laesense ya dithekiso tsa diphahlo tse kgolo
Source URL: https://www.gov.za/st/services/petroleum/laesense-ya-dithekiso-tsa-diphahlo-tse-kgolo
Language: st
Date scraped: 2025-03-05 18:18:38

==================================================

Motho ofe kapa ofe ya batlang ho rekisetsa bareki dihlahiswa tsa peteroliamo ho di sebedisetsa mabaka a bona a fapaneng, o tlamehile ho etsa kopo ya laesense Lefapheng la Diminerale le Eneji.

Laesense e o dumella ho reka le ho rekisa dihlahiswa tsa peteroliamo ka bongata (dilitara tse 1 500 kapa ho feta, ho tswa ka kgwebisano ya dihlahiswa tsa peteroliamo) ho tswa ho mohlahisi ya nang le laesense. Laesense e tla sebetsa fela nakong eo kgwebo ya hao e ntseng e phela.

Motho ya entseng kopo o tlamehile ho fumana karabo nakong ya matsatsi a 90 ho tloha letsatsing leo a neng a etsa kopo ka lona.
