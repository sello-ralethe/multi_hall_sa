==================================================

Uma ufuna ukungenisa impahla ethile okunemibandela ethile ukuyingenisa kuleli noma into esisebenzile noma ngabe intoni oyidingayo lapha eNingizimu Afrika, kufanele uthole imvume kwi-Nkomishani ephethe ezohwebo zomhlaba (ITAC).

Uhlu lwezimpahla ezilawulwa ngaphansi kohlelo lokungena kwempahla kuleli luyatholakala kuwww.itac.org.za.

Uma uletha imininingwane ongatholakala kuyo, loluhlu lungathunyelwa kuwe nge -emailed, fax noma luposelwe ekhelini lakho.

Kungathatha izinsuku ezintathu ukuphothulwa kwesicelo sakho.

ITAC ayikhokhisi mali ngokusiza kodwa kuyomele ukhokhe intela yosizo kwa SARS. Inani lentela libalwa kusukela esambeni saleyo mikhiqizo noma izimpahla ( ebekiwe ngokwezinhlelo kumakhasimende noma abathengi kanye nomthetho sivivinyo. Imininingwane ngentela ekhokhwayo vakashela I websaythi  yakwaSARS.
