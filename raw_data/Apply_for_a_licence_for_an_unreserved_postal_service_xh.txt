Title: Iinkonzo zeposi ezilawulwa ngamashishini abucala

Language: xh

==================================================

Abantu (bezimele) kunye namashishini abucala bangasifaka isicelo sokufumana ilayisenisi yokuhambisa iinkonzo zeposi ezilawulwa ngamashishini abucala.

Iinkonzozeposiezilawulwa ngamashishini abucala ziquka:

Isiqinisekiso sokubhalisa sisebenza ngokusemthethweni isithuba sonyaka omnye ukusuka kumhla wokukhutshwa kwaso.

Ilayisenisi iza kube sele ikhona emva kweentsuku ezingama-30.
