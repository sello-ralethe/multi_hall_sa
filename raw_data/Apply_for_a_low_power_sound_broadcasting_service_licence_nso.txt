Title: Laesense ya ditirelo tša kgašo mo makaleng a mannyane
Source URL: https://www.gov.za/nso/services/communication/laesense-ya-tirelo-ya-kga%C5%A1o-ya-modumo-wa-maatla-fase
Language: nso
Date scraped: 2025-03-05 17:42:24

==================================================

Molao wa Kgašo, wa 1999 (Molao wa 4 wa 1999),o hlaloša laesense ya tirelo ya kgašo mo makaleng a mannyane, bjalo ka tirelo ya kgašo ya mananeo a setšhaba, praebete goba a bohle gomme bokgakala bja kgašo ye ga bo a swanela go feta wata ye tee.

Laesense ya ditirelo tšaa kgašo mo makaleng a mannyane, e ka fiwa magorong a a latelago:

Bobedi dilaesense tšeo di gašago mananeo a kgwebo le tšeo di gašago mananeo a setšhaba di tla šoma mengwaga ye meraro, gomme laesense ya ditiragalo tša go ikgetha e tla šoma matšatši a 30.

Dikgopelo tšeo di sa felelago di tla bušetšwa go mokgopedi gomme go ka se amogelwe maikarabelo afe goba afe a tšhitišo.

Tumelelo ya laesense ye e ka tšea dikgwedi tše pedi go ya go tše tharo.

Ditefo 2020/21

Foromo ya dikgopelo e tla ba karolo ya dišetulo tšeo di tla phatlalatšwago ka Kuranteng ya Mmušo.
