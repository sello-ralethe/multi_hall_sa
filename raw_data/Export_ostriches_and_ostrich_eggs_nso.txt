Title: Kgopelo ya thomelontle ya dimpšhe le mae a dimpšhe

Language: nso
Date scraped: 2025-03-05 17:37:14

==================================================

Go iša ntle dimpšhe goba mae a dimpšhe, o swanetše go dira kgopelo ya tumelelo ya go iša ntle  go Mmušakarolo wa Kaonafatšo ya Diphoofolo matšatši a 30 pele ga go iša ka ntle.

Ka morago ga go dira kgopelo, o tla hwetša tumelelo go tšwa go Tšhempara ya Kgwebo ya Dimpšhe ya Afrika Borwa (South African Ostrich Business Chamber).

Ge go na le mpshikela wa dinonyane goba mohuta o mongwe le o mongwe wa bolwetši, dimpšhe di ka se išwe ntle go fihlela ge seka seo se fetile.

O swanetše goba motsebi wa molao, mohlala, John Peter Citizen, goba John Peter Citizen T/A Diepsloot Stud, goba Diepsloot Stud CC, goba Diepsloot Stud (Pty) Ltd, goba Diepsloot Ltd.

Dikgopelo di ka tšea matšatši a 30 go sepdišwa.

R390

Dira kgopelo ya go iša ntle dimpšhe goba mae a dimpšhe (Apply for exportation of ostriches or eggs)
