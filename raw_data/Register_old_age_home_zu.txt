Title: Bhalaisa isikhungo sokugcina abantu asebekhulile
Source URL: https://www.gov.za/zu/services/services-organisations/register-business-or-organisation/bhalaisa-isikhungo-sokugcina
Language: zu
Date scraped: 2025-03-05 17:10:02

==================================================

Uma ufuna ukubhalisa isikhungo sokugcina abantu asebekhulile uwedwa noma inhlangano, kufanele ufake isicelo sakho ehovisi eliseduze nawe lezenhlalakahle.

Kungathatha amasonto amane ukwenziwa kwesicelo sakho.

Ukufaka isicelo nje kukodwa kungamahala kodwa ke uyakhokha ukuthola ezinye izitifiketi njengaleso esikhishwa ngabezemnvelo nempilo.

Lamafomu ayatholakala ehovisilezenhlalakahle,eliseduze nawe
