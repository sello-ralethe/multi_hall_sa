Title: Bhalisa iYeza leMfuyo

Language: xh
Date scraped: 2025-03-05 18:22:23

==================================================

Ukungenisa, ukwenza, ukuvelisa okanye ukuthengisa iyeza lemfuyo eMzantsi Afrika, kufuneka ubhalise kwiGosa loBhaliso loMthetho wama-36 we-1947.

Amayeza emfuyo asetyenziswa kwizilwanyana zasekhaya, imfuyo yasefama, iinkukhu, iintlanzi okanye kwizilwanyana zasendle uku:

Inkampani yelizwe laphesheya ayikwazi ukufaka isicelo sobhaliso eMzantsi Afrika. Ungakwazi kuphela ukufaka isicelo ukuba inkampani yakho ibhaliswe eMzantsi Afrika okanye ukwiqumrhu elisemthethweni elibhaliswe eMzantsi Afrika.

Kufuneka ube nezi  mfuneko zilandelayo:

Ukuba ufuna ukubhalisa iYeza leMfuyo elikhoyo/ elibhaliswe egameni lenye inkampani okanye omnye umntu, kufuneka ube nemvume ebhaliweyo evela kumnini wobhaliso phambi kokuba ubhalise.

I-ofisi yeGosa loBhaliso ithumela rhoqo ngonyaka iincwadi zohlaziyo kunye namaxwebhu ezicelo kubo bonke abantu ababhalisileyo.
