==================================================

Icebo lokutyala imali kwiinqwelo (AIS) luncedo olenzelwe ukukhuthaza ukukhula nemveliso kwicandelo lenqwelo ngokutylala imali kwicomponents ezintsha okanye intlobo ezithatha ithuba lezinye izinto ezizakukhulisa indawo zokwenza inqwelo, zivule amathuba emisebenzi okanye ziqinise amaxabiso eenqwelo.

Fumana ngokubanzi ngale nkqubokwisebe lezorwebo nophuhliso (iDTI).
