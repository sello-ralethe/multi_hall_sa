Title: Kgopelo ya go mpshafatša tumelelo ya phoofolo go kgoboketša matšhedi
Source URL: https://www.gov.za/nso/services/services-organisations/permits-licences-and-rights/animal-improvement/kgopelo-ya-go
Language: nso
Date scraped: 2025-03-05 18:03:54

==================================================

Go šutiša phoofolo  yeo o e ngwadišitšego go kgobokanya semene  go ya senthareng ye nngwe, o swanetše go thoma ka go dira kgopelo  ya go mpshafatša  tumelelo ya phoofolo go kgobokanya semene.Ga o hloke go tšea mohlala wa madi a phoofolo yeo e amegago. Ga o hloke gape setifikeiti sa ngaka ya diphoofolo ka ntle le ge o šutiša phoofolo ka baka la bolwetši bja tlhako le molomo lefelong leo phoofolo e beilwego gona. Le ge go le bjalo o swanetše go kgokagana le Kgoro ya Temo, Dithokgwa  le Boreahlapi go ba fa mabaka a go šutiša phoofolo.

Matšatši a 30.

Ditefišo tša 2018/19
