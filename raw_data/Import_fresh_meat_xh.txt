Ukuba ufuna ukufaka kweli lomzantsi Afrika inyama (ekrwada) (kuquka inyama ezahlukileyo zangaphakathi), okanye ufuna ukuhambisa inyama elizweni lase Mzantsi Afrika, umthetho unyanzelisa ukuba ufake isicelo sephepha mvume lokuthenga phesheya okanye iphepha mvume lokuthutha kumlawuli wempilo yezilwanyana. Ufanele ukufumana le mvume phambi kokuba impahla oyithengayo ishiye ilizwe esuka kulo.

Umlawuli wempilo yezilwanyana eMzantsi Afrika uzakukhupha kuphela iphepha mvume lokuthenga phesheya inyama (ekrwada) kumazwe apho abone imeko  waza wazivuma inkonzo zonyango lwezilwanyana nesimo sempilo yezilwanyana. (fumana ulwazi kuElmarieV@daff.gov.za, Tel. 012 3197700 okanye feksela kule nombolo 012 329 6892).

Kune migaqo nkqubo ehlukileyo yemveliso  nempilo yezilwanyana kulizwe ngalinye. Kuba imibandela yempilo iyaguqu-guquka nceda uthinte abalawuli bempilo yezilwanyana (Tel: 012 319 7420 /7524 /7700, Feksi 012 329 6892) ukuze ufumane imibandela ebekiweyo yephephamvume lokuthenga izilwanyana phesheya.

Impahla engena isuka kwilizwe elingayivelisanga ayamkelwa ngesiqhelo.

luxanduva lakho njengomthengi phesheya ukuqinisekisa ukuthi uyafunda kwaye ungachasani nemiqathango yephepha mvume lonyango lwezilwanyana.

Ukuya ngomthehto wokhuseleko lwenyama wonyaka ka 2000 (Umthetho 40 wonyaka 2000) inyama yomkhenkce ingathengwa kuphela kwizilarha phesheya, amaziko okunqunqa amkelwe Ligosa eliphezulu lika zwelonke loMzantsi Afrika.

Uluhlu lwamaziko avunyiweyo linga fumaneka apha okanye tsalela umnxeba kule nombolo 012 319 7700 okanye Feksa isicelo sakho kule nombolo 012 329 6892.

Inyama esuka phesheya kwezilwandle ihamba ngolwandle kufanele izengezingxobo ezinomkhenkce kuphela. Imithwalo eyahlukanisiweyo ayizukwamkelwa.

Inyama (ekrwada) ingangena kweli kuphela xa ikumkhenkce omiselweyo kwiphepha mvume lonyango lwezilwanyana.

Xa uqgrirha wezilwanyana karhulument kwiofisi kazwelonke esamkele isicelo sokufaka impahla kweli,  kuthabatha nje intsuku ezintathu ukuya kwezintlanu ezokusebenza ukuthi isicelo siphumeleliswe nokuthi imvume ikhutshwe.
