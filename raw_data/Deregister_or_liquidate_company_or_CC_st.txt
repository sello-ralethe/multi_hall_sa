Title: Kopo ya ho hlakola ngodiso kapa ho fedisa khampani ya ba mmalwa (CC)
Source URL: https://www.gov.za/st/services/services-organisations/deregister-business/kopo-ya-ho-hlakola-ngodiso-kapa-ho-fedisa
Language: st
Date scraped: 2025-03-05 17:26:21

==================================================

Ha khamphani kapa Khoporeishine ya hao ya Tshebedisano e emisa ho sebetsa, o ka hlakola ngodiso ya yona le Khomishine ya Thepa le Boikgathatso ba Dikhamphani (CIPC). Hape e ka fediswa ka lebaka la tlhaolo.

Thola lesedi le fetang lena mabapi le tlhakolo ya ngodiso kapa nehelano ya khamphani ho tswa hoKhomishine ya Thepa le Boikgathatso ba Dikhamphani (CIPC)kapa letsetsa CIPC ho 086 100 2472.
