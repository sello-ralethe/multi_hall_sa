THRIP inkqubo yokubambisana efumana imali kwisebe LezoRhwebo noPhuhliso (iDTI) kwaye ilawulwa liqumrhu lophando likazwelonke (NRF). Ngokuhlulelana ngendleko nabavelisi bempahla, iTHRIP incedisa ncubabuchopho, ubunjineli nokubambisana ngophando lwezolwazi kujoliswe izidingo zolwazi ukukhuthaza uphuhliso noku hamba hamba kwabaphandi nabfundi kwimibutho ithatha inxaxheba.

Fumana ngokubanzi ngale nkqubokwiqumrhulophando lika zwelonke/iDTI.
