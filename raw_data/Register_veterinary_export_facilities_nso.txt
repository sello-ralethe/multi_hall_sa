Title: Ditlabelo tsa thomelontle ya diphoofolo
Source URL: https://www.gov.za/nso/services/services-organisations/export-permits/ditlabelo-tsa-thomelontle-ya-diphoofolo
Language: nso
Date scraped: 2025-03-05 17:38:41

==================================================

O ka romela ntle fela diphoofolo goba ditšweletšwa tša diphoofolo ge di etšwa mafelong ao a latelago melao ya boditšhabatšhaba goba melao ya naga yeo e rekago ntle. Mafelo ao a dumeletšwego a bohlabelo, mafelo a tswalelelo, senthara ya kemarišo ya maitirelo, senthara ya go tšea pelwana, polase, lefelo la go bea diphoofolo, feme ya go khuša dijo, lefelo la go boloka dijo.

E fapana go ya ka diprofense.

Ditshenyagelo di fapana go ya ka diprofense.

Difomo ga di hwetšagale onelaeneng, kgokagana le Ngaka ya Diphoofolo ya Mmušo ya Profense goba  le Kgoro ya Temo ya Bosetšhaba(Department of Agriculture, Forestry and Fisheries)

Provincial Veterinary Services
