Title: Ngwadiša khamphani ya go se šomele lešokotšo
Source URL: https://www.gov.za/nso/services/register-business-or-organisation/kgopelo-ya-go-ngwadi%C5%A1a-khamphani-ya-karolo-21
Language: nso
Date scraped: 2025-03-05 17:05:39

==================================================

Gore khamphani ya lena wa setšhaba e dumellwe go šoma go ya ka molao, e swanetše go ngwadišwa le Companies and Intellectual Property Commission (CIPC) bjalo ka ye e sa šomelego go dira lešokotšo (NPC).

Di-NPC ke khamphani ye e hlongwago ka maikemišetšo a go thuša batho, go šireletša tikologo goba go tšwetša pele masolo a go hola setšhaba ka kakaretšo. Di akaretša dikereke, mekgahlo ya babotlana le ya setšo. Moreromogolo wa NPC ke go hola setšhaba, e sego go dira lešokotšo.Letseno le thoto ya khamphani ya mohuta wo ga di arolelwe balaodi, maloko goba bahlankedi ba mokgahlo, ntle le ge e le gore seo se dirwa ka maikemišetšo a go ba lefa malebana le tirelo ye a e abilego khamphaning yeo. Matlotlo le letseno ka moka tša khamphani ye e sa šomelego go dira lešokotšo di swanetše go šomišwa go fihlella maikemišetšo a khamphani, bjalo ka ge a hlalošitšwe mo Memorantamong wa Khamphani (MOI).

Bonnyaneng, ke bathomi ba bararo ba ba swanetšego go tlatša le go saena MOI. Bonnyaneng, balaodi ba bararo ba swanetše go thwalwa.

Di-NPC di swanetše go dula di na le bonnyane bja molaodi o tee (ntle le ge e le gore MoI e bontšha palo ya go feta yeo ya balaodi). Go thwalwa ga balekodi ba dipuku le mongwaledi wa khampahni ga se ga kgapeletšo, ke ga boikgethelo.

O ka ngwadiša Khamphani ya go se šomele lešokotšo bjalo ka:

Leina la khamphani ya NPC le tlo felela ka “NPC”.

Hlokomela: Ge leina le le šišintšwego le ka ganetšwa, khamphani e ka no ngwadišwa gomme nomoro ya yona ya boingwadišo e tlo ba leina la khamphani yeo, go fihla nakong yeo leina la maleba le beeleditšwego,dumelletšwego.
