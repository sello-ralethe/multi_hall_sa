Should you want to manufacture, import or build motor vehicles in South Africa for profit, you must first register with the provincial department of transport.

Once the department receives your application, it will send an inspector to determine if you comply with the relevant regulations. Your business will also be subjected toSouth African Police Service (SAPS)clearance.
