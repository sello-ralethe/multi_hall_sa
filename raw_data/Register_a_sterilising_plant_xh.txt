Title: Isicelo sokuhlaziya ukubhalisa izityalo ezibulalala iintsholongwane
Source URL: https://www.gov.za/xh/services/fertilizers-farm-feeds-agricultural-remedies/isicelo-sokuhlaziya-ukubhalisa-izityalo
Language: xh
Date scraped: 2025-03-05 18:21:59

==================================================

Ukusebenzisa indawo apho kutshatyalaliswa iintsholongwane kwiimveliso zezilwanyana, kufuneka ubhalise neGosa loBhaliso loMthetho wama-36 we-1947 (Act 36 of 1947). Indawo apho kutshatyalaliswa iintsholongwane kwiimveliso zezilwanyana isetyenziselwa ukutshabalalisa iintsholongwane kwiimveliso zezilwanyana okanye kuzo naziphi na izilwanyana ezinethambo lomqolo okanye ezingenalo ezichazwe nguMphathiswa.Ukuze ube selungelweni:

Xa iGosa loBhaliso lifumene isicelo sakho kunye neengxelo zokuhlolwa, umhloli ovela kwicandelo loMthetho wama-36 we-1947 uya kuhlola ukuba indawo apho kutshatyalaliswa iintsholongwane kwiimveliso zezilwanyana izithobele na iimfuneko zalo mthetho (Act 36 of 1947).

Ukuba iGosa loBhaliso lonelisekile yingxelo yokuhlola, indawo apho kutshatyalaliswa iintsholongwane kwiimveliso zezilwanyana iya kubhaliswa kuze kukhutshwe isiqinisekiso. Isiqinisekiso sendawo apho kutshatyalaliswa iintsholongwane kwiimveliso zezilwanyana sisemthethweni iminyaka emithathu. Kufuneka ufake isicelo sokuhlaziya phambi komhla wama-31 kweyoKwindla kunyaka ubhaliso oluphelelwa ngawo.

Qaphela: IGosa loBhaliso loMthetho wama-36 we-1947 liya kucima ubhaliso ngalo naliphi na ixesha ukuba:

Kuthatha ukuya kwiinyanga ezine.

Imali ehlawulwayo yowama- 2024/25

Application for registration of a sterilising plant (Isicelo sokubhalisa indawo yokutshabalalisa iintsholongwane kwiimveliso zezilwanyana)
