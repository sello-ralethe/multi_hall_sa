==================================================

UMthetho wezoSasazo, wowe-1999 (uMthetho uNombolo 4 wowe-1999), uchaza inkonzo yosasazo lwesandi lomgama omfutshane njengenkonzo yosasazo yoluntu, yenkampani yabucala okanye karhulumente engasebenzisi mandla angaphezulu kwe-watt enye.

Ilayisenisi yosasazo lwesandi lomgama omfutshane ingakhutshwa kwezi meko zilandelayo:

Ilayisenisi yeenkonzo zosasazo lwesandi lomgama omfutshane yenkampani erhwebayo kunye neyoluntu zisebenza isithuba seminyaka emithathu, ize yona ilayisenisi yeenkonzo zosasazo lwesandi lomgama omfutshane lwemisitho ekhethekileyo isebenze iintsuku ezingama-30.

Iifomu zokufaka isicelo ezingagcwaliswanga ngokupheleleyo ziza kubuyiselwa kumfaki-sicelo kwaye i-Icasa ayizikuthatha xanduva ukuba oku kuye kwabangela inkxamleko.

Inkqubo yokukhupha le layisenisi ingathatha iinyaga ezimbini ukuya kwezintathu.

Ifomu yokufaka isicelo iza kuba yinxalenye yamaxwebhu azizihlomelo aza kupapashwa kwiGazethi kaRhulumente.
