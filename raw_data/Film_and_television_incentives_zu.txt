Title: Isikhwama esibhekele amafilimu kanye nezinhlelo zikamabonakude
Source URL: https://www.gov.za/zu/services/services-organisations/business-incentives/isikhwama-esibhekele-amafilimu-kanye-nezinhlelo
Language: zu
Date scraped: 2025-03-05 17:22:47

==================================================

Umongameli wase Ningizimu Afrika unikezela ngesikhwama sokwenzancono amafilimu nemikhiqizo kamabonakude. Lesikhwama sifaka amafilimu aphesheya kwezilwandle, nemikhiqizo kamabonakude kanye nemikhiqizo ezoheha imikhiqizo yamafilimu aphesheya ukuba ikhiqizwe kuleli kanye nemafilimu alapha Emzansi Afrika ahlose ukusiza imikhiqizo yamafilimu.

Imininingwano iyatholakala kumnyango wezohwebo.
