Title: Ngwadiša yunione ya bašomi
Source URL: https://www.gov.za/nso/services/trade-unions/mokgahlo-wa-ba%C5%A1omi-le-bengmo%C5%A1omo-mo-go-kgoro-ya-ba%C5%A1omi
Language: nso
Date scraped: 2025-03-05 18:30:24

==================================================

Go ngwadiša yunione ya bašomi, o swanetše go dira kgopelo go Morulaganyimogolo wa Dikamano tša Bašomi Kgorong ya Mešomo le Bašomi.

Yunione ya bašomi ke mokgatlo wa bašomi wo o lwelago le go šireletša ditokelo tša maloko a yona mo mererong ya go swana le meputso le maemo a mošomo, kudukudu ka go swara ditherišano le bengmešomo. A se kgapeletšo gore diyunione tša bašomi di ngwadišwe le Kgoro ya Bašomi, eupša diyunione tšeo di ngwadišitšwego le kgoro ye di nale maatla a go šišinya.

Romela kgopelo ya gago (Registration as a Trade Union, LRA 6.1) go The Registrar of Labour Relations, Department of Employment and Labour, Private Bag X117, Pretoria, 0001. Go hwetša tshedimošo ka botlalo, ikgokaganye leKgoro ya Mešomo le Bašomi.
