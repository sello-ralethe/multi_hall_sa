Title: Kgopelo ya Ngwadišo bjalo ka molaodi wa boswayi
Source URL: https://www.gov.za/nso/services/services-organisations/permits-licences-and-rights/animal-improvement/kgopelo-ya-ngwadi%C5%A1o
Language: nso
Date scraped: 2025-03-05 18:02:43

==================================================

Ge o nyaka go swaya diphoofolo gore o hwetše tšhelete, o swanetše go dira kgopelo le Kgoro ya Temo, Dithokgwa le Boreahlapi ( Mmušakarolo: Tlhaolo ya Diphoofolo) gore o ngwadišwe bjalo ka mosepediši wa go swaya.

Gore o khwalifaye go ngwadiša o swanetše go feleletša ditaelo tša thuto ye e latelago:

Bjalo ka mosepdiši wa go swaya, o tla nyaka go swara retšistara ya diphoofolo tšeo o di swailego.

O tla ngwadišwa bjalo ka mosepedišo wa go swaya mola o feditše thuto.

R 200
