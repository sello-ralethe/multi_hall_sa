Title: Ngodisa jwaloka mohiri ho Letlole la Inshorense ya ba Lahlehetsweng ke Mesebetsi
Source URL: https://www.gov.za/st/services/services-residents/world-work/uif/ngodisa-jwaloka-mohiri-ho-letlole-la-inshorense-ya-ba
Language: st
Date scraped: 2025-03-05 18:32:07

==================================================

Ho latela molao o hloka ho ngodisa le Letlole la Inshorense ya ba Lahlehetsweng ke Mesebetsi (UIF) mme o be le kabelo Letloleng haeba o hirile batho bakeng sa nako e fetang dihora tse fetang 24 ka kgwedi.

Kabelo ya kgwedi le kgwedi bakeng sa UIF ke diperesente tse pedi tsa moputso wohle wa kgwedi le kgwedi wa bahiruwa ba hao. O tlamehile ho hula phesente e le nngwe ya moputso wohle wa kgwedi le kgwedi wa mohiruwa wa hao mme wena jwaloka mohiri o lokela ho kenya phesente e le nngwe.
