Title: Isicelo sokwamukelwa ukuqoqa imbewu yesilisa yenkunzi yenkomo

Language: zu
Date scraped: 2025-03-05 17:57:08

==================================================

Ukuqoqa imvelaphi yezilwane ezilwaneni kufanele uthole imvumo ku Mbhalisi kuqala.

Kufanele uthole amasampula egazi esilwane ofuna ukusisebenzisa. Uwathumele kumcwaningi we DNA testing. Umcwaningi uyobe esekunikeza isitifiketi sohlobo DNA lwlesilwane. Lesisitifiketi sidingwa ngu breeder’s wezilwane ukuqinisekisa imvelaphi yesilwane.

Kufanele kwaziwe Veterinarian elizocubungula isilwane. Xhumana ne nehhovisi le Veterinary eSikhungweni sangakini sezolimo, amahlathi kanye nezinhlanzi. Isilwane esizonikezela ngemvelaphi yaso kufanele sibe semimeni esihle sempilo. I Veterinarian izobhekisisa isidoda sezilwane nokunye okusemqoka. Emva kocwaningo, i Veterinarian izigcwalisa bese isayina ingxenye okufanele iyigcwalise efomini. Okunye ongakwenza ukuba uziqokele wena i Veterinarian.

Uma usuhambisile isicelo sakho, u Mbhalisi kanye nomkhiqizi wezilwane bazobhekisisa imininingwane ethulwe i Veterinarian kanye nocwaningo olwenziwe esilwaneni. Uma begculiseka, u Mbhalisi uzokwazisa ukuthi ulungiselele ukuhlolwa kwesilwane ukuze uthole isitifiketi. Uma kukhona okungahambi kahle, u Mbhalisi uzokwazisa ukuba  ulethe isitifiketi se karyotype . Uma u Mbhalisa nomkhiqizi wezilwane kanye ne yezilwane bengagculisekile bazokwazisa.

Qaphela: Uma ufuna ukuqoqa imvelaphi  yezilwane ezahlukene, kufanele ufake isicelo ngesilwane ngasinye.

Kuthatha izinsuku ezingamashumi amathathu.
