Title: Lenaneo la Mananeokgoparara a Bohlokwa
Source URL: https://www.gov.za/nso/services/services-organisations/business-incentives/lenaneo-la-mananeokgoparara-bohlokwa
Language: nso
Date scraped: 2025-03-05 17:21:57

==================================================

CIPke thušo ya mašeleng a kabelo ya tefo ya diprotšeke tše di diretšwego go kaonafatša mananeokgoparara a bohlokwa mo Afrika Borwa.

Thušo ya mašeleng e akaretša ditshenyegelo tša tlhabollo tša maswanedi go tloga go bonnyane bja 10% go ya go bontši bja 30% go ya go ditshenyegelo tša tlhabollo tša palomoka ya mananeokgoaparara a maswanedi.

Hwetša tshedimošo ka botlalo ka lenaneo le go tšwa goKgoro ya Bogwebi le Intaseteri.
