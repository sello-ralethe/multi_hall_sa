Title: Section 12i Tax Allowance Incentive (12i TAI)

Language: en
Date scraped: 2025-03-05 17:24:54

==================================================

The Section 12i Tax Allowance Incentive is designed to support Greenfield investments (i.e. new industrial projects that utilise only new and unused manufacturing assets), as well as Brownfield investments (i.e. expansions or upgrades of existing industrial projects). The new incentive offers support for both capital investment and training.
