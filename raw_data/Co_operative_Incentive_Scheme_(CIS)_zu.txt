==================================================

Isikhwama sosizo singamashumi ayisishiyagalolunye kuqhathaniswe neshumi lemali yosizo ezinkampanini ezisemthethweni. Inkampani yosizo kumazinga aphansi inamalunga ayisihlanu noma ngaphezulu obekungabantu abampomfu ngaphambilini.

Inhloso ukuba kwenziwencono izinga lebhizinisi elisizayo, ngokwehlisa inani lokuqhuba ibhizinisi nokuthuthukisa igunya lamabhizinisi abantu abansundu.
