Title: Ngwadiša khamphani ya gago
Source URL: https://www.gov.za/nso/services/services-organisations/register-business-or-organisation/ngwadi%C5%A1a-khamphani-ya-gago
Language: nso
Date scraped: 2025-03-05 17:11:13

==================================================

O ka ngwadiša khamphani ya gago bjalo ka khamphani yeo e sa dirego letseno goba yeo e dirago letseno le Komišene ya Dikhamphani le Ditheo tša Boikgopolelo le Boitlhamelo (CIPC). Khamphani go ya ka semolao ke motho yo a nago le bokgoni gotee le maatla a go itirela ka boyena. Molao o bona khamphani bjalo ka motho.

Dikhamphani tšeo di hwetšago letseno di ka kopana ka tlase ga mehuta ye e fapanego ya dikhampani ka fase ga Molao wa Dikhamphani.

Hwetša dintlha ka botlalo mabapi le go ngwadiša khamphani goKomišene ya Dikhamphani le Ditheo tša Boikgopolelo le Boitlhamelo (CIPC)goba o leletše (CIPC) mo go 086 100 2472.
