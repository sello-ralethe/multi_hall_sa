==================================================

Ukuze uhlanze isitshalo kufanele ubhalisele ku Mbhalisi we Act 36 ka 1947. Ukuhlanza izitshalo kusetshenziselwa ukuhlanza imikhiqizo yezilwane noma ngabe vertebrate or invertebrate oludalulwe uNgqongqoshe.

Ukuze uphumelele:

Uma u Mbhalisi esethole isicelo sakho nemininingwano yocwaningo, Ifomu locwaningo ku Act ka 1947 uzocwaninga ukuthi isihlanzi zithelo siyayilandela yini imigomo ye Act (Act 36 of 1947).

Uma uMbhalisi egculisekile ngocwaningo, isithelo sizobhaliswa bese kukhishwa isitifiketi. Isicifiketi singasetshenziswa izinyanga ezintathu kuphela. Kumele ubhalisele ukuvuselela isitifiketi ngo 31st ku Mach onyakeni la isitifiketi siphela isikhathi.

Qaphela: u Mbhalisi we Act 36 of 1947 angacisha isitifiketi noma inini uma:

Kuthatha isikhathi esingangenyanga
