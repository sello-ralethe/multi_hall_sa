Ukungenisa kuleli, ukukhiqiza noma ukudayisa ikhambi lezemvelo e Mzansi Afrika. Kumele ubhalise ne Registrar of Act 36 of 1947.

Uvunyelwe ukubhalisa uma ungowakuleli noma ongowakuleli ngokomthetho.

Uma ikhambi ofuna ukulibhalisela selibhaliswe omunye umintu, kufanele uthole imvumo kulowo muntu ngaphambi kokuba ubhalise.

Kufanele ufake ikhambi ephaketheni elihlanzekile futhi uluvale. Awuvunyelwe ukuthi uphinde usebenzise iphakethe uma usuthulule ikhambi. Uma ikhambi luwuketshezi, Kufanele uqaphelisise ukuthi ikhambi alichitheki uma usulithulula. Iphakethe noma isigubhu sakho kufanele sihambisane nemthetho ye South African National Standards 10229.
