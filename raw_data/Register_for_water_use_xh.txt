Title: Bhalisela ukusebenzisa amanzi
Source URL: https://www.gov.za/xh/services/services-organisations/permits-licences-and-rights/mining-and-water/bhalisela-ukusebenzisa
Language: xh
Date scraped: 2025-03-05 18:07:31

==================================================

Bonke abasebenzisa amanzi kufanele ba bhalise kwiSebe Lezamanzi. Kufanele ubhalisele ukusebenzisa amanzi ukuba awufumani manzi kumnikezeli ngenkonzo, ooGunyaziwe basekuhlaleni, ibhodi yamanzi, ibhodi Yonkcenkceshelo, uMasibambisa ka rhulumente okanye indawo ekhupha amanzi ngobuninzi ukuze:

Kwilixa elizayo, Xa abasebenzisa amanzi kufunwa bafake izicelo zeliyisenisi, abo abangabhalisanga baza kwehlisa amathuba abo okufumana ilayisenisi yokusebenzisa amanzi.

Bonke anantu okanye amaqumrhu okufunekaBabhalisele ukusebenzisa amanzibanga qhagamshela nayiphi naIofisi yesithili yeSebe Lezamanzi.
