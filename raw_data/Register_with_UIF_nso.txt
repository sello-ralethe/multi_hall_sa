Title: Ingwadiše bjalo ka mongmošomo UIF
Source URL: https://www.gov.za/nso/services/services-residents/world-work/uif/ingwadi%C5%A1e-bjalo-ka-mongmo%C5%A1omo-uif
Language: nso
Date scraped: 2025-03-05 18:32:02

==================================================

O gapeletšwa ke molao go ingwadiša le Sekhwama sa Inšorentshe ya Tahlegelo ya Mošomo (UIF) le go lefela sekhwama seo ge o thwetše bašomi diiri tše 24 le go feta ka kgwedi.

Tefelo ya kgwedi ya UIF ke diphesente tše pedi tša palomoka ya mogolo wa mošomi wa gago wa kgwedi. O swanetše go goga phesente e tee ya palomoka ya mogolo wa mošomi wa gago wa kgwedi le wena bjalo ka mongmošomo o lefele phesente e tee.
