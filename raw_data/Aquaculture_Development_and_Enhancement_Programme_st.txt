Title: Tlhabollo ya Bonono ba tse Phelang ka metsing le Lenaneo la Ntshetsopele (ADEP)

Language: st
Date scraped: 2025-03-05 17:15:42

==================================================

ADEP ke lenaneo la ditsiane le fumanehang ho mekgatlo ya Aforika Borwa e ngodisitsweng e sebetsang ka diketsahalo tsa sethato, tse mahareng le tse tlatseletsang tsa bonono ba tse phelang ka metsing. Hona ho bakeng sa ditshebeletso ka bobedi e leng tsa metsing le tsa metsi a hlwekileng (ho tswadisa dihlapi le thuo ya dihlapi) le tlhahiso, ho sebetsana le ho boloka bonono ba tse phelang ka metsing le dihlapi. Kerante e nehelwa ka ho otlolloha dikopo tse tjhaelletsweng monwana bakeng sa diporojeke tse ntjha, tse ntlafatswang kapa tse atoloswang.

Lenaneo le nehelana ka kerante ya ditjeho tse arolelanwang ho fihlela ho maksimamo ya R40 million ho ditjeo tsa ho ba maemong a amohelehileng ka metjhini le disebediswa, disebediswa tsa motheo tse ngata, thuo ya naha kapa meaho, ntlafatso ya meaho e hirilweng le diketsahalo tsa ntlafatso tse maemong a matle jwaloka ka ha di radilwe karolong ya 8 yamelao ya tataiso ya ADEP.

Thola lesedi le fetang lena mabapi le lenaneo lena ho tswa hoLefapha la Kgwebisano le Indaseteri.
