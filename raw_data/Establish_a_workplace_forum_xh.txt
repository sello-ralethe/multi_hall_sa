Title: Faka isicelo sokumilisela iqonga labasebenzi
Source URL: https://www.gov.za/xh/services/trade-unions/isicelo-sokuseka-iqonga-lasemsebenzini
Language: xh
Date scraped: 2025-03-05 18:29:03

==================================================

Iqonga labasebenzi yikomiti yabasebenzi ekhethwe ngabasebenzi emsebenzini. Badibana nabasebenzi rhoqo ukubonisana ngeendaba zomsebenzi.

Iqonga labasebenzi alithathi indawo yengxoxo mboniswano, kodwa libhekana nemiba engasombululwa ngothethwano kunokuya kwisithebe seengxoxo. Oku kuquka imiba enga xoxisi ngemivuzo, njengoku zisa ubuciko obutsha.

Amaqonga ase msebenzini amiselwa ukuze:

Fumana ngokupheleleyo malunganokumilisela iqonga labasebenziokanye qhagamshela kwikomishoni yothethwano, ukubonisana nokuthatha izigqibo (CCMA).
