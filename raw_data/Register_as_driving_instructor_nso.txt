Title: Kgopelo ya  go ingwadiša bjalo ka mohlahli wa bootledi
Source URL: https://www.gov.za/nso/services/services-organisations/driving-and-transport/kgopelo-ya-go-ingwadi%C5%A1a-bjalo-ka-mohlahli-wa
Language: nso
Date scraped: 2025-03-05 17:44:42

==================================================

Gore o kgone go tsebega semmušo bjalo ka motho yo a hlahlago batho bao ba ithutelago go otlela, o swanetše go ingwadiša le Kgoro ya Dinamelwa. Motho yo a nyakago go ba mohlahli wa baithutelabootledi o tlo šoma fela ka mohuta wa dikoloi tšeo a filwego laesense ya gore a hlahle batho ka tšona.

Hlokomela: O tlo hlahlela baithuti fela legoro la koloi yeo o filwego laesense ya lona. Setifikeiti sa mohlahli se šoma lebaka la ngwaga fela.

Phemiti e ka no ba e lokile mo nakong ya dibeke tše tshela go ya go tše seswai.

Ikgokaganye le kantoro ya tikologo ya geno ya dilaesense go tseba ka ga ditefo.

Difomo di hwetšagala mafelong a go dira diteko tša go otlela (mafelo a diteko).

Contact the licensing office at your local municipality
