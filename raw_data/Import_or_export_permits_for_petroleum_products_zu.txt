Uma ufuna ukuhambisa noma ukungenisa umkhiqizo wamafutha kuleli laseNingizimu Afrika, kufanele uqale uthole igunya emnyangweni wezokumbiwa phansi (DME) yokuba uthole imvume kwi-Nkomishani Ephethe ezohwebo zomhlaba (ITAC).

Ukungeniswa nokuhanjiswa kwemikhiqizo yamafutha kulawulwa imithetho nemigomo yokuhambisa nokungenisa amafutha kuleli kanti ke inkampani noma umuntu ofuna ukulanda noma ukuhambisa imikhiqizo yamafutha ngaphandle kufanele abe nemvume yokungenisa nokuhambisa impahla ngaphandle.

Ukunikezelwakwemvume yokungenisa noma ukuhambisa umkhiqizo wamafutha ngaphandle kwakuleli kuphethwe uMnyango wezokumbiwe phansi (DME).

Ukuze  ubhekelelwe ekutheni uthole igunya, kufanele uthintane ne DME maqondana nemigudu elandelwayo.

Izimpahla ezifana namafutha angahluziwe, amafutha emoto, upharafini, lubricating oils, ugesi wamafutha, petroleum jelly, petroleum bitumen and paraffin wax zingena ngaphansi kwemigomo ethile elawula ukungena nokuphuma kwamafutha kuleli.

Qaphela: imvume yokuhambisa impahla ngaphandle ihlala izinyanga ezingu 12 kanti eyokuzingenisa yona uhlala izinyanga eziyisithupha

Isikhathi esimisiwe sokukhishwa kwemvume yokuhambisa impahla ngaphandle iba izinsuku ezintathu sokusebenza.

ITAC ayikhokhisi mali ngokusiza kodwa kuyomele ukhokhe intela yosizo kwa SARS. Inani lentela libalwa kusukela esambeni saleyo mikhiqizo noma izimpahla ( ebekiwe ngokwezinhlelo kumakhasimende noma abathengi kanye nomthetho sivivinyo. Imininingwane ngentela ekhokhwayo vakashela I websaythi  yakwa SARS.
