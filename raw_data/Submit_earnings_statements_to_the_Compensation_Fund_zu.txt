==================================================

Njengomqashi obhalisile, kudingeka ufake ingxenye ethile yephesenti  lomholo womsebenzi esikhwameni sokunxephezela.

Kufaneleulethe izitatimendeesinemininingwane yale miholo njalo ngonyaka.

Izitatimende zemiholo kufanele zichaze uhlobo lwebhizinisi lakho, inani labasebenzi kanye nesamba obaholele sona (amaholo) ngesikhathi sokuhlolwa, kusukela ekuqaleni kukandasa kuze kube sekupheleni kukaNhlolanja (unyaka wentela).
