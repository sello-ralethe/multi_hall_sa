Ukuze uthumele izityalo nemveliso yazo, kufanele ufake isicelo semvume yokukhuseleka kwazo, esikhutshwa nguMbutho Wokhuseleko Lwezityalo (NPPO) kwilizwe elithengayo.Kufanele ufumanise ngemibandela yabo yokuthenga kwamanye amazwe ezokukhuseleka kwe mveliso echaphazela imveliso yakho yezityalo. Ukuba impahla  ayichasanga nemigaqo yokuthenga, iNPPO izakukhupha isiqinisekiso sokukhuseleka kwazo.Imveliso yezityalo kufanele ithunyelwe neqobelo lesiqinisekiso sokukhuseleka kwayo.

Ofanele kukwenza

It might take about 48 hours to process your application provided there are no additional activities.

Imali ehlawulwayo yowama 2025
