Title: Bhalisa ku-UIF ukuthi ungumqashi
Source URL: https://www.gov.za/zu/services/services-residents/world-work/uif/bhalisa-ku-uif-ukuthi-ungumqashi
Language: zu
Date scraped: 2025-03-05 18:31:56

==================================================

UMthetho udinga ukuthi ubhalise ku-UIF futhi unikele kulesi Sikhwama uma uqashe abantu isikhathi esingaphezu kwamahora angama-24 ngenyanga.

Imali oyinikela ku-UIF ngama-2% womholo ongakasuswa lutho womsebenzi wakho ngenyanga. Kumele uthathe i-1% emholweni ongakasuswa lutho womsebenzi wakho ngenyanga bese nawe njengomqashi unikela nge-1%.

Thola ulwazi oluthe xaxa mayelananokubhalisa komqashi kwi-UIFnoma thintana nabakwa-UIF.
