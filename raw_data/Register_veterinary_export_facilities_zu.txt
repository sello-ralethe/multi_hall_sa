Mayela nokufaka isicelo semvume uphinde ubhalisele igumbi lokuhlolwa kwezilwane ezithunyelwa kwamanye amazwe.Â Ungahambisa izilwane noma imikhiqizo yazo emazweni angaphandle kuphela uma zivela endaweni ehambisana nezinqubo mgomo ezisezingeni lomhlaba noma umthetho wokungenisa ezweni. Lezizindawo kungaba abahlinzi abasemthethweni, izindawo ezihlukanisa izilwane, izindawo zesidoda okungesona, indawo okuqoqwa kuyona imbewu, indawo egaya ukudla, indawo okubekwa kuyona ukudla.

Kuyahluka ngezifundazwe.

Inani liyahluka ngezifundazwe.

Amafomu awatholakali ngobuchwepheshe bokuxhumana, xhumana no dokotela wezilwane esifundazweni esiseduzane noma ku Mnyango wezolimo kuzwelonke.
