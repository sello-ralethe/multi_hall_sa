Ingabe ufisa ukuvula isikhungo sezemfundo ephakeme eNingizimu Afrika? Kumele uqale ubhalise njengesikhungo sezemfundo ephakeme esizimelekuMnyango Wezemfundo Ephakeme Nokuqeqesha(i-DHET) ukuze uthole igunya ngokomthetho lokuhlinzeka ngezinhlelo zemfundo ephakeme.

Izikhungo zezemfundo ephakeme ezizimele zihlinzeka ngamazingaoHlaka Lweziqu Lukazwelonke(i-NQF) (Amazinga esi-5 kuya kwele-10) anjengawamanyuvesi kahulumeni noma amanyuvesi ezobuchwepheshe.

Inhloso yokubhalisa ukuqinisekisa ukuthi:

Izinhlelo zezikhungo ezizimele kumele zibhaliswe ku-DHET, zigunyazweiKomidi Lezingaqophelo Lezemfundo Ephakeme (i-HEQC) loMkhandlu Wemfundo Ephakeme (i-CHE)futhi zibhaliwe ku-NQFuPhiko Lweziqu LwaseNingizimu Afrika (i-SAQA). Izinhlelo kumele zaziswe ezweni lonke nakumazwe ngamazwe.

Cishe ezinyangeni eziyi-18 ngaphambi kokuba isikhungo sakho sifise ukusebenza, kumele uthumele isicelo sokubhaliswa kuNhloko Yezikhungo Zezemfundo Ephakeme Ezizimele. Isicelo Sokuguqulwa kumele sithunyelwe ngosuku olunqunywe iNhloko Yesikhungo (uMqondisi Jikelele).

Qaphela: Sicela uxhumane neNhloko Yesikhungo ngoshintsho uma ufisa ukulwenza ngenqubo yesicelo.
