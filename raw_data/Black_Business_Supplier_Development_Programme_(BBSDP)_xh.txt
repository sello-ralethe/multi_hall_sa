Inkqubo phuhliso yokunika amashishini amnyama (BBSDP) sisibonelelo sokuhlula indleko esinikwa oosomashishini abakhasayo abamnyama ukubanceda ukuthi baphucule ukwazi ukumelana namanye amashishini kwabo nokuthi ishishini linyamezele. Oku kukubanceda ukuthi babe yinxalenye yoqoqosho nokuvula amathuba emisebenzi.

Lenkqubo inika isibonelelo esifika kwisigidi seeranti
