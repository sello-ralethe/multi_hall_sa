Title: Isicelo sokubhalisela ukusetshenziswa kwamanzi
Source URL: https://www.gov.za/zu/services/services-organisations/permits-licences-and-rights/mining-and-water/isicelo-sokubhalisela
Language: zu
Date scraped: 2025-03-05 18:07:38

==================================================

Bonke abasebenzisa amanzi kudingeka babhalise kuMnyango wezaManzi. Kufanele ubhalisele ukusebenzisa amanzi uma ungawatholi amanzi kumhlinzeki wezinsiza, iziphathimandla zasendaweni, ibhodi yezamanzi, ibhodi yezokuchelela, isikimu sezamanzi sikahulumeni noma abanye abahlinzeki abakhulu futhi amanzi uwasebenzisela lokhu:

Esikhathini esizayo, uma abasebanzisi bamanzi kudingeka bafake izicelo zamalayisensi, labo abangazange babhalise bazokwehlisa amathuba abo okuthola ilayisensi yokusebenzisa amanzi.

Bonke abantu noma izinhlangano okudingekazibhalisele ukusebenzisa amanzizingathinta noma ngeliphiiHhovisi lesiZinda loMnyango wezaManzi.
