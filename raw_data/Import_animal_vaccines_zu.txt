Ungangenisa noma unikezele noma udayise imithi yokusetshenziswa ezilwaneni eNingizimu Afrika uma usubhalisile ngokuvundisa, ukufida emphulazini,Amakhambi ezolimo kanye namakhambi emfuyo womthetho 1947( Umthetho 36 ka 1947).Uma ufuna ukungenisa imithi egunyaziwe ukuze isetshenziswe ezilwaneni, kumele uthole imvume yokuhlolwa kwezilwane kuMqondisi obhekele ezempilo zezilwane. Lemvume yokungenisa iqunyazwe okwesikhashana esincane kanye nomthamo owodwa vo. Kunemithetho yokungeniswa nokuhlolwa kwezilwane ebekiwe. Njengalokhu imithetho yezempilo ishintsha njalo njalo,Sicela ithintane noMqondisi obhekele ezempilo zezilwane (uCingo 012 319 7507/7684/7700/7673/7456, Isikhahlamezi 012 329 6892,VetPermits@daff.gov.za) ukuze uthole izinto okumele uziphathe ngaleso sikhathi.Uma ufuna ukungenisa imithi engagunyaziwe ngenhloso yokuzoyizama ukuyisebenzisa lapha eNingizimu Afrika, udinga izimvume ezimbili. Imvume eyodwa evela kuMbhalisi womthetho 36 ka 1947 enye ivele ku Mqondisi obhekele ezempilo zezilwane.Isicelo semvume esisuka kuMbhalisi wothetho 36 ka1947 kumele siphelezelwe isicelo esiphelele semithetho yokuzama ukuyisebenzisa.Umqondisi obhekele ezempilo zezilwane wenza aphinde angenise imvume kuphela uma sebethole imvume ekopeliwe ekhishwe uMbhalisi womthetho 36 ka 1947. Imithi engeniswe ngenhloso yokuzama ukuyisebenzisa akumele neze ithengiswe. Uma kunesimo esiphuthumayo lapho kuqubuka khona isifo kanti kayikho imithi ekhona ezweni ukulapha, kungadingeka ukuba kusetshenziswe imithi engabhalisiwe noma engagunyaziwe.

Emva kwalokho ke, wena oqondene nokungenisa kumele ufake isicelo ku,Medicines Control Councilwohla lomthetho 21 ngokuphathelene nemithi kanye nokuphathelene noketshezi okuqaphelwe ngokomthetho (umthetho 101 ka 1965, njengalokho uchibiyelwe ngomthetho 90 ka 1997).Â Kudingeka futhi , kuMqondisi obhekele ezempilo zezilwane, imvume yokungena kwemithi ukungena kwelinye izwe. Bazokwenzela lemvume yokungena uma usuthole igunya kwiMedicines Control Councilwaleth nezinkomba. Lapha ke ,imithi engabhalisiwe ingathengiswa okwesikhathi esithize kuze kuba iyabhaliswa.

Qaphela: Azikho izicelo eziyocutshungulwa ngaphandle kokukhokha kuqalauphinde uveze izinkomba. Izinkomba kukhokha zingathunyelwa ngesikhahlamezi ku: 012 329 8292 /VetPermits@daff.gov.za.

Uma nje udokotela kahulumeni kanye nehhovisi likazwelonke selisamukele isicelo sokungenisa, kuthatha izinsuku ezintathu kuya kwezinhlanu ukucubungula isicelo nokukhipha imvume.
