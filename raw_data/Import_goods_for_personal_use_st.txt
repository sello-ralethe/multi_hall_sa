Title: Kopo ya ho amohela diphahlo tsa ditswantle tsa bowena kapa tsa motho

Language: st
Date scraped: 2025-03-05 17:33:00

==================================================

Haeba o batla ho tlisa dithepa tse laotsweng kapa tse sebedisitsweng kahara Aforika Borwa, o hloka tumello ho tswa ho Khomishene ya Tsamaiso ya Kgwebisano Matjhabeng (ITAC).

Lenane la thepa e itshetlehileng mehatong ya taolo ya ho tliswa le ho romelwa ha thepa le fumaneha howww.itac.org.za. Ha o isa dintlha tsa hao tseo ho ka iteanywang le wena ka tsona, lenane le ka boela la poswa, la fekeswa kapa la imeilelwa ho wena.

Ho ka nka nako e ka fihlang ho matsatsi a mararo ho phethela tshebetso.

Khomishene ya Tsamaiso ya Kgwebo ya Matjhaba ha e lefise ditjeho dife kapa dife tsa tshebeletso empa o tla lokela ho lefa lekgetho la madiboho ho Ditshebeletso tsa Lekeno la Aforika Borwa (Sars). Lekgetho la madiboho le sebetswa e le phesente ya boleng ba thepa( e behilweng ho disekejule tsa Molao wa Lekgetho la Madiboho le Ditefello). Ha o batla ho tseba haholwanyane ka Lekgetho la Madibohoetela webosaete ya Sars.
