Title: Ukususa utshwala obungeniswayo obubuya ngaphandle
Source URL: https://www.gov.za/zu/services/liquor/ukususa-utshwala-obungeniswayo-obubuya-ngaphandle
Language: zu
Date scraped: 2025-03-05 18:05:01

==================================================

Uma ufuna ukususa utshwala obusuka ngaphandle esikhumulweni semikhumbi, kumele ufake isicelo sokuthola isitifiketi sokususa utshwala emNyangweni weZolimo, Amahlathi kanye nokuDoba okhona eMtateni noma esikhumulweni semikhumbi ukuze ukwazi ukubuhambisa uyodayisa noma yikuphi eNingizimi Afrika.

Uma uMnyango weZolimo namaHlathi kanye nokuphathelene nokuDoba usivuma isicelo sakho, bayobe sebenikezela utshwala bakho obuvaliwe endaweni lapho kugcinwa khona izimpahla ngendlela  ephephile  maqede bese bazisa uMhloli wakuleyo ndawo yokubeka izimpahla ephephile.

Kumele uhambe uye lapho kubekwe khona utshwala bakho ufike unikeze uMhloli oyomfica khona lencwadi noma isitifiketi sakho  sokususa utshwala nokuthi uphinde uthuthe bonke utshwala bakho phambi kwakhe.

Angeke uvunyelwe ukususa utshwala esikhumulweni semikhumbi ubuhambise noma yikuphi lapho ungabudayisa khona lapha eNingizimu Afrika ngaphandle kwephepha mvume.

Kasikho isikhathi esibekiwe salokhu.
