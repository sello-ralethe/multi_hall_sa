Title: Bhalisela ukuba umsunguli (umakhi noma umkhiqizi wezimoto)

==================================================

Maqondana nokubhalisa njengomkhiqizi noma njengomhwebi wezimoto ezingena ezweni ezivela ngaphandle

Uma ufuna ukukhiqiza, ukungenisa izimoto ezweni noma ukwakha izimoto eNingizimu Afrika ukuze uthole inzuzo, kufanele ubhalise nomnyango wezokuthutha wesifundazwe.

Uma umnyango sewuthole isicelo sakho, uzothumela umhloli ukuze anqume ukuthi ingabe uyahambisana yini nezimiso zomthetho ezifanele. Kuzofanele ibhizinisi lakho liqinisekiswe ngokusemthethweni ukuthi alinacala yini wuphiko lwezamaphoyisa lwaseNingizimu Afrika i-South African Police Service (SAPS).

Ukubhalisa kuncike emvumeni ka-MEC ofanele futhi nesitifiketi sikhishwa ngaleyo mvume.

Ukuze uthole izindleko xhumana nomnyango wakho wasekhaya wezokuthutha.
