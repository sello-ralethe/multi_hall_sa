Title: Izinhlelo zemigomo yebhizinisi
Source URL: https://www.gov.za/zu/services/services-organisations/business-incentives/izinhlelo-zemigomo-yebhizinisi
Language: zu
Date scraped: 2025-03-05 17:17:29

==================================================

Umongameli wase Ningizimu Afrika usungule izinhlelo zemigomo yamabhizinisi etholakala ngokusebenzisa uhlelo lwangaphandle kusukela ngo Ntulikazi kunyaka ka 2007. Phakathi kuka Ntulikazi kunyaka ka 2007 kanye no Ndasa kunyaka ka 2010, loluhlele luvulele amathuba omsebenzi abalelwa kwizigidi eziyisithupha okwaheha abatshalizimali ezibalelwa kwizigidi ezingamakhulu amathathu nantathu. Loluhlelo olubhekele imigomo yamabhizinisi lihlose ukuhela abatshalizimali kanye novula amathuba omsebenzi e Ningizimu Afrika.

Imininingwano iyatholakala kumnyango wezohwebo.
