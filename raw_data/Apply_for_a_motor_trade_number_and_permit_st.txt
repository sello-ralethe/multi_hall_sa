Title: Ho etsa kopo ya phemiti ya kgwebo ya koloi le nomoro
Source URL: https://www.gov.za/st/services/services-organisations/driving-and-transport/ho-etsa-kopo-ya-phemiti-ya-kgwebo-ya-koloi-le
Language: st
Date scraped: 2025-03-05 17:43:45

==================================================

Bakeng sa ho kganna koloi e sa ngodiswang mmileng wa setjhaba mona Afrika Borwa, o lokela ho etsa kopo ya phemiti ya kgwebo ya koloi le nomoro pele.

Tlhoko ena e sebetsa ho basebedisi bohle ba makoli, ho kenyelletswa le bahlahisi, ba tlisang makoloi ho tswa dinaheng tsa ka ntle, boradikonteraka, dutheo tsa ditjhelete kapa bahwebi ba batlang ho rekisa, ho fapanyetsana, ho lokisa kapa ho tlisa makoloi ho ditlelaente tsa ona.

Leba kantorong ya heno ya sephethephethe e haufinyana o tle le tse latelang:

Tlatsa foromo ya MTN 1 bolaoding ba sebakeng sa heno ba ngodiso.

Bolela hore ke ka baka lang o batlang dinomoro tsa kgwebo le palo ya dinomoro tsa kgwebo tseo o di batlang.

Kopo e sebetswa ka lona letsatsi leo.

Iteanye le ofisi ya heno ya dilaesense bakeng sa ditjeho.

Foromo ya ho etsa kopo  ya Nomoro ya Kgwebo ya Koloi - MTN1 (Application and notice i.r.o motor trade number and blank temporary permits [Form MTN1])

Dintlha tsa ho iteanya

Contact the registration authority service centre at your local municipality
