Title: Lenaneo la Thekgo ya Boeti
Source URL: https://www.gov.za/nso/services/services-organisations/business-incentives/lenaneo-la-thekgo-ya-boeti
Language: nso
Date scraped: 2025-03-05 17:49:03

==================================================

Lenaneo la Thekgo ya Boeti le bušetšwa tšhelete ya seatleng yeo maikemišetšo a yona e lego go thekga tlhabollo ya dikgwebo tša boeti tšeo di tlago hlohleletša tlholo ya mešomo le go oketša phatlalalo ya go ya ka peakanyo ya mafelo ya peeletšo ya boeti.

Thušo ya mašeleng ke ya tlhomo goba koketšo ya ditshepedišo tša boeti go swana le:

Hwetša tshedimošo ka botlalo gotšwa go Kgoro ya Boeti.
