Title: Register old age home
Source URL: https://www.gov.za/services/services-organisations/register-business-or-organisation/register-old-age-home
Language: en
Date scraped: 2025-03-05 17:09:30

==================================================

If you want to register an older persons' residential facility or old age home as an individual or organisation, you must apply at your nearest Department of Social Development office.

It may take four weeks to process your application.

The actual application is free but you will pay for other certificates such as the environmental health certificate.

The application forms are available from your nearestsocial development office.
