Title: Ukuvavanywa kweesampuli ezigunyazisiweyo zembewu

==================================================

Ukuze kuvavanywe ngokusemthethweni iisampulu zembewu ukuba zisulungekile na ngenkangeleko, ukukhula kunye/okanye zikulungele na ukutyalwa, abahlalutyi beesampulubeSebe lezoLimo, amaHlathi kunye nokuLoba kufuneka bathathe iisampulu.

Iisampulu zembewu ngokusemthethweni kufuneka zithobele imigangatho yoMthetho wokuPhuculwa kweziTyalo (PIA). Ukuba imbewu ayiyithobelanga imigangatho yoMthetho, kufuneka irhoxiswe ingathengiswa okanye ibhalwe elinye igama.

Qaphela:

Iingxelo ngeemvavanyo ezenziweyo zithunyelwa kumhlalutyi wesampulu lowo ebethathe isampulu. Ukuba uyazifuna ezi ngxelo, iNgxelo yokuHlalutywa kwembewu iya kukhutshwa kwaye ixabiso elifunekayo liya kuhlawulwa.

Khumbula ukufaka inombolo yoluhlu lwamaxabiso ngeenjongo zokuba kwaziwe ukuba ifakwe ngubani na imali.

Hlawula umrhumo wokuvavanya imbewu uze uthumele ubungqina bentlawulo ku-OSTL ngefeksi okanye nge-imeyili ukuze kukhululwe ingxelo.

Iintlobo zezityalo zivavanywa phantsi kweemeko ezahlukileyo kwaye ubude bovavanyo bungohluka ukusuka kwiintsuku ezimbalwa ukuya kwiiveki ezimbalwa.
