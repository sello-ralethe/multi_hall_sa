Title: Icebo lokukhuthaza oomasibambisane
Source URL: https://www.gov.za/xh/services/services-organisations/business-incentives/icebo-lokukhuthaza-oomasibambisane
Language: xh
Date scraped: 2025-03-05 17:21:20

==================================================

Icebo lokukhuthaza oomasibambisane (CIS) linomlinganiselo ka 90no10 ukulinganiswa noomasibambisane ababhalisiweyo. Oomasibambisane banamalungu amahlanu nangaphezulu ibengabantu ababecalucalulwa ngokwembali.

Injongo kukuphucula ukusebenza komasibambisane ngokwehlisa indleko zabo zokulawula ishishini ngoncedo oluhambisana nophuculo loqoqosho lwabo bonke abamnyama i BBBEE.

Fumana ngokubanzi ngaleDepartment of Small Business Development.
