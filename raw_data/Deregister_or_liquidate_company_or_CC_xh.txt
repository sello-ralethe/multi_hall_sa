Ukuba inkamapni yakho okanye umasibambisane uyayeka ukusebenza,ungayisula kwikhomishoni yobumnini bekhampani nolwazi (CIPC).

Fumana ulwazi malunga nokusula okanye ukubhangisa ikhampani kwiKhomishoni yobumnini beekhampani nolwazi (CIPC)okanye qhagamshela i CIPC apha 086 100 2472.
