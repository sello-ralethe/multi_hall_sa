Title: Isicelo sokubhalisa amayeza ezolimo

Xa ufuna ukuthenga kumazwe angaphandle, ukwenza, okanye ukuthengisa amachiza ezolimo eMzantsi Afrika kufanele ubhalisa phantsi komthetho 36 wonyaka ka 1947.

Ungakwazi ukubhalisa ukuba ungummi welomzantsi Afrika kuphela  okanye umbutho one ofisi kweli lase Mzantsi Afrika.

Ukuba elichiza selibhaliswe phantsi komnye umntu kuyafuneka ufumane imvume  kuloomntu phambi kokuba ulibhalise.

Faka amachiza kwisingxobo esicicekiley nesivalekileyo.  Awuvumelekanga ukuba uphinde usisebenzise isingxbobo emva kokuba usisebenzisile. Apho elichiza lingamanzi, qinisekisa ukuthi isingxobo asichithi ichiza xa ulikhupha. Isingxobo sakho kufuneka singa chasane nommiselo kazwelonke wase Mzantsi Afrika 10229.

Isingxobo sakho masibhale:

Sithabatha iinyanga ezisithandathu ukuya kwezilishumi nambini.
