==================================================

Uma ufuna ukufaka omaka ezilwaneni ngenhloso yokwenza imali, kufanele ufane isicelo kwa Department of Agriculture, Land Reform and Rural Development(Registrar: Animal Identification) ukuze ubhaliselwe ukufaka omaka ezilwaneni.

Kufanele ufundele lokhu okulandelayo:

Njengomuntu ofaka omaka ezilwaneni, kufanele ugcine uhla lobufakazi bezilwane ozifake umaka.

Uzobhaliswa ukuba ufake omaka ezilwaneni uma usuphothule izifundo eziqondene nalomsebenzi.

R 200

Isikhungo sinukezela ngamafomu uma sewuphothule isifundo esibhekelene nalomsebenzi.
