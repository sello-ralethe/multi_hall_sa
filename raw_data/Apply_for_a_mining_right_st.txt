Title: Etsa kopo bakeng sa tokelo ya ho rafa
Source URL: https://www.gov.za/st/services/services-organisations/permits-licences-and-rights/mining-and-water/etsa-kopo-bakeng-sa
Language: st
Date scraped: 2025-03-05 18:06:50

==================================================

Ha o batla tokelo ya ho rafa,o hloka tumello ho tswa ho Lefapha la Mehlodi ya Dirashwa hore o ka rafa dirashwa tulong e itseng.

Tokelo ya ho rafa ha e ya tshwanela ho feta nako ya dilemo tse 30.

Tokelo ya ho rafa e dumellwa haeba:

Ikopanye le Lefapha la Mehlodi ya Dirashwa bakeng sa ditjeho.

Dikopo di tlameha ho kengwainthaneteng.
