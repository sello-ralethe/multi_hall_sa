Malunga nokubkalisa isikhululo sovavanyo lwezithuthi (i-VTS)

I-VTS liziko elivavanya ukulungela kwezithuthi ukuba zisetyenziswe kwiindlela  zikawonkewonke zaseMzantsi Afrika. Ukuba isithuthi silupasile uvavanyo, isatifiketi sokulungela ukuhamba endleleni siza kunikezwa.

Ukubhalisa isikhululo sovavanyo lwezithuthi (i-VTS), kufuneka ubhale iafidavithi okanye isiqiniselo esichaza izizathu zokubhalisa oko uze uyi(si)ngenise ku-MEC.

Iafidavithi okanye isiqiniselo e(si)lindelekileyo kufuneka i(si)qulathe:

Ukuba nje i(si)funyanwe, u-MEC uza kusithathela ingqalelo isicelo sakho.

Ukuba isicelo sakho samkelwe, oku kulandelayo kufuneka kuhlangatyezwane nako:

Ukuhlolwa nokuvavanywa kwesithuthi kwisikhululo sovavanyo njengoko kufuneka kuluhlu lwemithetho yendlela yokusebenza emayilandelwe SABS 047 "Ukuvavanywa kwezithuthi ukuba zilungele ukuhamba endleleni".

Ofanele kukwenza

Qaphela: Ungalindeleka ukuba ungenise amaxwebhu njengoko kufunwa ngu-MEC.

Ixesha elimiselweyo

Kungathatha iinyanga ezintandathu ukuqhuba isicelo sakho.

Qhagamshelana neofisi yonikezo zilayisenisi yengingqi yakho ukufumana indleko.
