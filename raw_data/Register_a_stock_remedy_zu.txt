Title: Ukubhalisela amakhambi emfuyo

Language: zu
Date scraped: 2025-03-05 18:22:26

==================================================

Ukungenisa, ukukhiqiza noma ukudayisa amakhambi emfuyo e Ningizimu Afrika kufanele ubhalise ku Act 36 of 1947.

Imithi yemfuyo isetshenziswa emfuyweni, ezinhlanzini nasezilwaneni eziyingozi:

Inkampani yakwamanye amazwe angeke ikwazi ukufaka isicelo kuleli lase Ningizimu Afrika. Ungakwazi ukubhalisa kuleli uma inkampani yakho engeyakuleli noma.

Kumele ube naloku okulandelayo:

Uma ufuna ukubhalisa imakhambi akhona noma igama lomuthi osukhona ngelinye igama lenkampani kufanele ube nencwadi ephuma kumnikazi obhalisile ekugunyazayo ukuba ubhalise imithi.

hhovisi lika Mbhalisi lithumela izincwadi zokuvuselela kanye namafomu okufaka zicelo kibobonke ababhalisile ngaphambilini.
