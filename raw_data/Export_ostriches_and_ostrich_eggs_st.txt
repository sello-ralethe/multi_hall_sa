==================================================

Ho tsamaisa dimptjhe kapa mahe a dimptjhe, o tlameha ho etsa kopo ya phemiti ya ho tsamaisa ho tswa ho Mongodisi wa Ntshetsopele ya Diphoofolo matsatsi a 30 pele ho tsamaiso.

Ha o entse kopo, o tla fumana tumello ho tswa ho ba Ditjhemba tsa Kgwebo ya Dimptjhe ba Aforika Borwa (South African Ostrich Business Chamber).

Ebang ho ena le lefu la dinonyana kapa bohloko bofe kapa bofe, dimptjhe di ka se tsamaiswi hofihlela matshwao a lefu a nyametse.

O tlamheha ho ba motho wa molao, ho tea mohlala: John Peter Citizen, kapa John Peter Citizen T/A Diepsloot Stud, kapa Diepsloot Stud CC, kapa Diepsloot Stud (Pty) Ltd, kapa Diepsloot Ltd.
