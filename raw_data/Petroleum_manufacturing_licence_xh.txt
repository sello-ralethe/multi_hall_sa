Title: Isicelo sephepha-mvume lokuvelisa ivaselina

Language: xh
Date scraped: 2025-03-05 18:11:25

==================================================

Iphepha-mvume lokuvelisa ivaselina liyakuvumela ukuba uvelise izinto ezenziwa ngevaselina uzithengise kwabanamaphepha-mvume okuthengisa ngezixa ezikhulu, nabanye abathengisi ngaphandle kwabathengisa ngaphandle kelizwe.

Qaphela: akukho mntu onokuvelisa ivaselina engenawo amaphepha-mvume.

Ungathathwa njengonalo ihepha-mvume lokuvelisa ivaselina, kodwa kufuneka ufake isicelo zingaphelanga iinyanga ezintandathu zokuqala ukusebenza komthetho xa bowusele usebenza njengomvelisi wevaselina ngaphambi komhla weshumi elinesixhenxe ku Matshi 2006.

Isigqibo kufuneka sithathwe zingadlulanga iintsuku ezingama-90 ukususela kumhla iSebe elifumene ngawo isiqinisekiso sokupapasha isaziso sesicelo.
