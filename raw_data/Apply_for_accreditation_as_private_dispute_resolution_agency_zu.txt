Ukuqashelwa kwezinhlangano ezizimele ezinikeza izinsizakalo zokuxazululwa kwezingxabanoInhlangano ezimele noma umkhandlu wezingxoxo ungase ufake isicelo kuKhomishana Yokubuyisana, Ukuxazulula Nokwahlulela (i-CCMA) icele ukuba iqashelwe ukuba: uxazulule izingxabano ngokubuyisana, futhi:

I-CCMA ingase idinge imininingwane eyengeziwe esekela isicelo futhi, ngenxa yaleyo njongo, ingase idinge ukuba ofake isicelo abe khona kumhlangano noma kwimihlangano eyobe ihleliwe.

Ukuthola ulwazi oluthe xaxamayelana nesitifiketi sezinhlangano ezizimele thintana ne-CCMA.
