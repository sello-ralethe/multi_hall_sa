Title: Kopo ya ho ngodiswa o le motshwai
Source URL: https://www.gov.za/st/services/services-organisations/permits-licences-and-rights/animal-improvement/kopo-ya-ho-ngodiswa
Language: st
Date scraped: 2025-03-05 18:02:49

==================================================

Ha o batla ho tshwaya diphoofolo bakeng sa ho etsa tjhelete, o tlameha ho etsa kopo ho ba Lefapha la Temothuo, Meru le Botshwasi (Mongodisi: Tshebahatso ya Diphoofolo) hore o ngodiswe jwaloka motshwai.Hore o ngodiswe o tlameha ho phetha dithuto tse latelang:

Jwaloka motshwai, o lebelletswe ho boloka lenane la diphoofolo tsohle tseo o ditshwahileng.

O tla ngodiswa o le motshwai hafela o phethetse dithuto.

R 200

Setheo sa thuto se fana ka diforomo ha o phethetse dithuto.
