Ukwenza okanye ukuthengisa isichumiso esikwiqela loku-1, kufuneka ubhalise kwiGosa loBhaliso loMthetho wama-36 we-1947. Izichumiso zeqela loku-1 ziqulathe ngaphezulu kwe-100kg zenitrogen (N), phosphorus (P) nepotassium (K) njengezithako eziphambili. Zisekwaziwa njengezichumiso ezingengowo umgquba.Kufuneka ube ne-ofisi esemthethweni eMzantsi Afrika okanye umntu wasemthethweni one-ofisi eMzantsi Afrika ukuze ube selungelweni.Ukuba isichumiso ofuna ukusibhalisa sesibhaliswe ngomnye umntu, kufuneka ufumane imvume ebhaliweyo evela kulowo ubhalise isichumiso phambi kokuba usibhalise.Kufuneka uthumele isichumiso kuyo nayiphi na indlu yophando lwenzululwazi esemthethweni phambi kokufaka isicelo. Indlu yophando lwenzululwazi iya kuhlalutya kwaye ivavanye isichumiso ize ikunike isiqinisekiso sohlalutyo. IGosa loBhaliso aliyi kusamkela isicelo sakho ngaphandle kwesiqinisekiso sohlalutyo esivela kwindlu yophando lwenzululwazi esemthethweni.Qaphela: Kufuneka uhlaziye ubhaliso lwakho rhoqo emva kweminyaka emithathu phambi kwama-31 kweyoMnga.
