Title: Lenaneo la Ditekanyetso tsa Diporojeke tsa Khephithale

Language: st
Date scraped: 2025-03-05 17:20:04

==================================================

CPFP ke lenaneo la karolelano ya ditjeo le nang le seabo ho ditjeo dipatlisisong tsa ditekanyetso tse lebisang ho diporojeke tse etsetswang kantle ho Aforika Borwa, tse tlang ho eketsa dithoto tsa lehae tse rekiswang kantle ho naha le ho phephetsa maraka bakeng sa dithoto le ditshebeletso tse kgolo tsa Aforika Borwa.

Thola lesedi le fetang lena mabapi le lenaneo lena ho tswa hoLefapha la Kgwebisano le Indaseteri.
