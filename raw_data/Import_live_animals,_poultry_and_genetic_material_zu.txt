Uma ufuna ukungenisa imfuyo, izinkukhu kanye nokuhambisana nozalo lwezilwlane njengesidoda nembewu kufanele ubhalisele imvumo no Mbhalisi obhekele ukwenzna ngcono izilwane ukuze ukwazi ukungenisa izilwane noma uzalo e Ningizimu Afrika. Kufanele ubhalise ngaphambi kwezinsuku ezingamashumi amathathu ngaphambi kokuba ungenise izilwane.Kufanele futhi ubhalisele isitifiketi se verterinaty ku Mqondisi obhekele impilo yezilwane.
