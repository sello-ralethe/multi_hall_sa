Title: Ukufaka isicelo selungelo lokuvukuza emayini
Source URL: https://www.gov.za/zu/services/services-organisations/permits-licences-and-rights/mining-and-water/ukufaka-isicelo
Language: zu
Date scraped: 2025-03-05 18:06:37

==================================================

Uma ufuna ilungelo lokuvukuza, udinga imvume evela kuMnyango wezokuMbiwa Phansi yokuvukuza izimbiwa phansi endaweni ethile.

Ilungelo lokuvukuza akufanele leqe isikhathi seminyaka engama-30.

Ilungelo lokuvukuza liyanikezwa uma:

Maleyana nezindleko, thintana noMnyango Wezokumbiwa Phansi.

Izicelo kufanele zilethweku-inthanethi.
