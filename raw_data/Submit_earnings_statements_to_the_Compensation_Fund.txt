Title: Submit earnings statements to the Compensation Fund
Source URL: https://www.gov.za/services/compensation-fund/submit-earnings-statements
Language: en
Date scraped: 2025-03-05 18:31:20

==================================================

As a registered employer, you are required to contribute a certain percentage of employee’s earnings to the Compensation Fund.

You mustsubmit a statementdetailing these earnings every year.

The earning statements must stipulate the nature of your business, the number of employees and the amount you have paid them (salaries and wages) for the assessment period, from the beginning of March to the end of February (tax year).

Find out more aboutsubmitting earning statements to the Compensation Fundor contact theCompensation Fund.
