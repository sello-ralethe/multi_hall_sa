Title: iLayisensi yenqanawa yokuloba yeli lizwe

Language: xh
Date scraped: 2025-03-05 17:55:40

==================================================

Ukuze uqhube isikhephe sokuloba ekhaya eMzantsi Afrika kufanele isikhephe sibhaliswe kubalawuli bamanxweme. Kufanele ufake isicelo sesiqinisekiso sokukhuseleka kwesikhephe kwabasemagunyeni bokhuseleko lwaselwandle base Mzantsi Afrika (South African Maritime Safety Authority) kunye ne layisenisi yesikhephe sokuloba kwiSebe Lezolimo, Amamhlathi nokuLoba eKapa.

Ukuze uphumelele kufanele ube:

Isikhephe sokuloba ekhaya sinezinto zonke, izinto zokusebenza, indawo zokugcina, impahla namafutha akhwelisiweyo. Isenokuquka ukungceda enye okanye ezinye izikhephe ezikwenza umsebenzi wokuloba.

Okumele kwenziwe kuquka oku kulandelyo kodwa akuphelelanga apha:

Qaphela: Kukophula umthetho ukusebenzisa isikhephe esingabhaliswanga. Ukuba awuna lungelo lokuloba, kufuneka ube nesivumelano nomntu one lungelo lokuloba ukuze ukwazi ukuqhuba isikhephe sakho.

Isikhephe esibhaliselwe ukuloba ukwenza unzuzo asikwazi kusetyenziselwa ukuloba kokuzonwabisa.Isikhephe esibhaliselwe ukulobela inzuzo sinelungelo elahlukileyo lokuloba kwaye linga sebenziswa kwelo lungelo kuphela.

Ukuphela kwexesha le Layisinisi yokuloba ixhomekeke kwixesha lesiqinisekiso. iLayisenisi yesikhepha sokuloba inexesha elilingana nelesiqinisekiso seSAMSA.

Isicelo singa thatha intsuku ezintathu zokusebenza okanye ngaphezulu ukususela ngemini yesiqinisekiso sentlawulo, nokuthi isicelo sakho silandele imiqathango kakuhle.
