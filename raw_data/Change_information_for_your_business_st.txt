Title: Tsebiso ya ditokiso tsa setatemente sa motheo sa khampani ya ba mmalwa (CC)
Source URL: https://www.gov.za/st/services/services-organisations/change-registration/tsebiso-ya-ditokiso-tsa-setatemente-sa-motheo
Language: st
Date scraped: 2025-03-05 17:12:30

==================================================

Ha o etsa diphethoho dife kapa dife ho Khoporeishine ya hao ya Tshebedisano (CC), jwale ka ho hlwaya ofisiri e ntjha ya dibuka, o fetola aterese ya hao ya poso, o fetola kabo ya diphesente tsa dishere kapa o eketsa kapa o hlakola setho, o lokela ho fetola dintlha tsohle tsa CC dikantorong tsa Khomishine ya Thepa le Boikgathatso ba Dikhamphani (CIPC).

CIPC e hloka hore o boloke dintlha tsohle tsa CC ya hao di le motjheng ka dinako tsohle.

Thola lesedi le fetang lena mabapi le ho fetola tlhahisolesding bakeng sa Khoporeishine ya hao ya Tshebedisano (CC) hoKhomishine ya Thepa le Boikgathatso ba Dikhamphani (CIPC)kapa letsetsa CIPC ho 086 100 2472.
