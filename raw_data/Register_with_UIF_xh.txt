Title: Bhalisa nje ngomqeshi kwi UIF
Source URL: https://www.gov.za/xh/services/services-residents/world-work/uif/bhalisa-nje-ngomqeshi-kwi-uif
Language: xh
Date scraped: 2025-03-05 18:31:50

==================================================

Uyanyaneliswa ngumthetho ukuba ubhalise kwingxowa mali yabangaphangeliyo (Unemployment Insurance Fund (UIF) kwaye ufake imali kulengxowa mali ukuba uqashe abantu abasebenza ngapezulu kweyure ezingamashumi amabini anesine ngenyanga.

Inxaso mali oyifakayo ku UIF iziiphesenti ezimbhini zomvuzo wonke wenyanga womqashwa. Kufanele uthathe iphesenti ibenye kumvuzo womsebenzi kanti wena njengomqashi ufake iphesenti ibenye.
