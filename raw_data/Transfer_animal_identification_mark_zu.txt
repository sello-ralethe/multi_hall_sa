Title: Isicelo sokudlulisa yophawu lokumaka imfuyo

Language: zu
Date scraped: 2025-03-05 18:04:26

==================================================

Uphawu lokubekisa isilwane lungadluliselwa kumnikazi oyedwa kuya komunye umnikazi wemfuyo. Ukuze kwenzeke lokhu, Umnikazi omusha kufanele abhalisele ukudluliselwa kophawu lokubekisa izilwane emnyangweni wezemvelo, amahlathi kanye nezezinhlanzi.

Uma umnikazi wophawu esendlulile emhlabeni, Uphawu lokubekisa lungadluliselwa uma omele ezomthetho ekunikeza imvumo yophawu noma uma omele ezomthetho engekho, umnikazi omusha kufanele alethe isitifiketi esikhombisa ukuthi umnikazi wasekuqaleni usedlulile emhlabeni kanye” nencwadi emnikeza igunya” etholaka enkantolo ephezulu echazayo ukuthi ungumnikazi wezezilwane ngokusemthethweni.

Kungathatha isikhathi esilinganiselwa kumaviki amabili umsebenzi wokudlulisela uphothulwe.
