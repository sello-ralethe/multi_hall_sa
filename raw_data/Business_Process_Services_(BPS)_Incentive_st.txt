Title: Ditshebeletso tsa Tsamaiso ya Kgwebo
Source URL: https://www.gov.za/st/services/services-organisations/business-incentives/ditshebeletso-tsa-tsamaiso-ya-kgwebo
Language: st
Date scraped: 2025-03-05 17:19:18

==================================================

Mmuso wa Aforika Borwa o kentse tshebetsong lenaneo la ditsiane la Tsela ya ho Batla Ditshebeletso tsa Kgwebo Kantle le Nehelano ya Mesebetsi ka Phupu 2007. Mahareng a Phupu le Hlakubele 2010, ditsiane di thehile bonyane mesebetsi e 6 000 e metjha mme ka ho totobala ya hohela R303 million ka matsete. Ditshebeletso tsa Tsamaiso ya Kgwebo di ikemiseditse ho hohela matsete le ho theha mesebetsi ka hare ho Aforika Borwa ka ho aba ditshebeletso tsa kgwebo kantle.

Thola lesedi le fetang lena mabapi le lenaneo lena ho tswa hoLefapha la Kgwebisano le Indaseteri.
