Inkcazelo

ILayisensi yoSasazo lwaBahlali (Community Broadcasting Licence) ifezekisa iimfuno zosasazo zabahlali kwithuba lexesha eliphelela kwiminyaka emine. Iyavuseleleka, kodwa kufuneka wenze isicelo sokuvuselela ilayisensi kungelothuba elingaphantsi kweenyanga ezintandathu, kwaye zingadlulanga iinyanga ezintathu phambi komhla wokuphelelwa kwale unayo ilayisensi. Ivuseleleka kuphela xangaba umntu onelayisensi uhambisene nemiqathango exelwe ngugunyaziwe weelayisensi. Ilayisensi inikwa abahlali abaziseka njengenkampani yeCandelo 21, iQumrhu leNtembeko, iqumrhu lombutho wokuzithandela okanye nawuphina umbutho ovunyiweyo ngokwasemthethweni ongajonganga kwenza nzuzo. Iinjongo zeLayisensi yoSasazo lwaBahlali yeminyaka emine kukufezekisa ezi zinto zilandelayo:

ILayisensi yoSasazo lwaBahlali yeminyaka emine inganikwa kula macandelo alandelayo:

Le layisensi isebenziseka iminyaka emine.

ISebe lezoNxibelelwano lixhasa abantu abaneelayisensi zeminyaka emine ngobuxhakaxhaka bokusasaza nobokuvelisa, xangaba bayazifezekisa izinto eziyimfuneko kwiSebe.

Inkqubo emayilandelwe

I-ICASA iyakukhupha isimemo sezicelo zenkonzo yabahlali yosasazo lwezandi kwaye inike amazinga okushukuma kwamaza (frequencies) kwimimandla eyahlukeneyo. Njengomenzi-sicelo welayisensi, kufanele wenze ezi zinto zilandelayo:

Izicelo ezingagcwaliswanga ngokupheleleyo ziyakuphindiselwa kumenzi-sicelo kwaye akuyi kwamkelwa xanduva lonyibelo okanye ukonakalelwa okwenzekileyo.

Ixesha elithatyathwayo kunye nomgangatho ofikelelwayo

Isicelo singathabatha iinyanga ezi 3 ukuya kwezi 4.
