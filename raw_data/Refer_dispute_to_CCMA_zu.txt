Title: CCMA ukuze kube nokubuyisana noma ukubuyisana-ukuthathwa kwesinqumo sokulamula
Source URL: https://www.gov.za/zu/services/services-residents/world-work/employee-rights/ccma-ukuze-kube-nokubuyisana-noma
Language: zu
Date scraped: 2025-03-05 18:28:29

==================================================

Uma ungumsebenzi oxabene nomqashi wakho, noma umqashi oxabene nomsebenzi, ngezindaba ezinjengalezi:

Ungacela i-CCMA ukuba yenze ukubuyisana noma ukwahlulela engxabanweni yakho. Inyunyana noma inhlangano yomqashi ingase iqalise lesi sinyathelo. Awudingi imvume yabanye abathintekayo ngaphambi kokuba uthathe udaba uluyise kwa-CCMA.

Lezi zingxabano ezilandelayo ngeke ziyiswe kwa-CCMA:

Thola ulwazi oluthe xaxa mayelananokuyisa ingxabano kwa-CCMAnoma thintana nabakwa-CCMA.
