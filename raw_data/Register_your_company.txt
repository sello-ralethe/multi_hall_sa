==================================================

You canregister a company as a non-profit company or profit companywith the Companies and Intellectual Property Commission (CIPC). A company is a legal person which has capacity and powers to act on its own. The law sees a company in the same light as a natural person.

Profit companies may be incorporated under different types of companies under the Companies Act.

Find out more about registering a company from theCompanies and Intellectual Property Commission(CIPC) or contact CIPC on 086 100 2472.
