Title: Inkonzo zeposi ezilawulwa ngurhulumente

==================================================

Iinkonzo zeposi ezilawulwa ngurhulumente ziquka iileta, amakhadi anemiyalezo aposwayo, izinto ezishicilelweyo, iipasile ezincinci kunye nezinye izinto ezihanjiswa ngeposi.

Nceda uqaphele: Ngoku,yiPosi yoMzantsi Afrikayodwa enelayisenisi yokwenza oku. Akukho mntu unemvume yokuhambisa iinkonzo zeposi ezilawulwa ngurhulumente engenalo ilayisenisi yokwenza oko.

Ukukhutshwa kwelayisenisi yokuhambisa iinkonzo zeposi ezilawulwa ngurhulumente kuza kwenzeka kuphela emva kokuba uMphathiswa epapashe isimemo soko.
