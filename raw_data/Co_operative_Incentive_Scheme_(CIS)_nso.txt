Title: Sekema sa Dikabo tša Dikgwebopotlana
Source URL: https://www.gov.za/nso/services/services-organisations/business-incentives/sekema-sa-dikabo-t%C5%A1a-dikgwebopotlana
Language: nso
Date scraped: 2025-03-05 17:21:29

==================================================

CIS kethušo ya tšhelete ya seatleng papetšong ya 90:10 ya dikgwebopotlana tša mathomo tše di ngwadišitšwego. Kgwebopotlana ya mathomo e na le maloko a mahlano goba go feta aoo mongwe le o mongwe wa ona go ya ka histori a bego a hlokišwa menyetla (HID).

Maikemišetšo ke go kaonafatšakgonagalole phadišano ya dikgwebo tša dikgwebopotlanaka go fokotša ditshenyegelo tša tšona tša go dira kgwebo ka Dikabo tšeo di thekgago Matlafatšo ya Bathobaso Ekonoming ya Kakaretšo.

Hwetša tshedimošo ka botlalo ka lenaneo le go tšwa goDepartment of Small Business Development.
