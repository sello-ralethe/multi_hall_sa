Title: Ho ngodisa mokgatlo wa basebetsi
Source URL: https://www.gov.za/st/services/trade-unions/kopo-ya-ho-ngodisa-yunione-ya-basebetsi-kapa-ya-boramesebetsi-lefapheng-la
Language: st
Date scraped: 2025-03-05 18:30:28

==================================================

Ho ka ngodisa mokgatlo wa basebetsi, o ka kenya kopo ho Mookamedi wa tsa Mesebetsi le Kgiro, Lefapheng la Kgiro le Mesebetsi.

Mokgatlo wa basebetsi ke mokgatlo o lwanelang le hona ho sireletsa ditokelo tsa ditho tsa ona mabapi le ditaba tse kang dipuisano tsa meputso le mekgwa le maemo a tshebetso kgahlano le bahiri. Ha se setlamo hore mokgatlo wa basebetsi o ingodise le Lefapha la Mesebetsi le Kgiro, empa boingodiso bo fana ka menyetla e itseng.

Romela kopo ya hao (Registration as a Trade Union, LRA 6.1) ho The Registrar of Labour Relations, Department of Employment and Labour, Private Bag X117, Pretoria, 0001.

Bakeng la lesedi le eketsehileng ikopanye leLefapha la Kgiro le Mesebetsi.
