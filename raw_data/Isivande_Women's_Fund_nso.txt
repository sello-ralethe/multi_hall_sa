Title: Sekhwama sa Basadi sa Isivande (IWF)
Source URL: https://www.gov.za/nso/services/services-organisations/business-incentives/sekhwama-sa-basadi-sa-isivande-iwf
Language: nso
Date scraped: 2025-03-05 17:23:30

==================================================

IWF ke sekhwama sa go ikgetholla seo maikemišetšo a sona e lego go akgofiša matlafatšo ya ekonomi ya basadi ka go aba matlotlo a go araba, go šomišega le go fihlelelega kudu go feta ka moo go lego ka se sebaka. IWF e lebanya dikgwebo tše di ngwadišitšwego semmušo, e le dikgwebo tša go laolwa le/goba 60% ya tšona e le basadi tšeo di bilego gona ebile di šomile mengwaga ye mebedi goba go feta ka kadimo ya mašeleng ya gare ga R30 000 go fihla go R2 milione.

Hwetša tshedimošo ka botlalo ka lenaneo le go tšwa goKgoro ya Bogwebi le Intaseteri.
