Title: Ngodisa pheko ya diruwa
Source URL: https://www.gov.za/st/services/fertilizers-farm-feeds-agricultural-remedies/ngodisa-pheko-ya-diruwa
Language: st
Date scraped: 2025-03-05 18:22:33

==================================================

Ho tsamaisa, ho hlahisa, ho etsa kapa ho rekisa pheko ya diruwa Aforika Borwa, o tlameha ho ngodisa le Mongodisi wa Molao 36 wa 1947.

Pheko tsa diruwa di sebedisetswa diphoofolo tsa lapeng, leruo, dinonyana, ditlhapi kapa diphoofolo tse hlaha bakeng sa:

Khampani e sebelletsang mose e ka se kgone ho etsa kopo ya ngodiso Aforika Borwa. O ka etsa kopo feela ha khampani ya hao e ngodisitswe Aforika Borwa kapa o le motho wa molao ya ngodiisitsweng Aforika Borwa.O tlameha ho ba le ditlhoko tse latelang:

Ha o batla ho ngodisa Pheko ya Diruwa e seng e le teng/e ngodisitsweng lebitsong la khampani e nngwe kapa motho e mong, o tlameha ho fana ka lengolo la tumello ho tswa ho motho eo e ngodisitsweng ka ena pele o ngodiswa.

Selemo le selemo ofisi ya Mongodisi e romela mangolo a ntjhafatso le mabapi le diforomo tsa dikopo ho bohle ba ngodisitsweng.

Ditefiso tsa 2024/25
