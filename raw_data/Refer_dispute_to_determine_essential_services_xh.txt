Umqashi anga faka isicelo kwikomiti yeenkonzo ezingundoqo(Essential Services Committee (ESC)ukuze afumanise ukba ishishini liyinkonzo engundoqo.

Inkonzo engundoqo yinkonzo apho ukuba ithe yaphazamiseka, ingayingozi kwimpilo okanye impilo yabantu  umzekelo isibhedlele nenkonza zamapolisa.

Ngexesha loqhankqalazo, noba ngubani obandakanyekayo ngokuphikisana kokulandelayo angayibhekisa kwikomiti yenkonzo ezingundoqo:

Gcwalisa uxwebhuLRA4.2uthumele impikiswano ukuze icaciswe uze uyithumele kwikomishoni yothethwano, ukubonisana nokuthatha izigqibo (CCMA).

Fumana ngokupheleleyo malunganomsebenzi wekomiti yenkonzo ezingundoqookanye qhagamshela i-CCMA.
