Date scraped: 2025-03-05 17:37:05

==================================================

Ukuthumela ezweni langaphandle intshe kanye manaqanda awo, kufanele ufakele isicelo sezothutho lwanga phandle kumnyango wokubhalisela.

Ukuthuthukiswa kwezilwane  zingakapheli izinsuku ezamashumi amathathu ngaphambi kokuthuthela ngaphandle.

Emva kokufakela isicelo, uzothola Isigunyazo saseningizimu afrika kuphini lwezintshe.

Uma kukhona umkhuhlane wezinyoni nezinye izifo, intshe ngeke ithunyelwe kwilizwe langaphandle ungakapheli lowo mkhuhlane. Kumele ube ngumuntu.

Isicelo singathatha izinsuku ezingafinyelela kumashumi amathathu.
