Title: Bhalisa umkhandlu wokuxoxisana

Language: zu
Date scraped: 2025-03-05 18:29:47

==================================================

Izinyunyana zabasebenzi nezinhlangano zabaqashi zingafaka izicelo zokusungula imikhandlu yokuxoxisana.

Imikhandlu yokuxoxisana ibhekele izivumelwano ngezimo zokusebenza, ixazulula izingxabano zabasebenzi, isungule izikimu ezahlukahlukene iphinde iphawule ngezinqubomgomo nemithetho yezabasebenzi.

Thumela isicelo sakho ukuze ubhalise umkhandlu wokuxoxisanaÂ (Application for Registration of Bargaining Council, Form LRA3.3) ku-Registrar of Labour Relations, Department of Employment and Labour, Private Bag X117, Pretoria, 0001.

Noma ngumuphi umkhandlu wokuxoxisana ungakhetha ukuhlangana noma ukuhlanganiswa nomunye noma neminye imikhandlu yokuxoxisana. Emva kokubhalisa umkhandlu omusha, umabhalane ube esecisha ukubhaliswa okwehlukene kwemikhandlu yokuxoxisana ngamunye.

Thumela isicelo sokuhlanganiswa neminye imikhandlu yokuxoxisana (Application for Registration of Amalgamated Bargaining Council, LRA3.11) ku-Registrar of Labour Relations, Department of Employment and Labour, Private Bag X117, Pretoria, 0001.
