Title: Ukubhalisela indawo njengowenza umanyolo kanye ne nembewu (AI centre)
Source URL: https://www.gov.za/zu/services/services-organisations/permits-licences-and-rights/animal-improvement/ukubhalisela-indawo
Language: zu
Date scraped: 2025-03-05 18:03:12

==================================================

Ukulinganisela, ukupakisha, ukufaka amalebuli, ukugcina kanye nokudayisa imvelaphi yezilwane, kufanele ubhalise indawo osebenzela kuyo njenge AI centre.

Indawo yakho kufanele ibe nalokhu okulandelayo:

planLokhu okungenhla kufanele kubekhona epulanini lakho lendawo.

Kumele ube nepulani You gceke elikhombisa ubukhulu kanye nencazelo yalokhu okulandelayo:

Isilwane esigcinwe emagcekeni akho akufanele sihlangane nezinye izilwane ngaphandle kwengceke. Kufanele ubiyeke igceke.

Izilwane ezingciwe e quarantine akufanele zihlangane nezinye izilwane egcekeni. Kufanele uhlukanise izitebuli ezisetshenziswa ukugcina, ukuqoqa nokuhlola imvelaphi  yohlobo lwesilwane esithile ezindaweni ze quarantine. Qinisekisa ukuthi udoti awuyi kwamanye ama quarantine s noma kwezinye izitebuli.

Abantu abasebenza ngezingulube kuma quarantine akumele bahlangane nabanye abasebenzi abasebenza kwezinye izindawo egcekeni. There must be enough space in the premises for the animals to exercise.

Kufanele ubenamagumbi ahlukene ukwenza lokhu okulandelayo:

Abantu abaqoqa imikhiqizo kufanele bathole imvumo yokungena emagunjini  abhalwe ngenhla. Gcina amagumbi okuqoqela, okubala, ukupakisha, ukwenza amalebuli nokugcina imikhiqizo ihlanzekile ngaso sonke isikhathi.

Phansi, izindonga, usilingi la imikhiqizo yemvelo ehlala khona kufanele lihlanzeke ngaso sonke isikhathi futhi lipheephile njalo.

Akubekwe sikhathi.
