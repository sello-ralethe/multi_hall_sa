Title: Go dira kgopelo ya tokelo ya go epa diepša
Source URL: https://www.gov.za/nso/services/services-organisations/permits-licences-and-rights/mining-and-water/go-dira-kgopelo-ya
Language: nso
Date scraped: 2025-03-05 18:06:42

==================================================

Tokelo ya go epa diepša e fiwa ke Mmušo ka tšhomišano le Kgoro ya dimenerale le Enetši yeo e go fago maatla a go epa dimenerale mo lefelong le le itšego.

Tokelo ya go epa e ka se šome lebaka la mengwaga ye 30.

Tokelo ya go epa e fiwa ge e le gore:

Ikopanye le Lefapha la Mehlodi ya Dirashwa bakeng sa ditjeho.

Dikgopelo ka moka di swanetšwe go dirwa kainthanete.
