==================================================

Ukugqugquzelwa kwezemikhiqizo kuyinxenye lohlelo lwezimpahla zokugqoka kanye nezindwangu. Kusuka ekuqaleni kwezinhlelo ezigqugquzelwa Umnyango wezohwebo esigabeni esithile sezimpahla zokugqoka, izindwangu, izicathulo, izikhumba kanye namabhizinisi ezikhumba.

Imithetho yokugqugquzelwa kwemikhiqizo isiza ukuba izinkampani zethule amabhizinisi Ekomidini elibhekele ukwenzancono amabhizinisi. Usizo olubhekele uhlelo lokucubungula nalo luyatholakala.

Ungasebenzisa usizo lokuthuthukisa noma lokwenza inzuzo, noma izinhlelo zombili.

Imininingwano iyatholakala kumnyango wezohwebo.
