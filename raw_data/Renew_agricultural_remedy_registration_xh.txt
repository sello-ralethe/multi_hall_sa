Title: Isicelo sokuhlaziya okanye ukucima ubhaliso lweyeza lezolimo

Language: xh
Date scraped: 2025-03-05 18:27:08

==================================================

Ufanele ufake isicelo sokuvuselela okanye ukusula ubhaliso lwechiza lakho lezolimo rhoqo emva kweminyaka emithathu. Isicelo sakho kufanele sifakwe kumabhalane womthetho 36 wonyaka ka 1947 kwisSebe lezolimo ,Amahlathi Nezokuloba phambi komhla wama 30 kuMatshi wonyaka lowo ubhaliso luphelelwa ngawo.

ukuba elichiza ufuna ukulivuselela sele libhaliswe ngomnye umntu kufanele ufumane imvume kulowo mntu phambi kokuba ulivuselele.

Faka amachiza kwisingxobo esicicekiley nesivalekileyo. Awuvumelekanga ukuba uphinde usisebenzise isingxbobo emva kokuba usisebenzisile. Apho elichiza lingamanzi, qinisekisa ukuthi isingxobo asichithi ichiza xa ulikhupha. Isingxobo sakho kufuneka singa chasane nommiselo kazwelonke wase Mzantsi Afrika 10229.

Iofisi  kaMbhexeshi ithumela iincwadi  zokuvuselela namaxwebu  esicelo kubo bonke abantu ababhalisileyo ngapahambi kokuphela kweminyaka emithathu emiselweyo.

Isicelo sizakujongwa kuphela xakufunyenwe zonke inkcukacha malunga noku vuselelwa kobhaliso, kuquka uxwebhu A eliphindwe kathathu, uxwebu B noxwebhu C oluphindwe kabini  kunye nenhlawulo emiselweyo.

Imali ehlawulwayo yowama-2024/25

I-ofisi kambhexhesi ithumela iincwadi sokuvuselela nama xwebhu kubo bonke ababhalisileyo  phambi kokuba kuphele iminyaka emithathu.
