Title: Dira kgopelo ya naga ya go swara dinare polaseng ya gago
Source URL: https://www.gov.za/nso/services/agricultural-business-management/dira-kgopelo-ya-naga-ya-go-swara-dinare-polaseng-ya-gago
Language: nso
Date scraped: 2025-03-05 18:27:51

==================================================

Ge o nyaka go swara dinare,  o swanetše go ngwadiša naga le Kgoro ya Temo, Dithokgwa le Boreahlapi.O swanetše go hwetša pele tumelelo go tšwa Balaodi ba maleba ba profense ba tša pabalelo ya tlhago.

Nako ye e beilwego ya tumelelo ya kgopelo ka Balaodi ba Ngaka ya Diphoofolo ya Mmušo ya Profense e fapana go tloga go profense ye nngwe go ya go ye nngwe.Ge kgopelo e amogetšwe  Lefapeng la Maphelo la Diphoofolo, tshepedišo ya ngwadišo e ka tšea tekano ya matšatši a mošomo a lesome.

Ditshenyagelo tša tirelo di tla fapana, go ya profense yeo tirelo e diragakago go yona.
