Title: Isicelo sokuchibiyela noma sokukhansela isitifikedi sokubhalisela ikhaya labadala
Source URL: https://www.gov.za/zu/services/services-organisations/change-registration/isicelo-sokuchibiyela-noma-sokukhansela
Language: zu
Date scraped: 2025-03-05 17:13:58

==================================================

Ingabe ufuna ukuvala isikhungo sokugcina abantu asebekhulile bese ucisha nesitifiketi sakho? Kufanele wazise umnyango wezenhlalakahle kanye nabantu abadala abakhona kuso ezinsukwini ezingu 90 uthathe isinqumo sokuvala.

Isikhungo leso kufanele siqinisekise ukuthi abantu abadala bathola usizo lokwelulekwa ngezengqondo ngaphambi kokuba sivalwe isikhungo.

Kufanele utholele abantu abadala esinye isikhungo sokubagcina esibhaliswe ngokusemthethweni bese ubuyisela isitifiketi sakho ehovisi eliseduze nawe lezenhlalakahle.

Akunamlinganiso obekiwengezinga.

Kumahala.
