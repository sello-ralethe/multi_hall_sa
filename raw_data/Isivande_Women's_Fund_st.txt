IWF ke letlwele le ka thoko le ikemiseditseng ho potlakisa matlafatso ya moruo wa basadi ka ho nehelana ka ditjhelete tse lekaneng, tse sebedisehang, tse fanang ka boikarabelo mme di fumaneha hanghang.Â  Letlwele lena le tobane le dikgwebo tse ngodisitsweng, tse basadi ba etsang 60% botsamaising ba dikgwebo tseo mme di thehilwe kapa di sebeditse dilemo tse pedi kapa ho feta, ka louno ya ho tloha ho R30 000 ho isa ho R2 million.
