==================================================

Ingxowa mali yamanina Isivande (IWF) yingxowa mali ekhethekileyo enjogo yayo ikukukhawulezisa amadla amanina kwezoqoqosho ngokubanika imali efikelelkayo, esebenzisekayo, nesabela kwizidinga zabo ngokukhawuleza kunalendlela okwenzeka ngayo. IIWF ijoliswe kumakhosikazi abhaliswe ngokusemthethweni, amashishini anephesnti ezingamashumi asithandathu alawulwa ngoomama abeokoesebnza kwiminyaka emibini okanye ngaphezulungemali mboleko esuka ku R30 000 ukuya ku R2 miliyoni.

Fumana ngokubanzi ngale nkqubokwisebe lezorwebo nophuhliso (iDTI).
