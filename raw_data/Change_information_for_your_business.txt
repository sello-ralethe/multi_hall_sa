Title: Change information for your business
Source URL: https://www.gov.za/services/services-organisations/change-registration/change-information-your-business
Language: en
Date scraped: 2025-03-05 17:11:40

==================================================

When you make any change to a company, co-operative or close corporation (CC), such as appointing a new accounting officer, changing the postal address, altering share percentages or even adding or cancelling a member, you must amend the details at the Companies and Intellectual Property Commission (CIPC).

CIPC requires you to keep details of your registered business updated at all times.

Find out more about changing information for a company, co-operative or close corporation from theCompanies and Intellectual Property Commission (CIPC)or contact CIPC on 086 100 2472.
