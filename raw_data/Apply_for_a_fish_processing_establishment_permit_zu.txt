Title: Imvume Yokuprosesa Izinhlanzi

==================================================

Uma ukuhlanganyela ngohlelo lokwenziwa kwezinhlanzi, Kumele ube nemvumo yohlelo lokwenziwa kwezinhlanzi FPE permit. Ungabhalisela lemvumo uma uthole isimemo kwi Gazette ka Hulumeni. Nesikhungo sezolimo, Amahlathi nezezinhlanzi ziyasamukela lesisicelo.

I FPE kuba imoto, isikebhe noma indawo la ezinye zezinto zikhiqizwa kusetshenziswa izinhlanzi. Kungakhiqizwa nganoma iluphi uhlobo, ukusika, ukucuba ukuhlukanisa. Loluhlelo lubhekelene futhi nanendawo lapho izinhlanzi zi gqunywa khona, noma zithakwakhona ngenhloso yokwenza ibhizinisi kuleli nangaphandle.

Imiphumela itholakala emva kosuku lokugcina lokufaka izicelo. Isikhungo sezolimo, amahlathi kanye nezezinhlanzi isona esinekezela ngemiphumela. Uma uyitholile imvumo yohlelo lokwenziwa kwezinhlanzi kufanele uthole imvumo ekugunyaza ukuba usebenzise imvumo yakho.

Ungqongqoshe uzogunyaza imvumo ngaphandle kwezizathu ezithile. Uma uyitholile imvumo yohlelo lokwenziwa kwezinhlanzi kufanele uthole imvumo ekugunyaza ukuba usebenzise imvumo yakho.

Qaphela: Kufanele uvuselele imvumo yakho njalo ngonyaka. Uma ungalandeli imithetho yemvumo noma iphemithi kungadala ukuba imvumo yakho imiswe noma isulwe emabhukwini.
