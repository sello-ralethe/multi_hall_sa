Title: Sekema sa Ditsiane sa Tshebedisano Mmoho
Source URL: https://www.gov.za/st/services/services-organisations/business-incentives/sekema-sa-ditsiane-sa-tshebedisano-mmoho
Language: st
Date scraped: 2025-03-05 17:21:33

==================================================

CIS mmoho ke kerante ya kontane e lekanang hantle ya 90:10 bakeng sa dikgwebo tse ngodisitsweng tsa motheo tse sebedisanang mmoho. Kgwebo ya motheo e sebedisanang mmoho e na le maloko a mahlano kapa ho feta mme e le batho ba neng ba hloka menyetla.

Sepheo sa sekema sena ke ho ntlafatsa maemo le bokgoni ba dikgwebo tsena tse sebedisanang mmoho ka ho nyehlisa ditjeo tsa tsona tsa ho tsamaisa kgwebo ka ditsiane tse tshehetsang Matlafatso ya Moruo o Batsi Bathong ba Batsho.

Thola lesedi le fetang lena mabapi le lenaneo lena ho tswa hoDepartment of Small Business Development.
