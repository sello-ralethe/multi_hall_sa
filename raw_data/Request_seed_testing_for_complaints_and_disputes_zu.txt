Title: Ukuhlola imbewu lapho kunesikhalazo kanye nengxabano

Language: zu
Date scraped: 2025-03-05 17:52:43

==================================================

Uma ufuna ukwenza ucwaningo nezinhlamvu zezitshalo ezingacoekile noma ezingekho ezingeni, xhumana no Mqondisi wezokuhlolwa emnyangweni wezolimo, amahlathi kanye nezezinhlanzi ukuze izinhlamvu zihlolwe.

Umqondisi wezokuhlolwa uzocela osebenzela igumbi locwaningo ukuze kwenziwe uphenyo ngesikhalazo.

Nanoma ngubani, ithimba, inkampani noma ngumlimi angafaka isikhalo.

Imininingwano yasebhangi:

Ibhange: Standard BankIgama legatsha: ArcadiaInombolo yegatsha: 01-08-45Inombolo yasebhange: 011 244887Igama lesikhwama : NDA-Seed Testing Services

Ungakhokha ngesimanje noma ezindaweni okukhokhwa kuzo.

Khumbula ukufaka inombolo yesitatimende ukuze kubonakale ukuthi nguwena okhokhile.

Khokha inani lokuhlolwa kwezinhlamvu zezitshalo ku OSTL ngesikhahlamezi noma nge meyili ukuze kudedelwe imiphumela.

Qaphela: Uma okhalazayo isikhalazo sakhe singamukelekile, Ucubungulo lwenziwa emva kokuba kukhokhiwe.

I OSTL ibhekelele kakhulu ukuhlolwa kwezinhlamvu okukhalwazwa ngazo. Usisindo sesikhalazo. Isisindo sesifaniso, uhlobo lohlamvu kanye nohlobo lokuhlolwa okufanele lwenziwe. Ayikho imininingwano eyokhishwa. Izimpendulo zizotholakala emnyangweni uqobo (Bheka izinombolo ezibhalilwe).

Imali ekhokhwayo yowezi-2024/25

Umqondisi obhekele ezokuhlolwa uzodlulisela amafomu uma usuxhumane naye.
