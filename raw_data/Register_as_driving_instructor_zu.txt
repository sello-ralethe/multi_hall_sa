Title: Ukubhalisa njengomqeqeshi ofundisa ukushayela

Language: zu
Date scraped: 2025-03-05 17:44:35

==================================================

Ukuze ube umqeqeshi ofundisa ukushayela oqashelwayo eNingizimu Afrika, kumele ubhalise emnyangweni wezokuthutha wesifundazwe sakini. Abaqeqeshi abafundisa ukushayela kudingeka babe nesitifiketi esisho ngokuqondile izinga noma isigaba semoto abaqeqeshelwe ukufundisa abashayeli bayo.Amazinga abaqeqeshi abafundisa ukushayela ahamba kanje:

Uyobhekana kuphela nekilasi lezimoto onezincwadi zalo. Isitifiketi somqeqeshi sisebenza unyaka owodwa kuphela.

Imvume ingase ithathe amaviki ayisithupha kuya kwayisishiyagalombili.

Thintana nehhovisi lamalayisense langakini ukuze uthole ukuthi kubiza malini.

La mafomu ayatholakala kunoma yiyiphi i-DLTC noma kwisizindalwazi se-eNatis.

Contact the licensing office at your local municipality
