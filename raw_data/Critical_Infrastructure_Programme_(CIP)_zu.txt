Title: Uhlelo olubhekele isakhiwo esisemqoka
Source URL: https://www.gov.za/zu/services/services-organisations/business-incentives/uhlelo-olubhekele-isakhiwo-esisemqoka
Language: zu
Date scraped: 2025-03-05 17:21:53

==================================================

Uhlelo olubhekele isakiwo esisemqoka isiza ukwenzancono izakhiwo esisemqoka Eningizimu Afrika.

Lesisikhwama sisiza izindleko ezifanele kusukela emaphesentini ayishumu kuya kumaphesenti angamashumi amathathu kuyela ezindlekweni zonke zokuthuthukisa isakhiwo.

Imininingwano iyatholakala kumnyango wezohwebo.
