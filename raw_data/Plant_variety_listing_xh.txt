Title: Isicelo sokuqonda udidi lwesityalo

Language: xh

==================================================

Ukuthengisa imbewu okanye isetyana elisikwe (izakhi zokusasaza) kwisityalo, kufuneka kuqala usibhalise kuluhlu kweendidi.Phambi kokuba uthengise izakhi zokusasaza zeendidi ezithile zezityalo eMzantsi Afrika, kufuneka ufake isicelo kwiGosa loBhaliso lokuPhucula iziTyalo ukuze isityalo sakho sodidi olutsha samkelwe.

Ungacela iSebe lezoLimo, amaHlathi nokuLoba liquke udidi lwesityalo esitsha kuludwe lweendidi zezityalo ngokwemiqathango yoMthetho wokuPhuculwa kweziTyalo,1976 (ISicwangciso 2 soMthetho).

ISebe lezoLimo, amaHlathi nokuLoba lugcina uludwe lwezona ndidi zezityalo neziqhamo zibaluleke kakhulu. Eyona meko imisiweyo kukuba isityalo eso sibaluleke kangakanani  kuqoqosho lwaseMzantsi Afrika.Uhlobo lwesityalo lungafakwa kuludwe (lungamkelwa) ukuba ezi mfuneko zilandelayo kungahlangatyezwana nazo:

Ukuba ukhuselo ngokubhekiselele kumalungelo omntu okhulisa aphucule izityalo luyafunwa nalo, abafaka izicelo bayacetyiswa ukuba bafake izicelo zabo ngexesha elinye neGosa loBhaliso lwamaLungelo abaKhulisa baPhucule iziTyalo ukonga ixesha nemali.

Inkqubo yokubhaliswa kohlobo lwesityalo kuluhlu iya kuthatha ixesha lokulima elinye ukuya kumaxesha okulima amabini.
