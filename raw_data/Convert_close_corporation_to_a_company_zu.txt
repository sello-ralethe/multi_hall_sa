Uma wenza noma ngeziphi izinguquko kwi-close corporation(i-CC), njengokuqoka umphathi omusha, ukushintsha ikheli, izinguquko kumaphesenti amasheya ngisho noma ukufaka noma ukusula ilungu, kufanele uchibiyele imininingwane ye-CC kwiKhomishana yokuBhalisa iziNkampani neMpahla yokuZakhela (i-CIPC).

I-CIPC idinga ukuthi ugcine imininingwane yakho isesimeni ngaso sonke isikhathi.
