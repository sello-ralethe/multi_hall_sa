==================================================

Ukudayisa imbewu ephuma ezitshalweni, Kufanele ubhalisele ezitshalo ezahlukene.

Ngaphambi kokuba udayise izitshalo ezahlukene, kufanele ubhalisele ukwenza kancono izitshalo ukuze uhlobo oluhlukene lezitshalo zakho luzokwaziwa.

Ungacela u Mnyango wezolimo, amahlathi kanye nezezinhlanzi (DAFF) bafake isitshalo esisha ohlelweni lwezitshalo ezahlukene kuPlant Improvement Act, 1976 (Table 2 of the Act)

I DAFF igcina uhla lwezitshalo,  nezithelo.

Abakubhekelayo ukubaluleka kwesitshalo kuleli lase Ningizimu Afrika.

Uma kufanele ubenemvumo yokwenza imbewu, aba Bhalisi bayalulekwa ukuba balethe izicelo kanye nencwadi kanye kanye kuze bonge isikhathi nemali.

Kungathatha izigaba ezimbili esikhathi sonyaka .
