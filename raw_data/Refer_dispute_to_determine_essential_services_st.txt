Title: Etsa kopo komiting ya ditshebeletso tsa bohlokwa hore e tle e etse ditekolo hore na tshebetso eo efela e le tshebeletso ya bohlokwa
Source URL: https://www.gov.za/st/services/services-organisations/labour/labour-relations/etsa-kopo-komiting-ya-ditshebeletso-tsa
Language: st
Date scraped: 2025-03-05 18:28:54

==================================================

Mohiri a ka nna a etsa kopo Komiting ya Ditshebeletso tsa Bohlokwa (ESC) ho hlahloba hore na kgwebo eo efela e le tshebeletso ya bohlokwa.

Ka tshebeletso ya bohlokwa ho bolelwa tshebeletso eo, ha ho ka etsahala hore e sitisehe, seo se ka beha maphelo le bophelo ba batho kotsing kapa ho tlisa tshitiso e kgolo.

Nakong ya diseteraeke, mokga ofe kapa ofe o amehang tsekisanong ka ditaba tse latelang e ka nna ya romela taba eo komiting ya ditshebeletso tsa bohlokwa:

Tlatsa foromo ya LRA4.2- E tlhahisang ditsekisano bakeng sa ditshebeletso tsa bohlokwa ho ka lekolwa mme o e romele ho Khomishene ya Poelano le Bonamodi (CCMA).

Thola lesedi le fetang lena mabapi le mosebetsi waKomiti ya Ditshebeletso tsa Bohlokwakapa o buisane leKhomishene ya Poelano le Bonamodi.
