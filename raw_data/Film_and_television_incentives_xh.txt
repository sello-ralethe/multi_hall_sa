Title: Imali yemveliso ka mabona kude nemiboniso bhanya-bhanya
Source URL: https://www.gov.za/xh/services/services-organisations/business-incentives/imali-yemveliso-ka-mabona-kude-nemiboniso
Language: xh
Date scraped: 2025-03-05 17:22:43

==================================================

Urhulumente wase Mzantsi afrika uncedisa ngemali ukukhulisa imveliso kamabona kude kunye ne nemveliso emvakokuthabatha imifanekiso. Uncedo luquka umboniso bhanya-bhanya wakwamanye amazwe, umabonakude nemveliso emva kokuthathatha imifanekiso ukwenza umtsalane kubenzi bemiboniso bhanya-bhanya bamanye amazwe ukuthi basebenzise iindawo zaseMzantsi Afrika nokutho bakwazi ukuwenzela apha wonke umsebenzi wabo. Oku kuzakwenza ukuba umboniso bhanya-bhanya no mabona kude balapha eMzantsi Afrika basebnze nabo injongo ikukunceda abavelisi bemiboniso bhanya-bhanya ukuvelisa imiboniso yalapha ekhaya.

Fumana ngokubanzi ngale nkqubokwisebe lezorwebo nophuhliso (iDTI).
