Title: Ingodise jwalo ka mohlahisi kapa motho ya tlisang makoloi ho tswa kantle
Source URL: https://www.gov.za/st/services/services-organisations/driving-and-transport/ingodise-jwalo-ka-mohlahisi-kapa-motho-ya
Language: st
Date scraped: 2025-03-05 17:45:25

==================================================

Ha o ka batla ho etsa, ho reka dinaheng tsa kantle kapa ho aha dikoloi Aforika Borwa bakeng sa phaello, o tlameha ho qala ka ho ngodisa le lefapha la dipalangwang la porovense.

Hang ha lefapha le amohela kopo ya hao, le tla romela mohlahlobi ho fumana hore na o phethahatsa ho ya ka melawana e amehang. Kgwebo ya hao le yona e tla itshetleha tumellong yaTshebeletso ya Sepolesa sa Aforika Borwa (SAPS).

Boingodiso bo itshetlehile kamohelong ya MEC ya amehang mme setefekeiti sa boingodiso se ntshwa kamohelong e jwalo.

Iteanye le lefapha la dipalangwang la lehae la heno bakeng sa ditjeho.

Kopo le tsebiso mabapi le foromo ya moetsi/moreki wa dinaheng tsa kantle/moahi wa dikoloi (MIB) (Application and notice i.r.o registration of a manufacturer/importer/builder of vehicles [Form MIB])

Contact your local department of transport
