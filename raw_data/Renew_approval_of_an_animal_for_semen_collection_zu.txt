==================================================

Ngaphambi kokuhambisa isilwane osibhalisele ukuqoqa isidoda ukuya kwenye indawo , kufanele uqale ufake isicelo sokuvuselela imvume yokubhaliswa kwaso yokuqoqa isidoda.

Awudingi ukuthola igazi lalesi silwane. Awusidingi futhi isitifiketi sikadokotela wezilwane ngaphandle uma uhambisa isilwane ngenxa yesifo sezilwane esibizwa nge-foot and mouth disease endaweni lapho ugcine khona isilwane. Kodwa, kufanele uthintane noMnyango Wezolimo, Izinguquko Kwezomhlaba Nokuthuthukiswa Kwezindawo Zasemakhaya futhi ubanikeze izizathu zokuhambisa isilwane.

Kuthatha izinsuku ezingamashumi amathathu.
