Title: Isicelo sokunikezela intsimbi yokunombola nokuphawula izilwanyana

Language: xh
Date scraped: 2025-03-05 18:04:22

==================================================

Uphawu lwesilwanyana lungadluliswa ukusuka komnye ukuya komnye umnini wemfuyo. Ukwenze njalo, umnini omtsha kufanele afake isicelo sokudluliselwa kophawu lwesilwanyana kwisebe lwezolimo, Amahlathi nokuloba.

Xakusweleka umnikazi osemthethweni, uphawu linga dluliselwa kuwe ukuba umgcini mafa unika ilungelo lokuthi uphawu ludluliswe okanye ukuba akunamgcini mafa umnini omtsha angaveza isiqinisekiso sokusweleka kunye “nencwadi egunyazayo”  ekhutshwe yinkundla ephakamileyo echaza ukuthi ungumnini osemthethweni wezilwanyana.

Kungathabatha iiveki ezimbini ukudluliselwa kophawu.
