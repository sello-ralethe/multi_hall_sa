Title: Fetola kgwebo e nnyane go khampani
Source URL: https://www.gov.za/nso/services/services-organisations/change-registration/fetola-kgwebo-e-nnyane-go-khampani
Language: nso
Date scraped: 2025-03-05 17:14:54

==================================================

Ge o dira phetogo go koporase ye nnyane (CC), bjalo ka go thwala mohlankedi yo mofsa yo a rwalago maikarabelo, go fetola aterese ya poso, go fetola diperesente tša karolelano goba go tsenya goba go ntšha leloko, o swanetše go fetola dintlha tša CC ka go Komišene ya Dikhamphani le Ditheo tša Boikgopolelo le Boitlhamelo (CIPC).

CIPC e nyaka gore o boloke dintlha tša CC ya gago gore di dule di le mo maemong a maleba ka dinako tšohle.

Hwetša dintlha ka botlalo mabapi le go fetola tshedimošo ya koporase ye nnyane go tšwa goKomišene ya Dikhamphani le Ditheo tša Boikgopolelo le Boitlhamelo (CIPC)goba o leletše CIPC mo go 086 100 2472.
