Title: Ho romela ngangisano ho CCMA bakeng sa poelano kapa poelano le bonamodi
Source URL: https://www.gov.za/st/services/services-residents/world-work/employee-rights/ho-romela-ngangisano-ho-ccma-bakeng-sa
Language: st
Date scraped: 2025-03-05 18:28:37

==================================================

Haeba wena o le mohiruwa ya tsekisanong le mohiri wa hao kapa e ka ba ka lehlakore le leng hodima taba e jwaloka:

O ka kopa Khomishene ya Poelano le Bonamodi (CCMA) ho thusa ka poelano kapa bonamodi tsekong ya lona. Mokgatlo wa basebetsi kapa wa mohiruwa o ka nna wa qala ketsahalo ena. Ha o hloke hore o fumane tumello ya mokgatlo o mong pele o isa taba ena ho CCMA.

Ditseko tsena tse latelang di ke ke tsa romelwa ho CCMA:

Thola lesedi le fetang lena mabapi leho romela tseko ho CCMAkapa o iteanye leCCMA.
