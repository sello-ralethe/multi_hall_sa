Title: Amachiza okuthithela izifo kwizilwanyana
Source URL: https://www.gov.za/xh/services/services-organisations/import/amachiza-okuthithela-izifo-kwizilwanyana
Language: xh
Date scraped: 2025-03-05 17:30:01

==================================================

Ungathenga kumazwe anga phandle ,  wabe okanye uthengise amachiza okuthintela izifo kwizilwanyana kweli lomzantsi Afrika  xa sele ubhalisile ngomthetho wonyaka 1947 womgquba, ukutya kwezilwanyana, amachiza ezolimo namachiza emfuyo (Umthetho 36 wonyaka ka1947).Ukuba ufuna ukuthenga  ichiza lokuthintela izifo elibhalisiweyo ufuna ukulisebenzisa kwizilwanyana, kufanele ufumane iphepha mvume lwezilwanyana elisuka kubalawuli bezempilo yezilwanyana.

Lemvume yokuthenga ngahandle  yeyxeshana kwaye yeyomthwalo omnye kuphela. Kunemigaqo nkqubo yokulawula ukungena kwezilwanyana kweli. Njengoko imithetho yezempilo iguquguquka  qhagamshelana  noMbexeshi wempilo yezilwanyana kwezi nombolo zilandelayo: Tel 012 319 7507/7684/7700/7673/7456, feksela ku 012 329 6892,VetPermits@daff.gov.za) ukuze ufumane inkcukacha zamva.Ukuba ufuna ukufaka ichiza lokuthintela izifo elingabhaliswanga ukuze livavanywe kweli loMzantsi Afrika udinga amaphepha mvume amabini. Elinye iphephamvume lisuka kuMbexeshi womthetho 36 wonyaka ka 19747 kanti elinye lona lisuka kubalawuli bempilo yezilwanyana. Isicelo semvume esuka kuMbaxeshi womthetho 36 ka 1947 kufanele sikhatshwe ngumgaqo nkqubo yokwenza uvavanyo.Abalawuli bempilo yezilwanyana bakhupha imvume yokuthenga phesheya xa sele befumene ikopi yemvume ekhutshwe nguMbexeshi womthetho 36 wonyaka ka 1947. Ichiza lokuthintela izifo elisuka phesheya ngenjongo yovavanyo alithengiswa. Kwimeko engxamisekileyo apho kuqhambuke isifo libe lingekho ichiza elibhalisiweyo, singakho isidingo sokuthi lisetyenziswe ichiza elinga bhaliswanga.

Kuthi ke wena njengomntu ofake elichiza kweli ufake isicelo kwi khansela yabalawuli bamachiza (medicine control council) ngokwesigaba 21 somthetho sokukhululwa nokulawulwa kwamayeza namachiza (umthetho 101 wonyaka ka 1965, as amended ngumthetho 90 wonyaka ka 1997). Udinga imvume yokuthenga phesheya kubalawuli bempilo yezilwanyana ukuze ichiza lingene kweli lizwe. Bakhipha lemvume yokuthenga phessheya kuphela xa ufumene imvume kwi Khansela yabalawuli bamachiza kwaye nawe uveze ubungqina kubo. Phantsi kwale meko ichiza elingeka bhaliswa lingathengiswa okwethutyana kude kugqwitywe ubhaliso.

Qaphela: Akukho sicelo sizakujongwa ngaphandle kobungqina bokuthi kuhlawuliwe. Ubungqina behlawulo bungathunyelwa ngohlobo lwe Feksi kule nombolo: 012 329 8292 /VetPermits@daff.gov.za.

Ukuba nje igosa lonyango lwezilwanyana kwi ofisi ka zwelonke linga samkela isicelo kuthabatha iintsuku ezintathu ukuya kwekwezintlanu ukugqoba isicelo kukhutshwe ne phepha mvume.
