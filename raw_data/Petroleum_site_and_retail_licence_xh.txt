Title: Ukufaka isicelo sesiza nephapha-mvume lentengiso
Source URL: https://www.gov.za/xh/services/petroleum/ukufaka-isicelo-sesiza-nephapha-mvume-lentengiso
Language: xh
Date scraped: 2025-03-05 18:13:59

==================================================

Kufuneka ufake isicelo kwiSebe lezaMandla xa ufuna indawo yokugcina ipetroleum kunye nephepha-mvume lokuthengisa ukuba ufuna ukuthenga okanye ukuthengisa iimveliso zepetroleum kuluntu.

Qaphela: Umthengisi uvumelekile ukuba athenge iimveliso zepetroleum kumvelisi okanye ishishini elithengisa ngesixa elinephepha-mvume kuphela.

Iphepha-mvume liza kuhlala lisemthethweni ukuba nje ishishini lakho lisaqhuba.

Ixesha elimiselweyo

Isigqibo sithathwa zingekapheli iintsuku ezingama-90 ukusuka ngomhla iSebe lifumana ubungqina bokupapashwa kwesaziso sokufaka isicelo.
