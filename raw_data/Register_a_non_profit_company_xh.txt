Title: Bhalisa inkampani eNgenaNzuzo
Source URL: https://www.gov.za/xh/services/register-business-or-organisation/isicelo-sokubhalisa-inkampani-yecandelo-21section-21
Language: xh
Date scraped: 2025-03-05 17:05:24

==================================================

Ukuze umbutho wakho wasekuhlaleni uthathwe njengosemthethweni, kufuneka uwubhalise njengeNkampani eNgenaNzuzo (NPC) kwiKomishoni yeeNkampani noBunini bobuKrelekrele (CIPC).

IiNkampani eziNgenaNzuzo yimibutho esekelwe ukunceda abantu, ikhusele okusingqongileyo okanye ifuna inkxaso kwiphulo elithile. Ezi nkampani ziquka iicawe, imibutho yesisa kunye nemibutho yenkcubeko. Eyona njongo isisiseko yeNkampani eNgenaNzuzo kukunceda uluntu, asikokufumana nzuzo.

Ingeniso kunye nezinto ezizezayo azinakwabiwa phakathi kwababandakanyiweyo, amalungu, abalawuli okanye amagosa enkampani eNgenaNzuzo, ngaphandle kwembuyekezo ebonakalayo ngeenkonzo abazinikezeleyo. Zonke izinto zenkampani eNgenaNzuzo kunye nengeniso kufuneka zisetyenziswe ukuqhubela phambili iinjongo zenkampani ezichaziweyo, njengoko zichaziwe kuYilo lwemvumelwano yokuBandakanywa (MOI).

Ubuncinane bathathu ababandakanyiweyo ekufuneka batyikitye uYilo lwemvumelwano, MOI. Kufuneka kuqeshwe ubuncinane abalawuli abathathu.

Iinkampani ezingenanzuzo kufuneka ngalo lonke ixesha zibe nobuncinane umlawuli omnye (ngaphandle kokuba uYilo lwemvumelwano, MOI, lubonisa inani labalawuli elingaphezulu ubuncinane). Ukuqeshwa komphicothi-zincwadi okanye unobhala akunyanzelekanga kodwa bayazikhethela.

Ungabhalisa iNkampani eNgenaNzuzo njenge:

Igama lenkampani enganganzuzo liya kuphela ngegama elithi “NPC”.

Nceda qaphela: ukuba igama elicetywayo likhatyiwe, inkampani ingabhaliswa ize inombolo yokubhalisa ibe ligama lenkampani xa kubandakanywa, de kufike ixesha lokugcinelwa okanye lokwamkelwa kwegama elifanelekileyo.
