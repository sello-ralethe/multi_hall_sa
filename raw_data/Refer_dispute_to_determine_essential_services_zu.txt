Title: Ukufaka isicelo ekomidini lezinsizakalo ezisemqoka ukuze kutholwe ukuthi insizakalo ethile iyinsizakalo esemqoka
Source URL: https://www.gov.za/zu/services/services-organisations/labour/labour-relations/ukufaka-isicelo-ekomidini-lezinsizakalo
Language: zu
Date scraped: 2025-03-05 18:28:49

==================================================

Umqashi angase afake isicelo kwiKomidi Lezinsizakalo Ezisemqoka (i-ESC) ukuze kutholakale ukuthi ibhizinisi liyinsizakalo esemqoka yini.Insizakalo esemqoka yinsizakalo okungathi uma iphazamiseka, ifake ukuphila noma impilo yabantu engozini, isib. izibhedlela kanye namaphoyisa.

Lapho kunesiteleka, noma yibaphi abathintekayo engxabanweni mayelana nalezi zindaba ezilandelayo bangaziyisa ekomidini lezinsizakalo ezisemqoka:

Gcwalisaifomu i-LRA4.2– Ukudlulisela izingxabano e-CCMA ukuze kutholakale ukuthi izinsizakalo zisemqoka yini bese ulithumela kwa-CCMA).

Thola ulwazi oluthe xaxa mayelananomsebenzi we-Komidi Lezinsizakalo Ezisemqokanoma thintana ne-CCMA.
