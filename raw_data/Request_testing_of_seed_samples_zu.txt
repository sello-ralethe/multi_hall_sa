Ukuba nokuhlolwa ngokusemthethweni kwezinhlamvu ukuthi zitshaleke futhi zehlukanisekaabaCwaningi beMinyango weZemvelo, amahlathi kanye nezasemanzini kufanele bathole zibonelo bazicwaninge.

Izinhlamvu zocwaningo kufanele zivumelane nezigaba zeplant improvement act [pia] uma izinhlamvu zingamelani nomthetho, kazidayiswe noma zicwaningisiswe kabusha.
