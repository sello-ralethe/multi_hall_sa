Title: Tshomiso ya meetse
Source URL: https://www.gov.za/nso/services/services-organisations/permits-licences-and-rights/mining-and-water/tshomiso-ya-meetse
Language: nso
Date scraped: 2025-03-05 18:07:41

==================================================

Badiriši ka moka ba meetse ba swanetše go ingwadiša le Kgoro ya Merero ya Meetse. O swanetše go ingwadišetša tirišo ya meetse ge e le gore ga o hwetše meetse go tšwa go baabi ba ditirelo, ba taolo ya selegae, boto ya meetse, boto ya nošetšo, setlamo sa mmušo sa meetse goba baabi ba meetse ka bontši gomme o dirišetša meetse ao go:

Mo nakong ye e tlago, ge badiriši ba meetse ba swanetše go dira kgopelo ya dilaesense, bao ba sego ba ngwadiša ba tla dira gore menyetla ya bona ya go hwetša laesense ya go diriša meetse e be e mennyane.

Batho goba makala ka moka a swanetše goingwadišetša tirišo ya meetsegoba o ka ikopanya le ye nngwe le e nngwe yaDikantoro tša Dilete go Kgoro ya Merero ya Meetse.
