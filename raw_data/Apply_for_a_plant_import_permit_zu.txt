Title: Ukunakekela nokukhulisa izitshalo ukuze ukwazi ukuwelisa nokuthumela izitshalo
Source URL: https://www.gov.za/zu/services/export-permits-import/phytosanitary-permit
Language: zu
Date scraped: 2025-03-05 17:29:32

==================================================

Ukuze uthumele imbewu kanye nezitshalo kwamanye amazwe omhlaba, kumele ufake isicelo sokuvunyelwa ukunakekela nokukhulisa izitshalo. Thola iphepha mvume egunyazwe ngabezokuvikelwa kwezitshalo ezithunyelwa emazweni angaphandle.Kumele uqonde ukuthi yini ukunakekela nokukhulisa izitshalo, imigomo yokuthumela yalawo mazwe aqondene nezitshalo ozozithumelayo. Uma impahla yakho ivumelana nezimfuno zokuthumela kwakulelozwe, uMnyango wezokuvikelwa kwezitshalo uyobe usukhipha isitifiketi sokunakekela nokukhulisa izitshalo.Izitshalo kumele zithunyelwe kanye ne sitifiketi esingakopishwanga so kunakekela nokukhulisa izitshalo.

It might take about 48 hours to process your application provided there are no additional activities.
