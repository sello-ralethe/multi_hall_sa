Title: Isicelo sokungenisa iimveliso ze-oyile eyi-petroleum
Source URL: https://www.gov.za/xh/services/import/import-or-export-permits-petroleum-products
Language: xh
Date scraped: 2025-03-05 17:33:18

==================================================

Ukuba ufuna ukungenisa okanye ukuthumela iimveliso ze-oli ekrwada eMzantsi Afrika, kufuneka kuqala ufumane igunya elivela kwiSebe leziMbiwa naMandla (DME) lokufumana imvume evela kwiKomishoni yoLawulo lwezoRhwebo lwaMazwe ngaMazwe (ITAC).

Ukungeniswa nokuthunyelwa kweemveliso ze-oli ekrwada kuphantsi kolawulo lokungeniswa/nokuthunyelwa kwempahla kwaye ke ngoko inkampani okanye umntu oceba ukungenisa okanye athumele iimveliso ze-oli ekrwada eMzantsi Afrika kufuneka abe nemvume yokungenisa okanye yokuthumela impahla kumazwe angaphandle.

Ukukhutshwa kwemvume yokungeniswa kunye nokuthunyelwa kweemveliso ze-oli ekrwada kuphantsi kolawulo lwemiqathango yeSebe leziMbiwa naMandla (DME).

Ukuze isicelo sakho sijongwe ngeenjongo zokukunika igunya, kufuneka uqhagamshelane neSebe leziMbiwa naMandla malunga nenkqubo efunekayo.

Iimpahla ezifana ne-oli ekrwada, amafutha ezithuthi, iparafin, ii-oli zokuthambisa, iigesi ze-oli ekrwada, amafutha e-oli ekrwada, ibhitumeni, namafutha eparafin ziphantsi kolawulo lwemiqathango yokungeniswa kunye nokuthunyelwa kwempahla.

Qaphela: Iimvume zokungenisa impahla zisemthethweni kangangeenyanga ezili-12 ize iimvume zokuyithumela zibe semthethweni kangangeenyanga ezintandathu.

Ixesha elithathwayo lokukhupha iimvume zokungenisa impahla evela kumazwe angaphandle ziintsuku ezintathu.

iITAC ayijiki imali yokuhlawulela iinkonzo kodwa uzakuhlawula yempahla abaqokeleli be rhafu (SARS). Irhafu yempahla ibalwa ngomyinge wentengo yempahla leyo (ichaziwe kuluhlu lomthetho werhafu yemveliso yokuthengiswa). Malunga nolwazi malunga nerhafu yempahla yabaqokeleli be rhafu, vndwendwela iwebhu sayithi yabaqokeleli berhafu iSARS .
