Title: Ukubhalisa iziko lemfundo ephakamileyo labucala

Ingaba unomnqweno wokunikela ngemfundo ephakamileyo eMzantsi Afrika? Kufuneka uqale ubhalise iziko njengelemfundo ephakamileyo labucala kwiSebe leMfundo ePhakamileyo noQeqesho (i-DHET) ukuze ube negunya ngokwasemthethweni lokunikela ngeenkqubo zemfundo ephakamileyo.

Amaziko emfundo ephakamileyo abucala anikela ngemigangatho efana neyesiKhokelo-nkqubo seSizwe seziQinisekiso (i-NQF) (Umgangatho wesi-5 ukuya kowe-10) njengeeyunivesithi zikarhulumente okanye iiyunivesithi zobuchwepheshe.

Injongo yobhaliso kukuqinisekisa ukuba:

Iinkqubo zamaziko emfundo abucala zifanele ukubhaliswa e-DHET, zigunyaziswe yiKomiti yemiGangatho yeMfundo ePhakamileyo (i-HEQC) yeBhunga kwiMfundo ePhakamileyo(i-CHE) ngokunjalo zibhaliswe kwi-NQF liQumrhu laseMzantsi Afrika leziQinisekiso(i-Saqa). Iinkqubo zifanele ukwamkeleka esizweni nasehlabathini.

Kwiinyanga ezili-18 obona buncinane zokunqwenela ukuqalisa ukusebenza kweziko lakho ufanele ukufaka isicelo sokubhalisa kumBhalisi wamaZiko eMfundo ePhakamileyo abuCala. Isicelo sokuGuqulwa masingeniswe ngomhla omiselwe ngumBhalisi (uMlawuli-Jikelele)..

Qaphela: Nceda qhagamshelana nomBhalisi malunga nalo naluphi na utshintsho onqwenela ukulenza ngexesha lenkqubo yokufaka isicelo.
