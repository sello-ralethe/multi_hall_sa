Title: Kgopelo ya go fetoša goba go phumula setifikeiti sa boingwadišo sa legae la batšofadi
Source URL: https://www.gov.za/nso/services/services-organisations/change-registration/kgopelo-ya-go-feto%C5%A1a-goba-go-phumula
Language: nso
Date scraped: 2025-03-05 17:14:04

==================================================

Na o nyaka go tswalela lefelo la bodudi bja batšofadi gomme o khansele setifikeiti sag ago? O swanetše go tsebiša Kgoro ya Tlhabollo ya Leago ka maikemišetšo a gago mo nakong ya matšatši a 90 pele o dira bjalo.

O swanetše go kgonthiša gore batšofadi ba ba lego lefelong leo ba a eletšwa pele lefelo leo le tswalelwa.

O swanetše go hweletša batšofadi bao lefelo le lengwe le le ngwadišitšwego moo batšofadi bao ba tlogo dula gona gomme wena o bušetše setifikeiti sag ago Kgorong ya Tlhabollo ya Leago.

Ga go na nako ye e beilwego.

Tirelo ye ga e lefelwe.

Difoko tša kgopelo di hwetšagala ofising ya kgaufsi yaKgoro ya Tlhabollo ya Leago.
