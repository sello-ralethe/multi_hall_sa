Title: Isicelo sobhaliso yomhlaba wokugcina inyathi
Source URL: https://www.gov.za/zu/services/agricultural-business-management/isicelo-sobhaliso-yomhlaba-wokugcina-inyathi
Language: zu
Date scraped: 2025-03-05 18:27:47

==================================================

Uma unesifiso sokugcina izinyathi, kumele uqale ngokubhalisa umhlaba ngaphansi komnyango obhekelela ezolimo namahlathi kanye nabadobi owaziwa ngo Mnyango wezolimo, amahlathi nezezinhlanzi.

Kumele uqale ngokuthola imvume ophikweni lababhekelela ezemvelo kuleso sifundazwe.

Isikhathi esibekelwe ukuphumeleliswa kwezicelo izikhulu zabahlola izilwane esifundazweni.

Uma isicelo sesifike ku Mqondisi obhekele ezempilo zezilwane, ukubhalisa kungathatha cishe izinsuku eziyishumu.

Izindleko zingashiyana kuya ngezifunda okuxhunyanwe nazo.
