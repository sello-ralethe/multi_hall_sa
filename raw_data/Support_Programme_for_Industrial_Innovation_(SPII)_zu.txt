==================================================

Uhlelo lokusiza ukuletha imikhiqizo emisha lwenzelwe ukuthuthukisa ubuchwepheshe Eningizimu Afrika, ekulethweni kosizo lokwenzancono imikhiqizo nohlelo olusha. Loluhlelo lugxile ikakhulukazi esigawini sokufufusa, esiqala ekuhlanganisweni kocubungulo bese kugcina la umzekeliso womkhiqizo ukhiqizwa khona.

Imininingwano iyatholakala kumnyango wezohwebo.
