Title: Ukufaka isicelo sokudoba izinhlanzi ukuze uziphilise nokukhululwa ukuthi wenze njalo

Language: zu
Date scraped: 2025-03-05 17:56:12

==================================================

Kumele ubhalisele imvume yokudobela ukuziphilisa uma uhlala ngasogwini futhi ufuna ukusebenzisa ingcebo ephilayo yasolwandle.

Kufanele ufake isicelo kumakomidi asebenzisana nabaphathi basendaweni yakho asunguliwe.

Ukuze ufaneleke ukuthola imvume, kumele uhambisane nendlela yokuqinisekisa lokho okudingekayo.

Qaphela: Kuphambene nomthetho ukuziphilisa kanye nokudoba izikali ezincane zezinhlanzi ngaphandle kwemvume. Okwamanje ukuziphilisa kanye nokudoba izinhlanzi kuphethwe ngabemvume yezokudoba izinhlanzi kuze kube umthetho wokudoba izinhlanzi uyaphumelela.

Imvume ivuselelwa njalo ngonyaka. Ngaphambi kokuba unikwe imvume abasebenzela lomnyango bakuchazela yonke imibandela kanye nenani lomkhawulo.

Uma uhluleka ukuhambisana nale mithetho ungabhekana nomthetho okuhlanganisa ukumiswa kwemvume, ukusulwa noma ukuhoxiswa kwemvume yakho emabhukwini.

Inqubo yokufakwa kwesicelo ingathatha isikhathi esingange nyanga kuya ngokuthi mungakanani umthamo wezicelo. Izisebenzi zomnyango zizobiza umhlangano womphakathi ukunikezela izimvume kubobonke abadobi ababhalisile.
