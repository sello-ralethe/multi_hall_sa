==================================================

Njengomqashi obhalisileyo, kufuneka ufake phesenti ethile yomvuzo womsebenzi kwingxowa mali yembuyekezo.

Ufanele ukufaka iinkcukacha zomvuzo unyaka nonyaka.

iinkcukacha zomvuzo kufuneka zichaze uhlobo lweshishini lwakho, inani labasebenzi abaqashiweyo, imivuzo abayifumanyo (imivuzo kunye nemirholo) we xesha elimselweyo, ukusukela ekuqaleni kwenyanga ka Matshi ukuya ekupheleni kuka February (ixesha lerhafu).

Fumana ngokupheleleyomalunganokufaka iinkcukacha zemivuzo kwingxowa mali yembuyekezookanye qhagamshelaingxowa mali Yembuyekezo.
