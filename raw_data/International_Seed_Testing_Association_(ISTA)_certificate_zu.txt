Bhalisela isitifiketi sokuhlola imbewu kumhlaba wonke endaweni yokuhlola imbewu esemthethweni.

OSTL indawo yokuhlola esemthethweni (inombolo yegunya ZA01) ye ISTA. OSTL igunyazwe ukunikezela ngezitifiketi zokuhlola imbewu.

Ungabhalisela isitifiketi sembewu esi Wolintshi noma esi Luhlaza

Ukuthola isitifiketi esiluhlaza:

Igama lebhange: Standard BankIgama legatsha: ArcadiaIkhodi yegatsha: 01-08-45Inombolo yasebhangi: 011244887Igama lesikhwama: NDA Seed Testing Services

Ungakhokha ngendlela yesimanje noma ezindaweni okukhokhwa.

Khumbula ukufaka inombolo yesitatimende ukuze kuzobonakala ukuthi ukhokhela bani.

I OSTL ibhekele ukuhlolwa kwembewu ngesizathu soyidlulisela kwamanye amazwe. Izinhlobonhlobo zezitshalo zihlolwa ngendlela ehlukile nesikhathi sokuhlola sihlukile, kungaba izinsuku noma amaviki.
