Title: Vuselela isicelo sokubhalisa ukubulalala iintsholongwane kwisakhiwo

==================================================

Kufanele ufake isicelo ukuze uvuselele ubhaliso lokubulala iinntsholongwane kwisakhiwo rhoqo emva kweminyaka emithathu. Isicelo sakho ungasithumela kuNobhala woMthetho 36 we-1947 phambi komhla wama-31 kweyoKwindla wonyaka lowo umjikelo wokubhaliswa kwakho uphela ngawo.Kufanele uqhagamshelane nesebe lezoLimo kwi phondo lakho ukuze ufumane ugqirha wezilwanyana karhulumente enginqini yakho ukuze azohlola ukubulawa kweentsholongwane kwisakhiwo sakho. Ugqirha wezilwanyana uzakunika isatifikethi emva kohlolo uze esosatifikethi usifake kwisicelo sakho.Isatifikethi kufanele siqinisekise okubana:

Ukuba awukwazanga ukuvuselela ngomhla okanye ngaphambi komhla wama-31 kweyoKwindla wonyaka wokugqibela wokuphelelwa kokubhaliswa kwakho uNobhala woMthetho 36 we-1947 uzakusula ukubhaliswa kwakho kokuqala nesatifikethi sakho aszukusebenza.

1.    Qhagamshelana nesebe lezolimo lephondo ucele inkcukacha zogqirha wezilwanyana enginqini yakho.2.    Qhagamshelana nogqirha wezilwanyana umcele azokuhlola ukubulawa kweentsholongwane kwisakhiwo sakho ukuze ufumane isatifikethielisuka kuye.3.    Emva kokuba ufumene isatifikethi sikagqirha wezilwanyana, kufuneka ufumane ifomu lokufaka isicelo sokubhalisa ukubulawa kweentsholongwane kwisakhiwo uligcwalise.4.    Ifomu yakho yesicelo kufuneka ikhatshwe koku okulandelayo:

5.    Kufanele uhlawule imali yesicelo ngetsheki, i-postal order okanye i-money order ku: Director-General: Department of Agriculture, Land Reform and Rural Development, i-reference: Sterilising Plant. Ukuba uzakuzisa isicelo sakho ngesandla nemali yesicelo, qinisekisa ukuthi uza nobungqina bokuthi uhlawulile. Ukuba uhlawula nge-Electronic Transfer Account, nceda uyifake kule-akhawunti:Igama le-account: NDA-ACT36 of 1947Inombolo ye-account: 11 2031 02I-reference: 13SP1 kunye negama lesakhiwoIkhodi ye-branch: 01 0845Igama lebhanki: Standard Bank6.    Thumela ifomu yakho nobungqina bokuthi uhlawulile ku: The Registrar Act 36 of 1947, Private Bag X250, Pretoria 0001 okanye ungalihambisa ngesandla ulise ku: The Registrar Act 36 of 1947, Agriculture Building, 20 Steve Biko Street, Pretoria.
