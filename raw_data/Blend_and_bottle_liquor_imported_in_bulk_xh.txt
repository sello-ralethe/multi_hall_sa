Ukuxuba utywala obuthengwe kwamanye amazwe ngobuninzi ngenjongo yokuthengisa kufanele ufumane imvume kwiSebe Lezolimo, Amahlathi nezokuloba. Ungaxuba utywala obusuka kwamanye amazwe neemveliso zeli loMzantsi Afrika, amanzi okanye ezinye imveliso ezisuka phesheya kwezilwandle.

Akukho xesha elimiselweyo kule nkonzo.
