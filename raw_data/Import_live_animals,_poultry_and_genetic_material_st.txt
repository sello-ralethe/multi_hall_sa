Title: Amohela ditswantle tsa diphoofolo le dintho tsa le
Source URL: https://www.gov.za/st/services/animal-improvement/amohela-ditswantle-tsa-diphoofolo-le-dintho-tsa-le
Language: st
Date scraped: 2025-03-05 18:01:32

==================================================

Haeba o batla ho reka diphoofolo tse phelang, bomaditshibana le thepa ya dijine ka ntle e kang lero la botona le peo etsa kopo ya tumello le Mongodisi wa Ntlafatso ya Diphoofolo ho reka diphoofolo kapa thepa ya dijine ho tla ka Aforika Borwa. O tshwanela ho etsa kopo matsatsing a 30 pele o reka ka ntle.

O tshwanela hape ke ho etsa kopo ya setifikeiti sa tsa diphoofolo ho Motsamaisi wa Bophelo bo Botle ba Diphoofolo.

Temoso: Ho tloha ledibohong la ho kena, bomaditshibana le mahe ba tla tsamaiswa ka tlasa bodisa ba ngaka ya diphoofolo kapa ka koloi e kwetsweng ke mohlanka wa tsa diphoofolo.

Ho tla nka matsatsi a 30 ho hlophisa kopo ya hao.
