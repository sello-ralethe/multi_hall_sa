Title: Isicelo sokungenisa elizweni iimveliso zezilwanyana

Language: xh
Date scraped: 2025-03-05 17:30:33

==================================================

Ukuba ufuna ukufaka kweli loMzantsi Afrika izilwanyana eziphilayo, imveliso yezilwanyana, izinto ezinezifo ezosulelaya, umthetho unyanzelilsa ukuba ufake isicelo kwaye ufumane iphepha  mvume lokufaka izilwana kumlawuli wempilo yazilwanyana.

Kufanele ube nalemvume phambi kokuba isilwanyana okanye imveliso ithunyelwe ukuphuma kwelolizwe liyithumelayo.

Ukuba uthenga izilwanyana zefama ezifana neenkomo, iigusha, iibhokhwe, amahashe, iihagu, ingciniba, inkukhu (hayi iikati nezinja) okanye  incindi yemvelo  yezi zilwanyana. kufanele ufumane imvume kumbhexeshi wophuculo  lwezilwanyana kuqala.Imibuzo malunga nolwazi ngabagunyazisi bophuculo lwezilwanya tsalela umnxeba kule nombolo 319 7434 okanye 012 319 7424.

Kukho imigaqonkqubo yokuthenga izilwanyana nemveliso yezilwanyana kwamanye amazwe. Imibandela yezempilo ihlala iguquka ngako oko qhagamshela icandelo lemithetho yokungena nokuphuma kwe mpahla kubalawuli bempilo yezilwanyana, feksela apha 012 329 6892 ukuze ufumane ulwazi lwamva nje lwemibandela. Kungabakho eminye imithetheo elawula imveliso ezithile ngako oko kufuneka uqinisekise ukuthi  uyonelisa yonke imibandela esemthethweni.

Isaziso esibalulekileyo:

Ukuba nje igosa lonyango lwezilwanyana kwi ofisi ka zwelonke linga samkela isicelo kuthabatha iintsuku ezintathu ukuya kwekwezintlanu ukugqoba isicelo kukhutshwe ne phepha mvume.
