Inkqubo encedisa indlela ezintsha zokwenza kubavelisi bempahla (SPII) yenzelwe ukuphuhlisa uveliso ngobuxhaka-xhakais ngokuncedisa ngokwezimali kwimveliso ezingaqhelekanga okanye indlela zokewnza ezingaqhelekanga. SPII ijoliswe ngqo kuphuhliso oluqala apho kuphela khona uphando lokuqala.

Fumana ngokubanzi ngale nkqubokwisebe lezorwebo nophuhliso (iDTI).
