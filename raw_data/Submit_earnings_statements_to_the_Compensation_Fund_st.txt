Title: Ho nehelana ka ditatemente tsa meputso ho Letlole la Ditlhapiso
Source URL: https://www.gov.za/st/services/compensation-fund/ho-romela-ditatemente-tsa-kuno-letloleng-la-ditlhapiso
Language: st
Date scraped: 2025-03-05 18:31:36

==================================================

Jwaleka monngamosebetsi ya ingodisitseng, o lokela ho nehelana ka phesente e itseng ya meputso ya mosebeletsi ho Letlole la Ditlhapiso.

O lokela honehelana ka setatementese hlakisang makeno ana selemo le selemo.

Ditatemente tsa meputso di lokela ho bontsha mofuta wa kgwebo ya hao, palo ya basebeletsi ba hao le tjhelete eo o ba lefileng yona (meputso le mekgolo) bakeng sa nako ya hlahlobo, ho tloha maqalong a Hlakubele ho isa mafelong a Hlakola (selemong sa lekgetho).

Thola lesedi le fetang lena mabapi leho nehelana ka ditatemente tsa meputso ho Letlole la Ditlhapisokapa iteanye leLetlole la Ditlhapiso.
