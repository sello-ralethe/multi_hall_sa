Uncedo lwemveliso (PI) luyinxalenye yenkqubo yokukhuphisana kwezeMpahla nokuthunga. Isuka ekusungulweni kweenqubo ezenzelwe indawo nganye(CSPs) Â lisebe Lezorwebo nophuhliso (the dti), Kwezempahla, ukuthunga, izihlangu isikhumba nemveliso yezikhumba

Uncedo lwezimali lufuna ukupha ikampani ithuba lokuzithethelela kwidesika ye CTCP yophuhliso lwamashishini emveliso (IDC). Baphinde bazobe umqolo wedesika ye CTCP ukuthi ijongane nodaba lwazo.

Ungasebenzisa yonke inzuzo njenge sibonelo sokuphucula indawo okanye imali enenzala okanye indibanisela yazo zombini.

Fumana ngokubanzi ngalenkqubo kwisebe lezorwebo nophuhliso (iDTI).
