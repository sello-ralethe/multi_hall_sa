Title: Imvume nenombolo yokuhweba ngemoto
Source URL: https://www.gov.za/zu/services/services-organisations/driving-and-transport/imvume-nenombolo-yokuhweba-ngemoto
Language: zu
Date scraped: 2025-03-05 17:43:32

==================================================

Ukuze ushayele imoto engabhalisiwe emgwaqweni womphakathi eNingizimu Afrika, kumele uqale ufake isicelo semvume nenombolo yokuhweba ngemoto.

Inombolo yokuhweba ngemoto isetshenziswa ngabakhiqizi ababhalisiwe, abakhi bezimoto noma abazilanda kwamanye amazwe, izinkontileka ezithutha izimoto, abathengisi bezimoto noma izinhlangano zezezimali ukuze bashayele imoto engabhalisiwe noma engenalayisense emgwaqweni womphakathi lapho beqhuba ibhizinisi labo. Imisebenzi enjalo ibandakanya:

Isicelo sisetshenzwa ngalo lolo suku.

Thola ukuthi kubiza malini egunyeni lokubhalisa langakini.

Ifomu leNombolo Yokuhweba Ngemoto (MTN1) (Application and notice i.r.o motor trade number and blank temporary permits [Form MTN1])

Contact the registration authority service centre at your local municipality
