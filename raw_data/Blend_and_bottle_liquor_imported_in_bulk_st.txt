Title: Kopo ya ho tswaka jwala bo bongata ba setswantle
Source URL: https://www.gov.za/st/services/liquor/kopo-ya-ho-tswaka-jwala-bo-bongata-ba-setswantle
Language: st
Date scraped: 2025-03-05 18:05:46

==================================================

Haebe o tlo kopanya majwala ao o a rekilweng kantle ka bongata bakeng sa thekiso o tlamehile ho fumana tumello ho tswa ho lefapha la Temo, Meru le Botshwasi. O ka kopanya jwala le sehlahiswa sa Aforika Borwa, metsi kapa sehlahiswa se seng se tswang mose ho mawatle.

Ha ho na nako e itseng e behilweng bakeng sa tshebetso ena.

Ditefiso tsa 2024/25

Application for blending of liquor imported in bulk
