Title: Closing an older persons' residential facility
Source URL: https://www.gov.za/services/services-organisations/change-registration/closing-older-persons-residential-facility
Language: en
Date scraped: 2025-03-05 17:13:41

==================================================

Do you want to close an older persons' residential facility and cancel your registration certificate? You must notify the Department of Social Development and residents within 90 days of your intention to close the residential facility.

You must ensure that the older persons are offered proper counseling before the closing down of the residential facility.

You must find suitable accommodation for the older persons in another registered residential facility and return the registration certificate to your nearest Department of Social Development office.

There is no service standard.

The service is free.

The application forms are available from your nearestSocial Development office.
