Title: Go dira kgopelo ya laesense ya go tšweletša ditšweletšwa tša oli e tala
Source URL: https://www.gov.za/st/services/petroleum/go-dira-kgopelo-ya-laesense-ya-go-t%C5%A1welet%C5%A1a-dit%C5%A1welet%C5%A1wa-t%C5%A1a-oli-e-tala
Language: st
Date scraped: 2025-03-05 18:12:11

==================================================

Laesense ya ho hlahisa peteroliamo e o thusa hore o etse dihlahiswa tsa peteroliamo mme o di rekisetse barekisi ba nang le dilaesense ba rekisang dithoto tse ngata, kantle le ho rekisetsa mafatshe a kantle.

Ela hloko: motho ha a dumellwa ho etsa dihlahiswa tsa peteroliamo kantle le ho tshwara laesense ya ho hlahisa.

O tla nkuwa o tshwere laesense ya ho hlahisa, le ha ho le jwalo, o tlamehile ho etsa kopo ya laesense ya ho hlahisa nakong ya dikgwedi tse tsheletseng ho tloha mohla Molao o kenang tshebetsong, ha o ne o ntse o sebetsa jwaloka mohlahisi pele Molao o kena tshebetsong ka  la 17th Hlakubele 2006.

Qeto e lokela ho nkuwa nakong ya matsatsi a 90 ho tloha mohlang oo Lefapha le fumaneng bopaki ba phatlalatso ya tsebiso ya kopo.
