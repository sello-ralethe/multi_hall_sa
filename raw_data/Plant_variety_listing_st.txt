Title: Kopo ya ho lemoha mokga wa semela
Source URL: https://www.gov.za/st/services/services-organisations/permits-licences-and-rights/plant-production/kopo-ya-ho-lemoha
Language: st
Date scraped: 2025-03-05 17:52:32

==================================================

Ho rekisa peo kapa dikarolwana tsa yona (semela se ikatisang) tse tswang semeleng, o tlameha ho qala ka ho e ngodisa lenaneng la mefutafuta.

Pele o ka rekisa semela se ikatisang sa mefuta e itseng ya semela Aforika Borwa, o tlameha ho etsa kopo Ngodisong ya Ntlafatso ya Dimela hore mofutafuta wa semela sa hao se setjha o ananelwe.

O ka nna wa kopa Lefapha la Temothuo, Meru le Ditlhapi (DAFF) ho kenyeletsa mofutafuta wa semela se setjha mananeng a mefutafuta ho ya ka Molao wa Ntlafatso ya Dimela wa 1976 (Tafole ya 2 ya Molao).

DAFF e boloka manane a mefutafuta bakeng sa dijalo tse bohlokwa haholo tsa temo, meroho le ditholwana. Ntlha ya phumano ke bohlokwa ba semela moruong wa Aforika Borwa.

Mofutafuta wa semela o ka kenngwa lenaneng (ananelwa) haeba ditlhoko tse latelang di kgotsofaditswe:

Haeba ho ya ka ditokelo tsa bahlahisi ba dimela ho hlokeha tshireletso, bakopi ba eletswa ho isa dikopo ka nako e le nngwe le Mongodisi wa Ditokelo tsa Bahlahisi ba Dimela ho boloka nako le tjhelete.

Tsamaiso ya ho kenngwa lenaneng ha mefutafuta e tla nka sehla se le seng ho isa ho tse pedi tsa kgodiso.
