Title: Tshebeletso ya Poso e tsamaiswang ke Mmuso
Source URL: https://www.gov.za/st/services/communication/laesense-ya-ho-tsamaisa-tshebeletso-ya-poso-ya-risefe
Language: st
Date scraped: 2025-03-05 17:43:14

==================================================

Ditshebeletso tsa poso tse tsamaiswang ke mmuso di kenyeletsa mangolo kaofela, diposekarete, dikgatiso, diphasele tse nyenyane le dintho tse ding tse poswang.

Ka kopo ela hloko: Ha jwale,Poso ya Aforika Borwake yona feela mokga o nang le laesense. Ha ho motho ya ka tsamaisang tshebeletso ya poso e tsamaiswang ke mmuso ntle le ha a na le laesense ya ho etsa jwalo.

Ho fana ka dilaesense bakeng sa dibaka tse behetsweng thoko ho tla etsahala feela hang ha Letona le phatlaladitse memo ya hore ho etswe dikopo.
