==================================================

Ukungenisa ukudla okondla izilwane e Ningizimu Afrika, kufanele ufake isicelo sokuthola imvume bese ubhalisele nesicifiketi ku Mbhalisi we Act 36.

Kumele ungenise kulamachweba alandelayo:

Land border posts

International airports

International harbours

Inland

Uma unesitifiketi esibhalisiwe, awudingi ukuthola imvume yokungenisa. Ungasebenzisa isitifiketi esibhalisiwe ukungeni kuleli. Cofoza ngenzansi uzothola ukuthi ungasibhalisela kanjani isitifiketi.

Imigqomo ozoyisebenzisa ukungenisa kulei, kumele ibhalwe futhi ibe namalebuli lomkhiqizo owungenisa kuleli.

Ukuthola imvume yokungenisa izimpahla kuthatha isonto elilodwa. Ukubhalisela isitifiketi kuthatha izinyanga ezine.
