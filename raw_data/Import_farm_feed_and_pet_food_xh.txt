Title: Faka kweli ukudla kwezilwanyana nokwe zilwanyana zasendlini
Source URL: https://www.gov.za/xh/services/fertilizers-farm-feeds-agricultural-remedies/faka-kweli-ukudla-kwezilwanyana-nokwe
Language: xh
Date scraped: 2025-03-05 18:19:13

==================================================

Ukuze ufake kweli ukudla kwezilwanyana nokwezilwanyana zasendlini, kufanele ufake isicelo sephepha-mvume lokuthenga kumazwe angaphandle nesiqinisekiso sobhaliso ukusuka kumbhexeshi woMthetho 36.

Kufanele ufake kweli usebenzisa amazibuko okungena alandelayo:

Izibuko lomda osemhlabeni

Izikhululo zeenqwelo ntaka zamazwengamazwe

Amazibuko aselinxwemeni amazwe ngamazwe

Phaka elizweni

Ukuba unaso isiqinisekiso sokubhalisa, awudingi iphepha-mvume. Ungasebenzisa isiqinisekiso sokubhalisa ukufaka kweli.

Izingxobo ozakube uzisebenzisa ukufaka kweli kufanele zibhalwe zikwa phawuliwe ngokuchaza impahla oyifaka kweli.

Ofanele kukwenza

Imvume youfakakweli ithatha iveki ibenye. Isiqinisekiso sokubhalisa sithatha iinyanga ezine.
