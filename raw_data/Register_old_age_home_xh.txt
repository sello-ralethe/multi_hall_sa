Title: Bhalisa ikhaya labadala

Language: xh
Date scraped: 2025-03-05 17:09:53

==================================================

Ukuba ufuna ukubhalisa ikhaya labadala njengomntu oyedwa okanye njengombutho, kufuneka ufake isicelo kwi-ofisi yeSebe lezoPhuhliso loLuntu ekufutshane nawe.

Kungathatha iiveki ezine ukuchophela isicelo sakho.

Isicelo sona sisimahla kodwa uza kuhlawula iziqinisekiso ezifana nezokusingqongileyo nezempilo.

Amaxwebhu okufaka isicelo ayafumaneka kwi-ofisiyeSebe lezoPhuhliso loLuntuekufutshane nawe.
