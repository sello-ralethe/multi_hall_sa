Maqondana nokubhalisa isiteshi sokuhlola izimoto (VTS)

I-VTS yisikhungo esihlola ukufaneleka kwezimoto ukuthi zisetshenziswe emigwaqeni yomphakathi yaseNingizimu Afrika. Uma imoto ikuphasa ukuhlolwa, kuzokhishwa isitifiketi sokufaneleka ukuba semgwaqeni.

Ukubhalisa isiteshi sokuhlola izimoto (VTS), kufanele ubhale i-afidavithi noma isitatimende esisho izizathu zokubhalisa bese ihanjiswa ku-MEC.

I-afidavithi noma isitatimende esishiwo kufanele ibe nalokhu:

Uma seyitholakele, u-MEC uzocabanga ngesicelo sakho.

Uma isicelo sakho sivunyiwe, kufanele kuhlangatshezwane nokulandelayo:

Ukuxilongwa nokuhlolwa kwemoto esiteshini sokuhlola njengoba kudingeka kukhodi yokusebenza i-SABS 047 “Ukuhlolwa kwezimoto kokufaneleka ukuba semgwaqeni

Okufanele ukwenze

Yazi: Kungadingeka ukuthi ulethe imibhalo njengoba idingwa ngu-MEC.

Isikhathi esinqunyiwe

Kungenzeka kuthathe izinyanga eziyisithupha ukusebenza isicelo sakho

Kubiza malini

Xhumana nehhovisi lamalayisense lasekhaya lezindleko.

Amafomu okumele agcwaliswe

Isicelo kanye nesaziso maqondana nokubhaliswa kwesiteshi sokuhlola, i-TS1 (Application and notice in respect of registration as testing station, TS1).
