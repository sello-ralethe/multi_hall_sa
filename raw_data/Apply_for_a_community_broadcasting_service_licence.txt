Title: Apply for a community broadcasting service licence
Source URL: https://www.gov.za/services/communication/community-broadcasting
Language: en
Date scraped: 2025-03-05 17:41:01

==================================================

The community broadcasting licence allows a non-profit organisation to serve the broadcasting needs of a community for a maximum of four years. It is renewable on condition that you have complied with the conditions set out by the licensing authority.

Special event community sound licences can be issued for a period not exceeding 45 days.

Temporary community television licences can be issued for a period not exceeding one year.

A four-year community broadcasting licence may be granted for a:

sound broadcasting service

television broadcasting service.

TheDepartment of Communications and Digital Technologiessupports four-year licensees with broadcast and production equipment, provided they meet the requirements of the Department.

Note: TheIndependent Communication Authority of South Africa (Icasa)issued amoratorium on the licensing of terrestrial community television licenceson 29 March 2010. It issued amoratorium on new applications for community radio stationson 22 September 2015.
