Title: Isicelo sokungenisa izilwane nemikhiqizo ezilwane
Source URL: https://www.gov.za/zu/services/import/animals-and-animal-products
Language: zu
Date scraped: 2025-03-05 17:30:43

==================================================

Uma ufuna ukuletha izilwane eziphilayo, imikhiqizo yazo, izinto ezithelelekayo noma ezithathelanayo eNingizimu Afrika, umthetho onqunyiwe uthi kumele ufaka isicelo bese uthola imvume evela kumqondisi wempilo yezilwane. Kumele uthole le mvume ngaphambi kokuba umkhiqizo noma isilwane kungene emkhunjini ukusuka kulelozwe.

Uma ngabe uletha izilwane zasepulazini eziphilayo njenge zinkomo, izimvu, izimbuzi, amahhashi, izingulube, izintshe, izinkukhu (hhayi izinja namakati) noma isizukulwano salezizilwane, kumele uthole igunya ku Mbhalisi obhekele ukwenza ngcono impilo yezilwane ngaphambi kokuba kukhishwe imvume. Mayelana nemibuzo ephathelene negunya lokuthuthukiswa kwezilwane sicela ushayele kulenombolo yocingo: 012 319 7434 noma 012 319 7424.

Kunemithetho ebekiwe okumele ilandelwe yokulethwa kwezilwane nemikhiqizo yazo zisuka emazweni angaphandle. Imigomo yezokuphepha inokushintsha manje namanje, Sicele uthintane nethimba lemigomo yokulethwa nokuthuthwa kwezinto ziya emazweni angaphandle yomqondisi wezokuphepha kwezilwane ukuthola izidingo zakamuva, kulesisikhahlamezi: 012 329 6892. Kungaba nezinye izinqubo mgomo ezibekiwe ngemikhiqizo ngaminye kumele wenze isiqinisekiso sokuthi uyayilandela yoke imigomo ngokusemthethweni.

Okubalulekile:

Azikho izicelo eziphasiswayo ngaphandle kwesiqiniseko sokuthi kukhokhiwe. Thumela isiqinisekiso sokuthi ukhokhile ngesikhahlamezi ku: 012 329 8292 uqondise ku: Ina Labuschagne.

Uma u dokotela kahulumeni osebenzela ehhovisi likazwelonke esephumelelise imvumo sokungenisa, kuthatha izinsuku ezintathu kuya kwezinhlanu ukuphumelelisa isicelo kanye nokuthola iphemithi
