==================================================

Uma ufuna ukufaka isicelo selungelo lokuhlwaya kumele ufake isicelo ku-inthanethi ngokuvakashela isizindalwazi soMnyango WezokuMbiwa Phansi:http://portal.samradonline.co.za.

Ilungelo lokuhlwaya yimvume ekuvumela ukuba wena noma inkampani yakho ihlole noma iphenye umhlaba lowo ngenhloso yokuhlonza ukuthi kukhona okungaba noma okungumgodi womcebo wezimbiwa phansi.

Ilungelo lokuhlwaya lisebenza iminyaka emihlanu, emva kosuku lokuphelelwa yisikhathi ungacela ukuvuselelwa kwelungelo lokuhlwaya isikhathi esiyiminyaka engevile kwemithathu.

Ilungelo lokuhlwaya lizonikezelwa uma izidingo ezilandelayo kuhlangatshezwene nazo uma:

Izindleko zibalwa ngokubuka isimbiwa leso ofake isicelo saso.

Izicelo kufanele zilethweku-inthanethi.
