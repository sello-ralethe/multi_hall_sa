Title: Renew registration of sterilising plants

==================================================

You must apply to renew your sterilising plant registration every three years. You must submit your application to the Registrar of Act 36 of 1947 before 31 March of the year in which your registration cycle lapses.

You must contact your provincial department of Agriculture to get a state veterinary official in your area to supervise your sterilising plant. Should you fail to renew your sterilising plant registration on or before the 31st of March of the last year which your registration cycle lapses, the Registrar of Act 36 of 1947 will cancel your first registration and your certificate will be invalid.
