Title: Dira kgopelo ya phemiti le nomoro ya kgwebo ka dikoloi
Source URL: https://www.gov.za/nso/services/services-organisations/driving-and-transport/dira-kgopelo-ya-phemiti-le-nomoro-ya-kgwebo
Language: nso
Date scraped: 2025-03-05 17:43:38

==================================================

Gore o kgone go otlela koloi tseleng ya setšhaba mo Afrika Borwa, o swanetše go thoma ka go dira kgopelo ya phemiti le nomoro ya go gweba ka koloi.

Tirelo ye e lebane le bašomiši ka moka ba dikoloi, go akaretšwa le batšweletši ba dikoloi, bao ba rekago dikoloi dinageng tša ntle, borakontraka ba dikoloi le barekiši ba ba nyakago go rekiša, fetolantšha, lokiša goba go romela dikoloi go beng ba tšona. Nomoro ya kgwebo ya dikoloi e dumella batšweletši ba dikoloi, bao ba rekago dikoloi dinageng tša ntle, borakontraka ba dikoloi le barekiši ba dikoloi go otlela koloi ye e sego ya ngwadišwa mo tseleng ya setšhaba.

Eya ofising ya therafiki ya kgauswi le wena le tše di latelago:

Tlatša fomo MTN 1 ofising ya kgauswi le wena ya therafiki.

Hlaloša gore ke ka lebaka la eng o nyaka dinomoro tša kgwebo le gore o nyaka dinomoro tše kae tša kgwebo.

Kgopelo e phethwa ka lona letšatši leo.

Ikgokaganye le kantoro ya tikologo ya geno ya dilaesense go tseba ka ga ditefo.

Kopo ya Nomoro ya Kgwebo  ya Dirori, Foromo MTN1 (Application and notice i.r.o motor trade number and blank temporary permits [Form MTN1])

Contact the registration authority service centre at your local municipality
