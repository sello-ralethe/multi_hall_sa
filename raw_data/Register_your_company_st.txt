Title: Ngodisa khamphani ya hao
Source URL: https://www.gov.za/st/services/services-organisations/register-business-or-organisation/ngodisa-khamphani-ya-hao
Language: st
Date scraped: 2025-03-05 17:11:22

==================================================

O ka ngodisa khamphani jwalo ka e sa etseng phaello kapa e etsang phaello le Khomishine ya Thepa le Boikgathatso ba Dikhamphani (CIPC). Khamphani ke motho eo molaong a nang le bokgoni le matla a ho iketsetsa ka boyena. Molao o bona khamphani hantle jwaloka tsela eo e bonang motho ka yona.

Dikhamphani tse etsang phaello di ka kenyelletswa tlasa mefuta e fapaneng ya dikhamphani e welang ka tlasa Molao wa Dikhamphani.

Thola lesedi le fetang lena mabapi le ho ngodisa khamphani hoKhomishine ya Thepa le Boikgathatso ba Dikhamphani (CIPC)kapa letsetsa CIPC ho 086 100 2472.
