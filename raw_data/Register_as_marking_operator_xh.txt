Title: Isicelo sokubhalisela ukuba ngumphawuli wezilwanyana

==================================================

Ukuba ufuna ukwenza imali ngoku phawula izilwanyana, faka isicelo kwisebe lezolimo,amahlathi nokuloba (Umbhexeshi: amaphawu ezilwanyana) ukuze ubhaliswe njengomphawuli wezilwanyana.

Ukuze uphumelele ekubhaliseni kufuneka igqibe ezi zifundo zilandelaya:

Njengomphawuli kufuneka ugcine ingcwadana obhala kuyo izilwanyana oziphawulileyo.

Uzakubhaliswa njengomphawuli xa ugqiba ukuphumelela izifundo.

R 200
