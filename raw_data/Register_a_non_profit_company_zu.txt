Title: Ukurejista inkampani engenzi inzuzo
Source URL: https://www.gov.za/zu/services/register-business-or-organisation/isicelo-sokubhalisa-inkampani-ngaphansi-kuka-section-21
Language: zu
Date scraped: 2025-03-05 17:05:30

==================================================

Ukuze inhlangano yakho yomphakathi ihlonishwe njengesemthethweni, kufanele uyibhalise njengenkampani engzenzi inzuzo (non-profit company (NPC)) neKhomishini Yezinkampani kanye Nempahla Ebhaliwe (Companies and Intellectual Property Commission (CIPC)).

Ama-NPC yizinhlangano ezenzelwa ukusiza abantu, ukuvikela imvelo noma ukubumbana ngokwenza okuhle. Zingabandakanya amasonto, izinhlangano zokusiza ngesihle kanye nezinhlangano zamasiko. Inhloso enkulu ye-NPC ukusiza umphakathi, hhayi ukwenza inzuzo. Imali engenayo kanye nempahla kungabiwa kubahlanganyeli, amalungu, abaqondisi noma abasebenzi benkampani engenzi inzuzo, ngaphandle kwesinxephezelo esikahle semisebenzi abayenzile. Zonke izimpahla zenkampani engenzi inzuzo kanye nemali engenayo kumele kusetshenziselwe ukuqhuba inhloso yayo eshiwo, njengoba ibekiwe kuMemorandamu Yokuhlanganyela (MOI).

Inani eliphansi labahlanganyeli abathathu kufanele ligcwalise futhi lisayine i-MOI. Inani eliphansi labaqondisi abathathu kufanele liqokwe.

Izinkampani ezingenzi inzuzo kufanele ngazo zonke izikhathi okungenani zibe nomqondisi oyedwa (ngaphandle uma i-MOI ikhombisa inombolo ephansi yabaqondisi). Ukuqashwa komcubunguli wamabhuku kanye nonobhala wenkampani akugunyaziwe  kodwa akuphoqiwe

Ungabhalisa Inkampani Engenzi Inzuzo njenge:

Igama lenkampani engenzi inzuzo lizogcina ngo-“NPC”.

•    Sicela wazi: Uma igama eliphakanyisiwe laliwe, inkampani isangabhaliswa bese inombolo yokubhalisa ibe yigama lenkampani yakho ekuhlanganyeleni, kuze kube yileso sikhathi lapho igama elifanele seligciniwe noma selivunyiwe.
