Title: Apply for a commercial broadcasting licence
Source URL: https://www.gov.za/services/communication/commercial-broadcasting
Language: en
Date scraped: 2025-03-05 17:40:28

==================================================

A commercial broadcasting service licence is issued to a broadcasting service operating for profit or as part of a profit entity. TheSouth African Broadcasting Corporationis the only licensed public broadcaster in South Africa.

The application form and the proposed geographical areas form part of the specific invitation to apply published in the Government Gazette.

Licensing may take six to twelve months.

Tariffs 2023/24

The application form will be part of the schedules that will be published in the Government Gazette .
