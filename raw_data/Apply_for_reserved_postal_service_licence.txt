Language: en
Date scraped: 2025-03-05 17:42:40

==================================================

The reserved postal services include all letters, postcards, printed matter, small parcels and other postal articles.

Please note: Currently, theSouth African Post Officeis the only licensed entity. No person may operate a reserved postal service unless they have a licence to do so.

Licensing for reserved services will only happen once the Minister has published an invitation to apply.
