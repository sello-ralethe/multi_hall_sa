Title: Icandelo 12i loncedo lwerhafu
Source URL: https://www.gov.za/xh/services/services-organisations/business-incentives/icandelo-12i-loncedo-lwerhafu
Language: xh
Date scraped: 2025-03-05 17:25:00

==================================================

Uncedo lwe Rhafu lwe12i luzotywe ukuthi luncedise utyalo mali oluluhlaza (abavelisi abatsha abaneprojekthi esebenzisa impahla entsha naleyo engeka sebenzi ukenza imveliso), kunye otyalo mali oluqhelwkileyo (i.e. ukukhulisa okanye ukuphuculwa projekthi yemveliso). Uncedo olutsha luncedisa ngotyalo mali kunye noqeqesho.

Fumana ngokubanzi ngalenkqubo kwisebe lezorwebo nophuhliso (iDTI).
