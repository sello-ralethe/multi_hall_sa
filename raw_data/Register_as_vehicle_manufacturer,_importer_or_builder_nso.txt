Title: Ingwadiše bjalo ka motšweletši goba moreki wa dikoloi dinageng tša ntle
Source URL: https://www.gov.za/nso/services/services-organisations/driving-and-transport/ingwadi%C5%A1e-bjalo-ka-mot%C5%A1welet%C5%A1i-goba-moreki-wa
Language: nso
Date scraped: 2025-03-05 17:45:18

==================================================

Motho ofe le ofe yo a tšweletšago, a agago goba a fetolago sebopego sa dikoloi goba a rekago dikoloi dinageng tša ntle ka maikemišetšo a go di rekiša o swanetše go ingwadiša le Kgoro ya Dinamelwa.

Morago ga go amogela kgopelo ya gago, Kgoro ya Dinamelwa profenseng e tlo romela balekodi ba yona go yo lekola lefelo leo mokgopedi a šomelago go lona ka maikemišetšo a go kgonthiša gore lefelo leo le sepelelana le melawana ye e beilwego. Gape kgwebo ya gagwe e tlo swanela ke go sepela ka melawana yaSARS.

Ikopanye le Kgoro ya Dinamelwa profenseng ya geno oye le ditokumente tše di latelago:

Kgopelo e phethwa ka lona letšatši leo e dirilwego ka lona gomme setifikeiti sa boingwadišo se fiwa ka letšatši le le latelago.

Ikgokaganye le kantoro ya tikologo ya geno ya dilaesense go tseba ka ga ditefo.

Fomo ya MIB (Kgopelo le Tsebišo ya go ba Motšweletši/Moreki wa Dikoloi tša Dinaga tša Ntle/Moagi wa Dikoloi) (Application and notice i.r.o registration of a manufacturer/importer/builder of vehicles [Form MIB])
