Title: Ukufaka isicelo selungelo lokumba umgodi
Source URL: https://www.gov.za/xh/services/services-organisations/permits-licences-and-rights/mining-and-water/ukufaka-isicelo
Language: xh
Date scraped: 2025-03-05 18:06:32

==================================================

Ukuba ufuna ilungelo lokumba umgodi, kufuneka ufumane imvume kwiSebe lemiThombo yeziMbiwa ukuze umbe izimbiwa kwindawo ethile.

Ilungelo lokumba umgodi alinakuqhuba kangangexesha elingaphezulu kweminyaka engama-30.

Ilungelo lokumba umgodi ulinikwa ukuba:

Nxibelelana neSebe lemiThombo yeziMbiwa ukuze ufumane iinkcukacha ngemali ebhatalwayo.

Izicelo kufuneka zenziwekwi-intanethi.
