Title: Karolo 12i Ditsiane tsa Tumello ya Lekgatho

Language: st
Date scraped: 2025-03-05 17:25:15

==================================================

Karolo ya 12i ya Ditsiane tsa Lekgetho e etseditswe ho tshehetsa matsete a Greenfield (e leng diporojeke tse ntjha tsa indasteri tse sebedisang feela dithoto tsa dihlahiswa tse ntjha kapa tse sokang di sebediswa), mmoho le matsete a Brownfield (e leng katoloso kapa ntjhafatso ya diporojeke tsa indasteri tse ntseng di le teng). Ditsiane tse ntjha di fana ka tshehetso bakeng sa matsete a maholo le kwetliso.

Thola lesedi le fetang lena mabapi le lenaneo lena ho tswa hoLefapha la Kgwebisano le Indaseteri.
