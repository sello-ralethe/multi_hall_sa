==================================================

Uma ufuna ukungenisa inyama eluhlaza Eningizimu Afrika, noma ufuna ukuyidlulisa Eningizimu Afrika, umthetho udinga ukuba ufake isicelo bese unikezwa iphemithi yokungenisa kokuhlolwa kwezilwane noma iphemithi yokuthutha etholakala ku Mqondisi wezempilo zezilwane. Kufanele uthole iphemithi ngaphambi kokuba umthamo wenyama usuke ezweni okhishwa kuwo.

Umqondisi wezempilo zezilwane uzokhipha I phemithi lokungenisa noma iphemithi lokuthutha inyama eluhlaza emazweni la ahlole waphasisa ukuhlolwa kwezilwane kanye nezinga lezempilo zezilwane. (Imininingwano iyatholakala kuElmarieV@daff.gov.za, Inombolo yocingo 012 319 7700 noma isikhahlamezi 012 329 6892).

Imithetho yokungenisa ihlukene kuya ngomkhiqizo kanye nezempilo zezilwane izwe nezwe. Ezezempilo ziphoqa ukuba kube noshintsho emva kwesikhashana , sicela uthintane no Mqondisi wezempilo zezilwane (Inombolo yocingo: 012 319 7420 /7524 /7700, Isikhahlamezi 012 329 6892) ukuthola okudingakalayo okusha. Amaphemithi okungenisa kokuhlolwa kwezilwane anesikhashana nje futhi awokwenza noma ukungenisa umthamo owodwa nje kuphela.Ukungenisa emazweni esigaba sesithathu akuvamile ukuba kumukeleke.Kungumsebenzi wakho njengomngenisi ukuthi ufunde futhi uhambisane nemithetho yokungenisa kuleli.

Umthetho wokuphepha wenyama, yezinkulungwane ezimbili (Umthetho wamashumi amane wonyaka ka 2000) wenyama eseziqandisini ingangeniswa kubahlabi, abasiki kanye nabalungisa inyama abagunyazwe ihhovisi eliphezulu likazwe lonke Eningizimu Afrika.

Uhla lwezigungu luyatholakala lapha noma shayela ku 012 319 7700 noma uthumele ngesikhahlamezi isicelo ku 012 329 6892.

Umthamo wenyama ongeniswe usuka emazweni aphesheya eza ngezokuthutha emanzini ungangeniswa nga magogogo erifa kuphela. Inyama eluhlaza ingangeniswa kuphela uma iseziqandiseni amazingeni aphansi okubanda ebhaliwe ephemithini yokungeniswa.

Uma u-dokotela wezilwane osehhovisi likazwe lonke esephasise isicelo sakho sokungenisa, kuthatha izinsuku ezintathu kuya kwezinhlanu ukudlulisela isicelo sephemithi nokukhipha iphemithi.
