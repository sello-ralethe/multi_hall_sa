Title: Bhalisa njengomntu ofundisa abanye ukuqhuba
Source URL: https://www.gov.za/xh/services/services-organisations/driving-and-transport/bhalisa-njengomntu-ofundisa-abanye-ukuqhuba
Language: xh
Date scraped: 2025-03-05 17:44:31

==================================================

Ukuze ube ngumntu ofundisa abanye abantu ukuqhuba ngokusemthethweni eMzantsi Afrika, kufuneka ubhalise kwiSebe lezoThutho lePhondo. Abafundisa abanye ukuqhuba kufuneka babenesiqinisekiso esichaza udidi okanye uhlobo lwesithuthi abaqinisekiselwe ukufundisa abanye ngaso.

Abafundisa abanye ukuqhuba bahlelwa ngolu hlobo lulandelayo:

Uza kusebenzisa uhlobo lwesithuthi onephephamvume laso. Isiqinisekiso sokufundisa abanye ukuqhuba sisemthethweni unyaka omnye kuphela.

Imvume ingaba seyilungile kwiiveki ezintandathu ukuya kwezisibhozo.

Qhagamshelana ne-ofisi yakho yempephamvume yasekuhlaleni ufumane ixabiso.

Amaxwebhu ayafumaneka kuwo nawaphi na amaziko okuvavanyelwa impephamvume zokuqhuba okanye kwiwebhusayithi ye-eNatis.

Contact the licensing office at your local municipality
