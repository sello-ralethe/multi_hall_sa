Title: Lenaneo la Tshehetso ya Bohahlaudi
Source URL: https://www.gov.za/st/services/services-organisations/business-incentives/lenaneo-la-tshehetso-ya-bohahlaudi
Language: st
Date scraped: 2025-03-05 17:49:09

==================================================

Lenaneo la Tshehetso ya Bohahlaudi ke tjhelete ya kontane e buselletswang ya kerante eo sepheo sa yona e leng ho tshehetsa tlhabollo ya kgwebo ya bohahlaudi e tlang ho rotloetsa ho thehwa ha mesebetsi le ho eketsa ho atoloha ha matsete a bohahlaudi.

Kerante e etseditswe ho theha kapa ho atolosa ditshebetso tsa bohahlaudi tse jwaloka:

Batla lesedi le fetisisang hoLefapha la Bohahlaudi.
