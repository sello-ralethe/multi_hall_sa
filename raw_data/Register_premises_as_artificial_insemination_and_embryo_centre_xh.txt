Title: Bhalisa izakhiwo nje ngeziko lokuzalisa ngobuxhakaxhaka (AI centre)

Language: xh
Date scraped: 2025-03-05 18:03:02

==================================================

Ukuze ukwazi ukucubungula, ubeke ngendlea, unike amagama, ugcine okanye uthengise izinto zemveli, kufanele ubhalise iziko kuqala libe yi Al centre.

Isakhiwo sakho masibe noku okulandelayo:

Ezi zinto zibalwe ngentla kufanele ziqukwe kwiplani yendawo yakho.

Kufanele ube neplani yaphansi ebonakalisa mhlophe ubukhulu nobuncinane kunye nenkcukacha:

Izilwanyana ezigcinwe kwezi zakhiwo kufuneka zingadibani nezingaphandle kwezi zakhiwo. Biyela ngocingo izakhiwo jikelele.

Izilwanyana ezigcinwe kwindawo yezilwanyana ezine zifo akufuneki zidibane nezinye kwizakhiwe. Ufanele ube nezitala ezihlukileyo ezisetyenziswa ekuqokeleleni ,ekugcineni nokuvavanya icindi zemvelo kwindawo yezilwanyana ezinezifo. Qinisekisa ukuba izilwanyana azinikezelani ngezifo.

Abantu abasebenza nge hagu ezikwindawo yezigulayo akufanelanga badibane nabanye abasebenzi emsebenzini.

Kufanele kubekho indawo enkulu ukwenzela ukuba izilwanyana zikwazi ukuzilolonga.

Kufanele ube nezindlu ezahlukileyo zokwenza le misebenzi ilandelayo:

Abantu abaqokelela ezemvelo bangangeni kwezi zindlu sikhankanywe ngentla. Ezizindlu ekuqokelelwa, kucubungulwe, kubekwe, kubhalwe kungcinwe kuphinde kugcinwe izinto zemvelo kufuneka zihlale zicocekile ngamaxesha wonke.

Iindonga, phantsi nomphezulu wezindlu apho kuphathwa izinto zemvelo kufanele zicocwe rhoqo ukuze izilwanyana zikhuseleka.

Akukho xesha eliqingqiweyo kule nkonzo.
