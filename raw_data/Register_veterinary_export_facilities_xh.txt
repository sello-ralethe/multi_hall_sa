Title: Isicelo sokuvunywa nokubhaliswa kwamaziko okuthumela izilwanyana kumazwe
Source URL: https://www.gov.za/xh/services/services-organisations/export-permits/isicelo-sokuvunywa-nokubhaliswa-kwamaziko-okuthumela
Language: xh
Date scraped: 2025-03-05 17:38:28

==================================================

Ungakwazi ukuthengisa izilwanyana okanye imveliso yazo kumazwe angaphandle xa ziphuma kwizakhiwo ezihambisana nezinga lezomhlaba okanye imithetho yelizwe elithengayo. Ezindawo zingaquka isilarha esivunyiweyo, isitishi esikhuselekileyo kwizifo, iziko lokuzalisa ngobuxhaka-xhaka, izikolokugcina amaqanda enzalo, indawo yokudla izilwanyanaefama , indawo yemveliso yokutya, izakhiwo zokugcina ukutya.

Kuyahluka ukuya ngephondo.

Amaxabiso ayahluka ukuya ngokwamaphondo.

Amaxwebhu afumaneka  kugqirha we zilwanyana wephondo kuphela ngako oko qhagamshelana naye okanye  isebe lezolimo likazwelonke.
