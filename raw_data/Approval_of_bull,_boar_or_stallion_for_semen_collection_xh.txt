Title: Isicelo semvume yokuqokelela incindi yobudoda
Source URL: https://www.gov.za/xh/services/animal-improvement/isicelo-semvume-yokuqokelela-incindi-yobudoda
Language: xh
Date scraped: 2025-03-05 17:57:04

==================================================

Khon’ukuze ubenako ukuqokelela incindi yemveliso kwisilwanyana, kufuneka ufumane imvume kuMbhexeshi wophuculo lwezilwanyana.

Kufuneka uthathe umlinganiselo wegazi njengomzekelo wegazi lesilwanyana eso ofuna ukusisebenzisa ukuqokelela incindi zemveliso. Thumela umlinganiselo lowo wegazi kwicandelo elivunyiweyo lokuvavanya incindi zomzimba ukwenza uvavanyo. Icandelo elo lovavanyo lizakukunika isiqinisekiso esinenkcukacha zonke zomzimba ngesilwanyana eso. Isiqinisekiso siyafuneka ukuze abazalisi bemfuyo baqinisekise ngomnombo nobunjani besilwanyana.

Kuzokufuneka ugqirha wezilwanyana karhulumente asihlole isilwanyana eso. Qhagamshelana negosa lonyango lwezilwanyana kwiSebe leZolimo, kwiphondo okulo. Khon’ukuze isilwanyana sivumeleke ukuba sinikele ngencindi zemveliso kufuneka sibekanti sikwimo entle empilweni kwaye asinazifo. Ugqirha wezilwanyana uyakuhlola incindi zemveliso zesilwanyana eso ukuqinisekisa ukuba sikumgangatho oncomekayo kwaye siyazifikelela zonke imfuno. Emva kohlolo ugqirha wezilwanyana uzakugcwalisa  atyikitye isigaba isinikezela ngemvume soxwebhu. Usenokuzichongela owakho ugqirha wezilwanyana wabucala ukuba akutyikityele isiqinisekiso eso.

Xa sele usifakile isicelo sakho, umbhesexhi nentlanganisela yabazalisi bezilwanyana bazakuqinisekisa ubunyani benkcukacha ozifakileyo zikagqirha wezilwanyana nezo zisuka kwiziko lezovavanyo lwencindi zomzimba. Ukuba banelisekile zinkcukacha ezo, umbhexeshi uzakukwazisa ukuba uzise isilwanyana eso kugqirha wezilwanyana sivavanywe, khon’ukuze ufumane isiqinisekiso sovavanyo.

Ukuba kubekho ukungahambisani ngendlela ekudibaneni kwencindi zemveliso kuhlobo oluthile lwesilwanyana, uMbhexeshi uyawukukwazisa ukuba ufake isiqinesikiso sobume bencindi zesini zesilwanyana eso sisetyenzisiweyo. Ukuba uMbhexeshi kunye nentlanganisela yabazalisi bezilwanyana abanelisekanga, bayakukwazisa.

Qaphela: Ukuba ufuna ukusebenzisa izilwanyana ezibini nangaphezulu ukuqokelela incindi ezo zemveliso, kufanele ufake isicelo esahlukileyo ngesilwanyana ngasinye.

Iintsuku ezingamashumi amathathu.
