Before you can evaluate, pack, label, store or sell animal genetic material, you must first register your premises as an AI centre.

Animals kept on your premises must not have contact with other animals outside the premises. You must put a fence around the premises.

Animals kept in the quarantine areas must not have contact with each other or with other animals on the premises. You must have separate stables and pens used for collecting, keeping, examining and testing genetic material in the quarantine areas. Make sure that effluent does not flow from one quarantine stable or pen to another.

The people working with pigs in a quarantine area must not have contact with other workers in the centre.

There must be enough space in the premises for the animals to exercise.

People collecting genetic material must not have access to the above rooms. You must keep rooms used for collecting, evaluating, packing, labelling and storing genetic material clean at all times.

Floors, walls and ceilings of rooms where genetic material is handled must be cleaned regularly and kept safe for animals.

There is no time frame for this service
