Ukusebenzisa isikebhe sokudoba eNingizimu Afrika, kumele uhambise isikebheoPhikweni Lwezokuphepha Olwandle lwaseNingizimu Afrika (samsa)ukuze sihlolwe isikebhe kutholakale ukuthi ingabe sisakulungele na ukuhamba olwandle. I-Samsa izoqhuba inqubo yokuhlola bese ikhiphe iSitifiketi Sokuphepha.

Uma sowunesitifiketi, ungafaka isicelokuMnyango Wezolimo, Amahlathi NezokudobaeKapa ukuze uthole ilayisense yesikebhe sokudoba.

Isikebhe sokudoba yisikebhe noma umkhumbi olungiselwe noma osetshenziselwa:

Lokhu kubandakanya noma esiphi isikebhe esihlenga noma esisiza isikebhe esisodwa noma ngaphezulu olwandle ukuze sikwazi ukwenza noma omuphi umsebenzi ovumelekile ohambisana nokudoba, kodwa ongagcini ngokulungisa, ukuhlinzeka, ukulondoloza, ukugcina esiqandisini, ukuthutha, noma ukuqhuba ukusetshenziswa kokudotshiwe noma okuvuniwe.

Kumele ube nelungelo lokudoba kanye nemvume ukuze uzibandakanye emisebenzini yokudoba. Asikho isikebhe sokudoba esizobhaliswa ngaphandlekwelungelo lokudobakwelungelo lokudoba kanye nemvumo yokuzibandakanya emisebenzini yokudoba.

Isicelo singathatha izinsuku ezintathu zokusebenza noma ngaphezulu, kuncike ekutheni isicelo sifakwe ngendlela eyiyona yona.

Amanani

Xhumana nehhovisi le-SAMSAukuze uthole ulwazi ngamanani esitifiketi sezokuphepha.

Thola Isicelo selayisense yokusebenzisa isikebhe olwandle oluseduze, olwandle olusekudeni nasendaweni engaphandle kwakuleli eSikhungweni Sosizo kuMnyango Wezolimo, Ezamahlathi Nezokudoba eKapa e-Ground Floor, Foretrust Building, Martin Hammerschlag Way, Foreshore.
