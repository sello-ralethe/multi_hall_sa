Ngaphambi kokuba ungahambisa isilwanyana osibhalisele ukuqokelela isidoda kuso usiyisa kwenye indawo, kufanele uqale uvuselele imvume yokugcina isilwanyana ngenjongo yokuqokelela isidoda.

Awunyanzelekanga ukuba uthatheimilinganiselo yegazi lesilwanyana eso. Nencwadi ka gqirha wezilwanyana ayifuneki ukuba awuhambi ngenxa yesifo sezilwanyana i-foot & mouth desease apho isilwanyana besihlala khona. Noba kunjalo, kufanele uqhagamshelane neSebe lezoLimo, uBuyekezo lwemiHlaba noPhuhliso lwamaPhandle ubanike izizathu zokuthi kutheni ufuna ukuhambisa isilwanyana.

Iintsuku ezingamashumi amathathu.

Imali ehlawulwayo yowama- 2018/19
