Title: Imvume kunye nenombolo yokushishina ngeemoto

Language: xh
Date scraped: 2025-03-05 17:43:27

==================================================

Ukuze uqhube isithuthi esingabhaliswanga kwindlela zikarhulumente eMzantsi Afrika, kufuneka ufake isicelo semvume nenombolo yokushishina ngeemoto kuqala.

Inombolo yokushishina ngeemoto isetyenziswa ngabenzi beemoto ababhalisiweyo, abakhi okanye abazithenga kumazwe angaphandle, abanezivumelwano zezothutho lweemoto, abathengisi zimoto okanye amaziko abolekisa ngemali yokuthenga imoto ukuze la mashishini aqhube isithuthi esingabhaliswanga nesingenampephamvume kwindlela zikarhulumente njengenxalenye yoshishino lwabo. Olo shishino luquka:

Isicelo sichotshelwa size siphunyezwe kwangaloo mini.

Fumana imirhumo kwigunyabantu lobhaliso lakho elisekuhlaleni.

Uxwebhu lweSicelo seNombolo yoShishino ngeeMoto (MTN1) (Application and notice i.r.o motor trade number and blank temporary permits [Form MTN1])

Contact the registration authority service centre at your local municipality
