Ukuze ukwazi ukuthumela imbewu usebenzisa isiqinisekiso soBudlelwane bokuVavanya iMbewu yaMazwe ngaMazwe (Orenji kunye/okanye Zuba) kufuneka ufake isicelo kwindlu esetyenziselwa uphando lwenzululwazi esemthethweni  yoBudlelwane bokuVavanya iMbewu yaMazwe ngaMazwe.Faka isicelo sesiqinisekiso soBudlelwane bokuVavanya iMbewu yaMazwe ngaMazwe kwiNdlu yoPhando lwenzululwazi yoVavanyo lweMbewu ngokusemthethweni.

INdlu yoPhando lwenzululwazi yokuVavanya iMbewu ngokuseMthethweni lilungu elisemthethweni loMbutho woBudlelwane waMazwe ngaMazwe wokuVavanya iMbewu (inombolo esemthethweni ZA01). INdlu ezisetyenziselwa uPhando lwenzululwzi ngokusemthethweni lokuVavavnya iMbewu yaMazwe ngaMazwe igunyaziswa ukuba ikhuphe iZiqinisekiso sokuHlalutya iMbewu yaMazwe NgaMazwe.

Ungafaka isicelo sesiqinisekiso seMbewu (Orenji) okanye iSiqinisekiso seSampulu yeMbewu (Blue).

Ungahlawula ngekhompyutha okanye kwikhawuntala.Khumbula ukuquka inombolo yoludwe lwamaxabiso ngeenjongo zokuba kwaziwe ukuba ifakwe ngubani na.

INdlu yoPhando lwenzululwazi esemthethweni yokuVavanya iMbewu  inika ingqwalasela ephambili uvavanyo lweesampulu zembewu ngeenjongo zokuyithumela kumazwe angaphandle. Iintlobo zezityalo ezahlukileyo zineemeko ezahlukileyo zovavanyo kwaye ubude bovavanyo buyohluka ukusuka kwiintsuku ezimbalwa ukuya kwiiveki ezimbalwa.
